5 Subject: Internal security and public safety; federal immigration authorities;

7 Statement of purpose of bill as introduced: This bill proposes to require a
8 judicial warrant from a federal immigration authority before the authority
9 enters a nonpublic area of a sensitive location. It also would terminate and
10 restrict the Department of Corrections’ assistance to federal immigration
11 authorities to the extent permitted under federal law.
12 An act relating to immigration enforcement
13 It is hereby enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Vermont:
