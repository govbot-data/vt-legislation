11 status that is paid for by any source other than the State or by that person
12 themselves.
13 An act relating to travel disclosures for legislators and certain executive
14 officers
15 It is hereby enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Vermont:
16 Sec. 1. 3 V.S.A. § 1214 is added to read:
17 § 1214. TRAVELDISCLOSURES
18 (a) Applicability.
19 (1) A member of the General Assembly or an executive officer shall file
20 with the State Ethics Commission, or as otherwise directed by law, a disclosure
