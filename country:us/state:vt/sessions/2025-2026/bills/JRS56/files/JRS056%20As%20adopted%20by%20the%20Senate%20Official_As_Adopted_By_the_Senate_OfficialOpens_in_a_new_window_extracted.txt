Joint Senate Resolution
Offered by Senator Baruth,
J.R.S. 56. Joint resolution relating to final adjournment of the General Assembly
in 2026.
Resolved by the Senate and House of Representatives:
That the President of the Senate and the Speaker of the House of Representatives
adjourn their respective houses sine die on the twenty-ninth or thirtieth day of May,
2026.
