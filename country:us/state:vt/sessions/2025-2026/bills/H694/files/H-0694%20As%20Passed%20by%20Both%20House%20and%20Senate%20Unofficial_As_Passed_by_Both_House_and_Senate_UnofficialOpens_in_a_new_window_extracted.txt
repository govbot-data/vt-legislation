1 H.694
2 An act relating to approval of amendments to the charter of the Town of
3 Bennington concerning the Town Manager
4 It is hereby enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Vermont:
5 Sec. 1. CHARTER AMENDMENT APPROVAL
6 The General Assembly approves the amendments to the charter of the Town
7 of Bennington as set forth in this act. The voters approved the proposal of
8 amendment on March 4, 2025.
9 Sec. 2. 24 App. V.S.A. chapter 103 is amended to read:
10 CHAPTER 103. TOWN OF BENNINGTON
11 * * *
12 § 406. REMOVAL
13 (a) On Unless expressly modified by contract, on 90 days’ written notice
14 from the Select Board, the Town Manager may be removed without cause by a
15 majority of the Select Board at a meeting called for the purpose of voting on
16 removal. During the 90-day period, the Town Manager may be suspended
17 with pay.
18 * * *
19 Sec. 3. EFFECTIVE DATE
20 This act shall take effect on passage.
