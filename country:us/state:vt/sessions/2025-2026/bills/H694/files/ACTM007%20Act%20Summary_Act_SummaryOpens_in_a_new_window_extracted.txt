Act No. M-7 (H.694). An act relating to approval of amendments to the charter of the
Town of Bennington concerning the Town Manager
Subjects: Municipal charters; municipal and county government; amendments;
Town of Bennington
This act amends the charter of the Town of Bennington to authorize the Selectboard
and a Town Manager to agree to contract terms that modify the Town’s standard
requirement of 90 days’ written notice prior to removal of the Town Manager.
Effective Date: April 20, 2026
