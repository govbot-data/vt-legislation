Senate proposal of amendment
H. 10
An act relating to approval of amendments to the charter of the City of
Barre
The Senate proposes to the House to amend the bill in Sec. 2, 24 App.
V.S.A. chapter 1, in section 110 (fiscal year), following the words “shall begin
on the first day of July”, by striking out the words “and end on the last day of
June” and inserting in lieu thereof “and end on the last day of June”
