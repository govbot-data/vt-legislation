14 An act relating to adding voting student members to school district boards
15 It is hereby enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Vermont:
16 Sec. 1. INTENT
17 It is the intent of the General Assembly to include voting student members
18 on the boards of any new school districts the General Assembly enacts
19 pursuant to 2025 Acts and Resolves No. 73.

1 Sec. 2. 16 V.S.A. § 564 is added to read:
2 § 564. SCHOOL BOARDS; STUDENT MEMBER
3 (a) Notwithstanding sections 558, 729, and 747 of this title or any other
4 provision of law to the contrary, the school board of a town, city, or
5 incorporated or union school district or the member districts of a joint contract
6 district or a regional technical center school district, which is organized to
7 provide secondary education for its resident students or maintains a regional
8 technical center, shall have student members on the school board in accordance
9 with this section.
10 (1) Each school board of a school district that is organized to provide for
11 the education of its resident students in grades nine through 12 shall have one
12 voting student member from each of grades nine, 10, 11, and 12, for a total of
13 four voting student members, regardless of how many high schools the school
14 district operates.
15 (2) Each school board of a school district that is organized to provide for
16 the education of its resident students in grades seven and eight shall have one
17 nonvoting student member from each of grades seven and eight, for a total of
18 two nonvoting student members, regardless of how many schools the district
19 operates that provide education in grades seven and eight.
20 (3) Both nonvoting and voting student school board members shall serve
21 as a board member for the same term length as a nonstudent member;

1 provided, however, that in the case of a school district that operates schools for
2 some or all of the applicable grades, the student school board members shall
3 also be enrolled in and attending a school operated by the district for the entire

6 serve on the school board and a new student school board member shall be

9 (4) Both nonvoting and voting student school board members shall be
10 appointed by the superintendent of the school district, following a process
11 adopted by the school board that enables secondary students to participate in
12 the choice of the student members.
13 (5) Nothing in this section shall preclude a school board from appointing

15 (b) The provisions of this section shall supersede any educational charter
