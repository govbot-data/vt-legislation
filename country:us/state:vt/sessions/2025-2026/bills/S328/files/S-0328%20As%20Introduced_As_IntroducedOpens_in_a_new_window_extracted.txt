10 Statement of purpose of bill as introduced: This bill proposes to make multiple
11 changes related to housing. The bill proposes to require municipal
12 development plans to include information and analysis on housing targets,
13 extend the Down Payment Assistance Program tax credits, cap the allowable
14 rate of increase for mobile home lot rent increases, authorize the Vermont
15 Economic Development Authority to finance certain housing projects, and
16 create the Service-Supported Housing Advisory Council. The bill proposes to
17 restrict common interest communities from prohibiting a unit owner from
18 leasing a unit, utilizing a unit as a family child care home, and installing
19 electrical vehicle supply equipment. The bill proposes to make changes related
20 to municipal zoning requirements related to duplexes, multiunit dwellings, and
