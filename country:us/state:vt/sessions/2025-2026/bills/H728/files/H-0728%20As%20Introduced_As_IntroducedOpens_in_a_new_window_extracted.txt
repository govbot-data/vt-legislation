6 Statement of purpose of bill as introduced: This bill proposes to allow the
7 landlord of a dwelling unit to obtain a no trespass order prohibiting the tenant’s
8 invitees or licensees from entering the dwelling unit or the dwelling unit’s
9 common areas if the tenant consents to the order or if the invitee or licensee
10 subject to the order has violated the terms of the lease agreement.
11 An act relating to trespass in common areas
12 It is hereby enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Vermont:
13 Sec. 1. PURPOSE
14 The purpose of this act is to overrule the Vermont Supreme Court’s
15 decision in State v. Dixon, 169 Vt. 15 (1999), and allow the landlord of a
16 dwelling unit to obtain a no trespass order prohibiting the tenant’s invitees or
17 licensees from entering the dwelling unit’s common areas if the invitee or
18 licensee subject to the order has violated the terms of the lease agreement.
