7 Subject: Economic development; ski academies; special fund; education
8 Statement of purpose of bill as introduced: This bill proposes to create the
9 Vermont Skier Development Scholarship Fund, to be administered by the
10 Agency of Commerce and Community Development, to fund up to 20
11 scholarships for selected Vermont skiers and snowboarders to attend eligible
12 Vermont ski academies. The bill also proposes to prohibit school districts
13 from paying tuition under 16 V.S.A. chapter 21 for students to attend Vermont
14 ski academies. The Special Fund would be funded by the amount of sales and
15 use tax necessary to fund 20 scholarships at a rate of $25,000.00 per
16 scholarship, increased annually by an inflator.
