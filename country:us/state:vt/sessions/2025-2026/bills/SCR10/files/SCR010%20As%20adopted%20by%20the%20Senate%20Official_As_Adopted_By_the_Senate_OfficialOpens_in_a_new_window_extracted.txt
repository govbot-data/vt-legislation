S.C.R. 10. Senate concurrent resolution honoring University of Vermont Medical Center and University of Vermont
pediatricianextraordinaireDr. LewisR. First.
Whereas, one of the most respected academic and hospital chief pediatricians in the United States is Dr. Lewis
First, and
Whereas, as amagna cum laude biochemicalsciences graduateof HarvardCollege, theholder ofan M.D. degree from
Harvard Medical School, the recipient of a master’s degree in epidemiology from the Harvard School of Public Health,
and a former chief pediatrics resident at the world-renowned Boston Children’s Hospital, Dr. First has impeccable
professionalcredentials, and
Whereas, since 1994, he has chaired the pediatrics department at the Robert Larner, M.D. College of Medicine (the
Larner College of Medicine) at the University of Vermont (UVM), which is the longest tenure of any medical school
pediatrics chair in the nation, and, since 1995, Dr. First has also been Golisano Children’s Hospital at UVM Health’s
chiefofpediatrics, and
Whereas, under his dual leadership roles, pediatrics at both medical institutions is considered state of the art and child
andfamilyfocused, andhasgarnerednationalandinternationalrecognition, and
Whereas, notonlyisDr. Firstanoutstandingadministrator, clinician, andteacher, heis anadmiredscholarof pediatric
medicine, and, for16-plusyears, heservedaseditor-in-chiefofthepeer-reviewedmedicaljournalPediatrics, and
Whereas, his healthcare outreach has extended beyond students, patients, fellow physicians, and scientists with his
weeklyFirstwithKidsvideoandradioseries, and
Whereas, Dr. First is the recipient of many honors, including the UVM Alumni Association’s George V. Kidder
Outstanding Faculty Award, the Larner College of Medicine’s Distinguished Educator Award, and the Vermont Medical
Society’s Distinguished Service Award, and he was in the first cohort of the National Academy of Distinguished
EducatorsinPediatrics, and
Whereas, heisnowconcludinghismostpraiseworthycareer, now therefore be it
ResolvedbytheSenateandHouseofRepresentatives:
That the General Assembly honors University of Vermont Medical Center and University of Vermont pediatrician
extraordinaireDr. Lewis R. First, andbeitfurther
Resolved: That the Secretary of State be directed to send a copy of this resolution to Dr. Lewis First, the Larner
CollegeofMedicine, andGolisanoChildren’sHospital.
