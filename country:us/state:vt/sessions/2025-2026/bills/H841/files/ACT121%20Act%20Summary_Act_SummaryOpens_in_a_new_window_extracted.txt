Act No. 121 (H.841). An act relating to miscellaneous animal welfare procedures
Subjects: Public safety; animal welfare
This act gives the Director of Animal Welfare rulemaking authority. It establishes a
registration requirement and penalties for animal shelters, rescue organizations, pet
dealers, and any person importing one or more domestic pets into Vermont for adoption,
sale, transfer, or breeding. The act further requires pet dealers, animal shelters, rescue
organizations, and animal breeders to maintain commercial liability insurance. It requires
all wolf-hybrids six months and older be sterilized. The act authorizes the Animal
Welfare Fund to accept donations, grants, or gifts. The act requires the Director of
Animal Welfare to submit a report regarding what is needed to establish a comprehensive
animal welfare system in Vermont.
Effective Date: June 8, 2026
