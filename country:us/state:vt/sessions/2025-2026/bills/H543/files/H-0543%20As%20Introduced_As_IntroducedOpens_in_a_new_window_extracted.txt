9 An act relating to negligence actions and post-traumatic stress disorder
10 It is hereby enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Vermont:
11 Sec. 1. PURPOSE
12 The purpose of this act is to overrule the Vermont Supreme Court’s
13 decision in Zeno-Etheridge v. Comcast Corp., 219 Vt. 121 (2024) and permit a
14 post-traumatic stress disorder diagnosis to satisfy the required element of a
15 negligence claim that the plaintiff show actual injury.
16 Sec. 2. 12 V.S.A. § 1036a is added to read:
17 § 1036a. NEGLIGENCE; ACTUAL INJURY; POST-TRAUMATIC STRESS
