10 * * * Collective Bargaining Agreements; Fiscal Years 2027 and 2028 * * *
11 Sec. 1. COLLECTIVE BARGAINING AGREEMENTS; FISCAL YEARS
12 2027 AND 2028
13 (a) Fiscal year 2027. This act fully funds the first year of the collective
14 bargaining agreements between the State and the Vermont State Employees’
15 Association and the State and the Vermont Troopers’ Association for the
16 period of July 1, 2026, through June 30, 2027. The collective bargaining
17 agreements for most classified employees provide in fiscal year 2027 an
18 average 1.9 percent step increase and a 2 percent across-the-board increase on
19 July 12, 2026, and a 2 percent across-the-board increase on January 10, 2027,
20 for a total of a 5.9 percent increase.
