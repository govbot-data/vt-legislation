8 Subject: Education; school district board; school closure; power of electorate
9 Statement of purpose of bill as introduced: This bill proposes to require a
10 school district to obtain the affirmative vote of the town within which a school
11 is located before closing the school.
12 An act relating to voter approval prior to school closure
13 It is hereby enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Vermont:
14 Sec. 1. 16 V.S.A. § 564 is added to read:
15 § 564. SCHOOL CLOSURE
16 (a) Before closing a school that operates grades prekindergarten through
17 grade 12, or any subset of such grades, or before closing or relocating one or
18 more individual grades, a school district shall obtain the affirmative vote of the
19 town in which the school or grades facing closure are located.
