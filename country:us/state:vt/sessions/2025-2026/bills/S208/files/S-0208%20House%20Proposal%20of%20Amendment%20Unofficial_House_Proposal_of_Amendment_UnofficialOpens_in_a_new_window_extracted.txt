HOUSE PROPOSAL OF AMENDMENT S.208
2026 Page 1 of 2
1 S.208
2 An act relating to standards for law enforcement identification
3 The House proposes to the Senate to amend the bill by striking out all after
4 the enacting clause and inserting in lieu thereof the following:
5 Sec. 1. 20 V.S.A. § 2373 is added to read:
6 § 2373. STATEWIDE MODEL POLICY; LAW ENFORCEMENT
7 IDENTIFICATION STANDARDS
8 (a) As used in this section:
9 (1) “Facial covering” means any opaque mask, garment, disguise, or
10 other item that conceals or obscures the facial identity of an individual,
11 including a balaclava, gaiter mask, ski mask, and other similar types of facial
12 coverings.
13 (2) “Law enforcement agency” has the same meaning as in section
14 2351a of this title.
15 (3) “Law enforcement officer” has the same meaning as in section
16 2351a of this title.
17 (b) On or before July 1, 2027, the Law Enforcement Advisory Board shall
18 establish a model statewide policy governing the standards for law
19 enforcement identification and the wearing of facial coverings applicable to
20 law enforcement officers to ensure consistent statewide application of the
21 standards.
