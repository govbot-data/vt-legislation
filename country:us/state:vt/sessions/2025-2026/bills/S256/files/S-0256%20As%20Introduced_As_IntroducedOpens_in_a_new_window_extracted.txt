8 Statement of purpose of bill as introduced: This bill proposes to criminalize
9 the act of threatening to disclose a visual image of an identifiable person who
10 is nude or who is engaged in sexual conduct with the intent to compel a person
11 to produce nude images or images of sexual conduct, engage in sexual
12 conduct, or provide anything of value; to criminalize threatening a person with
13 the intent to compel a person to produce nude images or images of sexual
14 conduct or to engage in sexual conduct; to extend the statute of limitations for
15 photographing, filming, or recording a person in violation of the voyeurism
16 statute; to extend the statute of limitations for displaying or disclosing images
17 in violation of the voyeurism statute; to eliminate the statute of limitations for
18 civil actions against a person for knowingly disclosing, without the plaintiff’s
19 consent, a visual image of the plaintiff while the plaintiff is nude or engaged in
20 sexual conduct if the disclosure caused harm to the plaintiff; and to allow
21 recovery of damages for a disorder resulting from trauma in a tort claim
