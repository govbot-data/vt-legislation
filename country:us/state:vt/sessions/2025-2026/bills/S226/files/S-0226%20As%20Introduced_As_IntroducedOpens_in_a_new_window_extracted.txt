7 Statement of purpose of bill as introduced: This bill proposes to create the
8 Vermont Sister State Program to be overseen by the Sister State Program
9 Committee. The Committee, with the assistance of the Agency of Commerce
10 and Community Development, will review applications and recommend to the
11 Governor whether an official sister state relationship should be formalized.

13 It is hereby enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Vermont:

16 (a) Creation and purpose.

19 to the Program and to the Sister State Program Committee as required.
