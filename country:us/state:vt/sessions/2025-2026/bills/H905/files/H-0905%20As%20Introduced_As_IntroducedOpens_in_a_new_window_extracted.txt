7 Subject: Prohibited acts; public accommodations; unfair housing practices;
8 discrimination; hate crimes; Executive Branch; civil rights
9 Statement of purpose of bill as introduced: This bill proposes to align and
10 integrate civil rights protections across Titles 9, 13, and 21 and to create a
11 statutory definition of disparate impact for civil rights violations. The bill also
12 proposes to authorize a State private right of action for civil rights violations.
13 The bill proposes to require antibias and antidiscrimination training for State
14 and municipal employees, annual public reports by law enforcement agencies
15 related to certain offenses, and equity certifications from State contractors and
16 grant recipients. The bill proposes to create the State Civil Rights Data
17 Repository, the State Civil Rights and Equal Protection Fund, and the Civil
18 Rights Coordinating Council. Lastly, the bill proposes to prohibit
19 unauthorized federal collaboration that undermines Vermont civil rights
20 protections.
