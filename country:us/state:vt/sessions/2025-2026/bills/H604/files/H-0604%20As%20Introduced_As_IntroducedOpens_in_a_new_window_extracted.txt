8 An act relating to semiautomatic assault weapons
9 It is hereby enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Vermont:
10 Sec. 1. 13 V.S.A. § 4028 is added to read:
11 § 4028. SEMIAUTOMATIC ASSAULT WEAPONS; MANUFACTURE,
12 POSSESSION, AND TRANSFER PROHIBITED
13 (a) A person shall not manufacture, possess, or transfer a semiautomatic
14 assault weapon.
15 (b) A person who violates this section shall be imprisoned for not more
16 than one year or fined not more than $500.00, or both.
17 (c) This section shall not apply to any semiautomatic assault weapon
18 possessed by:
19 (1) a federal law enforcement officer or a law enforcement officer
20 certified as a law enforcement officer by the Vermont Criminal Justice
