9 Statement of purpose of bill as introduced: This bill proposes to authorize a
10 municipality to acquire through voluntary sale, judicial foreclosure, or other
11 lawful means, or alternatively to impose incrementally increasing fines on, any
12 residential property located within a designated downtown or village area that
13 has been abandoned. Property acquired would be used solely for a public
14 purpose, such as medical housing, transitional housing, rehabilitative or respite
15 housing, or youth or community facilities. Separately, the bill proposes to
16 authorize a municipality to acquire abandoned commercial property located
17 within a designated downtown or village area and dispose of the commercial
18 property to purchasers who demonstrate a material local economic presence or
19 commitment, operate or intend to operate a startup or small business, and
20 contribute to the downtown or village economic revitalization. In addition, the
21 bill proposes to authorize municipalities to enforce habitability standards for
