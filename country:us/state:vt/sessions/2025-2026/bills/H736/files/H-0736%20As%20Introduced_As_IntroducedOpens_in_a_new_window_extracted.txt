10 Subject: Health; health care facilities; prescription drugs
11 Statement of purpose of bill as introduced: This bill proposes to make
12 legislative findings regarding the lack of stock of medications for rare diseases
13 in many inpatient health care facilities and the difficulties that those facilities
14 may encounter in procuring needed medications in a timely manner when
15 caring for patients who enter the facility while on a treatment regimen for a
16 rare disease. The bill would require health care facilities to allow patients who
17 are on an inpatient status to continue to take all medically necessary
18 medications prescribed for them by a licensed health care provider, even if the
19 facility does not typically dispense or administer one or more of the
20 medications, in order to facilitate medication adherence and continuity of
21 patient care across settings.
