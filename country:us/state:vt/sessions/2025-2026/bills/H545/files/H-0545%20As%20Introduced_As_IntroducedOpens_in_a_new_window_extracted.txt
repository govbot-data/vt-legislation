5 Subject: Health; public health; immunizations; recommendations; standing
6 orders; liability; purchasing authority; insurance overage
7 Statement of purpose of bill as introduced: This bill proposes to authorize the
8 Commissioner of Health to issue immunization recommendations for children
9 and adults as part of the Department’s immunization program. This bill further
10 proposes to allow the Commissioner of Health to issue standing orders for
11 recommended immunizations and provides immunity from civil and
12 administrative liability for health care professionals acting in accordance with
13 the standing orders. It proposes to allow the Commissioner of Health to
14 purchase immunizations from either the Centers for Disease Control and
15 Prevention or another vendor. It also proposes to require health insurers to
16 cover immunizations recommended by the Commissioner of Health without
17 any co-payment, coinsurance, or deductible requirements. Lastly, this bill
18 proposes to amend the membership of the Vermont Immunization Advisory
19 Council.
