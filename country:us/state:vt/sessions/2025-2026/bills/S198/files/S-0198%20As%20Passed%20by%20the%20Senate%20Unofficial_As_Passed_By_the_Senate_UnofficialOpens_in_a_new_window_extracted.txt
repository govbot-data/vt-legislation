4 § 1013. DECEPTIVE TOBACCO PRODUCTS AND TOBACCO
5 SUBSTITUTES PROHIBITED
6 No person shall market, promote, label, brand, advertise, distribute, offer
7 for sale, or sell a tobacco product or tobacco substitute by:
8 (1) imitating a product that is not a tobacco product or tobacco
9 substitute, including:
10 (A) a food or brand of food commonly marketed to minors, including
11 candy, desserts, cereal, and beverages;

13 highlighters, pens, and pencils;

17 symbol known to appeal to minors, including a celebrity; a character in a
18 comic book, movie, television show, or video game; or a mythical creature;
19 (2) concealing the nature of the tobacco product or tobacco substitute; or
20 (3) using terms for, describing, or depicting a product described in
21 subdivision (1) of this section.
