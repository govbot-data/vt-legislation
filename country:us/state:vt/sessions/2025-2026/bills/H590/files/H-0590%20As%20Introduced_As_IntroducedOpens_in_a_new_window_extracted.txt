13 § 5292. PROHIBITION ON DETENTION IN ADULT FACILITIES OF
14 MINORS CHARGED OR ADJUDICATED AS DELINQUENTS
15 (a) A minor charged with a delinquent act shall not be detained under this
16 chapter in a jail or other facility intended or used for the detention of adults
17 unless the child is alleged to have committed a crime punishable by life
18 imprisonment and it appears to the satisfaction of the court that public safety
19 and protection reasonably require such detention.
20 (b) A minor who has been adjudicated as a delinquent child shall not by
21 virtue of such adjudication be committed or transferred to an institution or
