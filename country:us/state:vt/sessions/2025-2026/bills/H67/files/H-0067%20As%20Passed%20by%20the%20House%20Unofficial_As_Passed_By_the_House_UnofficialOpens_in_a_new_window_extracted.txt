2 An act relating to legislative operations and government accountability
3 It is hereby enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Vermont:
4 * * * Purpose and Findings * * *
5 Sec. 1. PURPOSE
6 The purpose of this act is to advance the principle of government
7 accountability by examining how evidence is used to inform policy, how State
8 laws are implemented, and how legislation may be structured to achieve its
9 intended outcomes. This act seeks to support consistent and transparent
10 accountability practices through simple, clear, independent, objective, and fact-
11 based processes.
12 * * * Pilot Government Accountability Project * * *
13 Sec. 2. PILOT GOVERNMENT ACCOUNTABILITY PROJECT
14 (a) Assignment. The Joint Fiscal Committee shall, in addition to its other
15 duties described in law and rules, conduct the Pilot Government Accountability
16 Project to examine governmental practices, make recommendations on
17 improving those practices, and develop effective tools for evaluating
18 government accountability.
19 (b) Selection of Project issues before the Committee. The Chief Fiscal
20 Officer of the Joint Fiscal Office, in consultation with the Speaker of the
21 House and the President Pro Tempore, will select issues for the Committee’s

9 Sec. 3. LEGISLATIVE JOINT FISCAL OFFICE CONSULTANT
