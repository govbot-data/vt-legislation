3 It is hereby enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Vermont:
4 Sec. 1. 33 V.S.A. § 4907 is added to read:
5 § 4907. UNACCOMPANIED HOMELESS YOUTH
6 (a) Definition. As used in this section, “unaccompanied homeless youth”
7 means an individual who is 16 years of age or older and is not in the physical
8 custody of a parent or guardian, including a youth who has run away from
9 home, who has been forced to leave home, or whose parents have left the area
10 and left the youth behind.
11 (b) Certification. An unaccompanied homeless youth may become
12 certified if the youth is:
13 (1) found by a school district’s liaison for homeless children and youths
14 or other appropriate staff person to be an unaccompanied homeless youth
15 eligible for services pursuant to the McKinney-Vento Homeless Assistance
16 Act, 42 U.S.C. § 11431 et seq.; and
17 (2) believed to qualify as an unaccompanied homeless youth, as that
18 term is defined by the McKinney-Vento Homeless Assistance Act by:
19 (A) the director of an emergency shelter program funded by the
20 Vermont Housing Opportunity Program (HOP) or the director’s designee;

18 23 V.S.A. § 608;
19 (B) obtain a vital event certificate without parental consent and at no
