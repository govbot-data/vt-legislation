1 § 807. PRIORITY OF MUNICIPAL LIENS
2 The filing of a notice of lien resulting from a judgment by a court of
3 competent jurisdiction for fines in an enforcement proceeding under the
4 Town’s Land Use Regulations; Property Maintenance Ordinance; or Housing,
5 Building, and Life Safety Ordinance shall have priority in law over any other
6 lien having priority in time. The underlying enforcement lien shall remain a
7 valid and subsisting lien upon the real property until the fines are fully paid or
8 otherwise discharged, but not longer than eight years from the date of the
9 judgment, unless it shall have been renewed pursuant to State law.
10 Sec. 3. EFFECTIVE DATE
11 This act shall take effect on passage
