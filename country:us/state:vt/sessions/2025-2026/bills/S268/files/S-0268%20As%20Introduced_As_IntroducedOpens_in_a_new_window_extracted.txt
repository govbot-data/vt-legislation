10 An act relating to internet lottery sales
11 It is hereby enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Vermont:
12 Sec. 1. 31 V.S.A. § 665 is added to read:
13 § 665. MOBILE AND INTERNET LOTTERY SALES; AUTHORITY AND
14 PROCEDURES
15 (a) Authority of the Board. The Board of Liquor and Lottery, through the
16 Commissioner of Liquor and Lottery, is authorized to sell tickets, products,
17 and subscriptions through mobile applications, by mobile devices, or over the
18 internet for the lottery games established and operated pursuant to this chapter.
19 (b) Purchases only within the State. All lottery purchases made through
20 mobile applications, by mobile devices, or over the internet shall be initiated
