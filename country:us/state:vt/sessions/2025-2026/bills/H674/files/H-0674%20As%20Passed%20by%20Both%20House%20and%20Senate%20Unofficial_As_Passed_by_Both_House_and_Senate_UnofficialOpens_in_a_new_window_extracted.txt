5 § 2479. VERMONT SISTER STATE PROGRAM
6 (a) Creation and purpose.
7 (1) The Vermont Sister State Program is created within the Agency of
8 Commerce and Community Development. The Agency shall provide support
9 to the Program and to the Sister State Program Committee as required.
10 (2) The purpose of the Program is to strengthen Vermont’s international
11 engagement and to foster mutually beneficial relationships with national and
12 subnational governments abroad, with a goal of promoting cultural exchange,
13 economic development, and educational cooperation.
