7 Statement of purpose of bill as introduced: This bill proposes to require each
8 hospital’s board of directors to include at least two representatives of the
9 public. It would also require hospitals to provide information to the Green
10 Mountain Care Board when they adjust the compensation for their executives.
11 An act relating to hospital governance
12 It is hereby enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Vermont:
