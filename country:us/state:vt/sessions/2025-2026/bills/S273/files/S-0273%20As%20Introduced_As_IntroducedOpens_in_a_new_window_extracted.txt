8 Statement of purpose of bill as introduced: This bill proposes to (1) require a
9 school district that does not maintain an elementary school or a high school to
10 designate eligible schools to serve as the public school of the district; (2) repeal
11 the definition of approved independent school and the approved independent
12 school approval process, while maintaining the recognized independent school
13 enrollment process; (3) create a definition of therapeutic schools and make
14 amendments to special education independent school tuition provisions; (4)
15 make conforming changes throughout Title 16 regarding the school
16 designation process; and (5) make conforming changes throughout the
17 Vermont Statutes Annotated based on the repeal of the approved independent
18 school definition and approval process.
19 An act relating to the provision of State-funded education in school districts
20 that do not maintain an elementary school or a high school
