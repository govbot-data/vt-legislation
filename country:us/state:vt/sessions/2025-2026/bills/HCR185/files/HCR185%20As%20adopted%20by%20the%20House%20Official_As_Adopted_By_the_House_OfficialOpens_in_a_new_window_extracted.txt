State of Vermont
House of Representatives
Montpelier, Vermont
Concurrent House Resolution
H.C.R. 185
Offered by Representatives Austin of Colchester, Bishop of Colchester, Critchlow of
Colchester, and Pezzo of Colchester
Offered by Senators Brennan, Baruth, Gulick, Norris and Vyhovsky
House concurrent resolution congratulating the 2025 Colchester Majors’ Division I
championship Little League Baseball team
Whereas, the 2025 Division I Little League Baseball championship finale featured two teams,
Colchester and Champlain Valley, comprising outstanding players 10–12 years of age, in an
exhilarating game played at Burlington’s Schifilliti Field, and
Whereas, this classic baseball encounter, during which the lead changed four times, was truly
in fluxuntil the final out was recorded, and
Whereas, although Champlain Valley initiated the scoring with a strong four-run opening
blast, Colchester responded with equal fervor, concluding the first inning with a four–all tie, but
the day’s memorable moment occurred in the bottom of the sixth, when, with his team down two
outs and trailing 11–9, a Colchester player swiped a three-run over-the-fence homer, cementing a
12–11 triumph, and
Whereas, at the State championship, Colchester reached the semifinal game, bowing to Essex
Town, the eventual title winner, and
Whereas, Colchester’s impressive squad consisted of Cooper Carney, Hayden Critchlow,
Weston Ellingson, Carson Hier, Fritz Jakobeit, David Litchfield, Kellan McLaughlin, Paxton
Merchant, Keegan Norris, River Shelvey, Dylan Tepper, and Oliver Wheelock, and
Whereas, Head Coach Brennan Carney and assistant coaches Mark Ellingson, Jamie
Merchant, and Steve Shelvey inspired the team to play its best, now therefore be it
Resolved by the Senate and House of Representatives:
That the General Assembly congratulates the 2025 Colchester Majors’ Division I
championship Little League Baseball team, and be it further
Resolved: That the Secretary of State be directed to send a copy of this resolution to the
Colchester Majors’ Little League Baseball team.
