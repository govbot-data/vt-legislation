1 (31) To regulate the surface water use of a public water used as a public
2 water source, regardless of where the public water is located, when the public
3 water is used by a public water system of the municipality and the municipality
4 owns all of the land adjoining the public water. Any ordinance adopted under
5 this section shall be in addition to any rules adopted by the Secretary of
6 Natural Resources pursuant to 10 V.S.A. § 1424, provided that, in the event of
7 a conflict between a municipal ordinance adopted under this section and a rule
8 adopted by the Secretary of Natural Resources, the municipal ordinance shall
9 control. In this subdivision:
10 (A) “Public water” has the same meaning as in 10 V.S.A. § 1422,
11 except that under this subdivision (31), “public water” does not mean
12 transboundary lakes or ponds.
13 (B) “Public water source” and “public water system” have the same
14 meanings as in 10 V.S.A. § 1671.
15 Sec. 2. EFFECTIVE DATE
16 This act shall take effect on passage.
