Senate proposal of amendment
H. 222
An act relating to civil orders of protection
The Senate proposes to the House to amend the bill in Sec. 2, 15 V.S.A.
§ 1103, in subdivision (c)(2)(J), in the second sentence, after “civil contempt
proceedings” by inserting “pursuant to Rule 16 of the Vermont Rules of
Family Proceedings”
