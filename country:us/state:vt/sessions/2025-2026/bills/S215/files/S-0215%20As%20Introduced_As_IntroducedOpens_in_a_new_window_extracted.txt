7 Statement of purpose of bill as introduced: This bill proposes to require the
8 imposition of late fees on a person who fails to comply with the time frames of
9 a corrective action plan for a hazardous materials release. The bill would also
10 allow the Secretary of Natural Resources to establish by rule a program for the
11 certification of environmental professionals providing services related to the
12 identification, investigation, and remediation of properties impacted by a
13 release of a hazardous material.
