5 Subject: Education; attendance; district of residence; military families
6 Statement of purpose of bill as introduced: This bill proposes to require school
7 districts that enroll a child whose parents or guardians have been relocated to
8 the school district under military orders to facilitate comparable special
9 education services that are in place within 30 days after the student’s
10 enrollment. This bill also proposes to allow a child of a member of the armed
11 forces to continue to attend the child’s school of origin during the school year
12 if the child’s parent or guardian who is a member of the armed forces receives
13 orders directing the member to a location outside the school district.
14 An act relating to military-connected students
15 It is hereby enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Vermont:
