12 § 3172. EMERGENCY PERSONNEL SURVIVORS BENEFIT AND
13 DISABILITY BENEFITS REVIEW BOARD
14 (a)(1) There is created the Emergency Personnel Survivors Benefit and
15 Disability Benefits Review Board, which shall consist of the State Treasurer or
16 designee, the Attorney General or designee, the Chief Fire Service Training
17 Officer of the Vermont Fire Service Training Council or designee, the
18 Executive Director of the Vermont Criminal Justice Council or designee, and
19 one member of the public to represent the interests of emergency personnel
20 appointed by the Governor for a term of two years.

6 § 3173. MONETARY BENEFIT
7 (a) The survivors of emergency personnel who dies die while in the line of

9 a permanent total disability in the line of duty may apply for a payment of
10 $80,000.00 from the State.

12 § 3175. EMERGENCY PERSONNEL SURVIVORS BENEFIT AND
13 DISABILITY BENEFITS SPECIAL FUND
14 (a) The Emergency Personnel Survivors Benefit and Disability Benefits
15 Special Fund is established in the Office of the State Treasurer for the purpose
16 of the payment of claims distributed pursuant to this chapter. The Fund shall
17 comprise appropriations made by the General Assembly, amounts transferred
18 by the Emergency Board when the General Assembly is not in session, and
19 contributions or donations from any other source. All balances in the Fund at
20 the end of the fiscal year shall be carried forward. Interest earned shall remain
21 in the Fund.
