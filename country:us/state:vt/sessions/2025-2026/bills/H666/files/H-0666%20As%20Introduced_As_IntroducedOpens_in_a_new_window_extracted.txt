1 § 3175. EMERGENCY PERSONNEL SURVIVORS BENEFIT SPECIAL
2 FUND
3 (a) The Emergency Personnel Survivors Benefit Special Fund is
4 established in the Office of the State Treasurer for the purpose of the payment
5 of claims distributed pursuant to this chapter. The Fund shall comprise
6 appropriations made by the General Assembly, amounts transferred by the
7 Emergency Board when the General Assembly is not in session, and
8 contributions or donations from any other source. All balances in the Fund at
9 the end of the fiscal year shall be carried forward. Interest All interest earned
10 on the Fund balance shall remain in be credited to the Fund.
11 (b) In the event that the balance of the Fund is insufficient to pay monetary
12 benefits awarded by the Board when the General Assembly is not in session,
13 the Emergency Board may, pursuant to its authority under 32 V.S.A. § 133,
14 transfer into the Fund additional amounts necessary to pay the monetary
15 benefits.
