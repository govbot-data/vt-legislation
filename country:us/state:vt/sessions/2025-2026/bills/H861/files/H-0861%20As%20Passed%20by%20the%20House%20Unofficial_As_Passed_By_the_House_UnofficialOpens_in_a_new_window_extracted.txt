8 Subchapter 7. Americans with Disabilities Act Coordinator
9 § 2331. AMERICANS WITH DISABILITIES ACT COORDINATOR
10 (a) There is created the permanent position of the Americans with
11 Disabilities Act Coordinator within the Agency of Administration for the
12 purpose of coordinating, across State government and in collaboration with
13 agency partners, all State programs, services, and activities, accessible to and
14 available for individuals with disabilities.
15 (b) The Coordinator shall be an individual with the lived experience of a
16 disability and shall have completed a recognized Americans with Disabilities
17 Act coordinator certificate program. The Coordinator shall have
18 comprehensive knowledge of the Americans with Disabilities Act, 42 U.S.C.
19 §§ 12101–12213, section 504 of the Rehabilitation Act of 1973, 29 U.S.C. §
20 794, and any related federal and State disability rights law. The Coordinator
21 shall be familiar with the structure, programs, services, and personnel of State
