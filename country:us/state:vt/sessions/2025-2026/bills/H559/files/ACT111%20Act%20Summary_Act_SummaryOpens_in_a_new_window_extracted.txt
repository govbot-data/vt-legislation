Act No. 111 (H.559). An act relating to the Parole Board
Subjects: Corrections; Parole Board
This act requires regular training for Parole Board members. It directs that the Parole
Board shall consist of seven regular members, instead of five regular members and two
alternates. The act further requires the Governor to consult with the Parole Board
Director and the Chair of the Parole Board and to consider a balance of knowledge and
experience in appointing Parole Board members. The act establishes the Parole Board
Legal Counsel Pilot Project, carries forward and appropriates money for the pilot project,
and requires the Parole Board Director to submit a report on the operation of the pilot
project. The act further requires the Parole Board Director to submit a proposed budget
to the Commissioner of Corrections and the Secretary of Human Services and to submit a
report detailing the budget development process. The act further clarifies that victims can
waive notifications from the Department of Corrections.
Effective Date: July 1, 2026
