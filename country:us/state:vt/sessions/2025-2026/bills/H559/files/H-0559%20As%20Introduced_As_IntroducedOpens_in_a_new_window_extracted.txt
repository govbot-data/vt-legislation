5 Subject: Corrections; Parole Board
6 Statement of purpose of bill as introduced: This bill proposes to require
7 training for members of the Parole Board; change the number of members of
8 the Parole Board from five regular members and two alternate members to
9 seven regular members; to amend the knowledge and experience background
10 required for members of the Parole Board; and amend the responsibilities of
11 the Director of the Parole Board.
