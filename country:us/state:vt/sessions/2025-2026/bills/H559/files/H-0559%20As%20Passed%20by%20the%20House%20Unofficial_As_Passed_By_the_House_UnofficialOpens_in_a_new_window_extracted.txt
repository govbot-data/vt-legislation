4 Sec. 4. PAROLE BOARD LEGAL COUNSEL PILOT PROJECT
5 (a) There is created the Parole Board Legal Counsel Pilot Project to provide
6 external legal support for:
7 (1) annual training to the Board, including on topics related to due
8 process and parole violations; and
9 (2) legal advice to the Board as needed related to Board hearings.
10 (b) The Office of the Attorney General shall coordinate with the Board and
11 the Agency of Human Services to identify and contract with external legal
12 support.
13 (c) As part of the fiscal year 2028 budget development process, the Agency
14 of Human Services and the Department of Corrections shall coordinate with
15 the Parole Board Director to evaluate the pilot project and determine resources
16 needed for Board external legal support for fiscal year 2028.
17 (d) On or before November 15, 2026, the Parole Board Director shall
18 submit a written report to the House Committee on Corrections and Institutions
19 detailing the operation of the pilot project. The report shall include a
20 recommendation regarding legal support for the Board going forward and the
21 resources needed.
