3 Sec. 4. PAROLE BOARD LEGAL COUNSEL PILOT PROJECT
4 (a) There is created the Parole Board Legal Counsel Pilot Project to provide
5 external legal support for:
6 (1) annual training to the Board, including on topics related to due
7 process and parole violations; and
8 (2) legal advice to the Board as needed related to Board hearings.
9 (b) The Board and the Agency of Human Services shall identify and
10 contract with external legal support in coordination with the Office of the
11 Attorney General.
12 (c) As part of the fiscal year 2028 budget development process, the Agency
13 of Human Services and the Department of Corrections shall coordinate with
14 the Parole Board Director to evaluate the pilot project and determine resources
15 needed for Board external legal support for fiscal year 2028.
16 (d) On or before November 15, 2026, the Parole Board Director shall
17 submit a written report to the House Committee on Corrections and Institutions
18 and the Senate Committee on Institutions detailing the operation of the pilot
19 project. The report shall include a recommendation regarding legal support for
20 the Board going forward and the resources needed.
