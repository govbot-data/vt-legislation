6 Subject: Crimes; humane and proper treatment of animals; cruelty to animals
7 Statement of purpose of bill as introduced: This bill proposes to increase the
8 scope of sexual conduct that can be prosecuted or adjudicated as animal cruelty
9 or aggravated animal cruelty. This bill further proposes to create a mechanism
10 to recover costs of care from the alleged defendant after an animal is seized
11 during an animal cruelty investigation. Additionally, this bill proposes to
12 impose mandatory sanctions, including animal possession bans of not less than
13 five years, for persons convicted of animal cruelty.
