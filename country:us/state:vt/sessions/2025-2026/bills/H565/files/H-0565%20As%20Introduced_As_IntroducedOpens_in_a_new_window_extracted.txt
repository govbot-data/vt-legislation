5 Subject: Commerce and trade; residential rental agreements; landlord tenant;

7 Statement of purpose of bill as introduced: This bill proposes to restrict a
8 landlord or a mobile home park owner from charging or receiving a security
9 deposit exceeding an amount equal to three month’s rent and to require that a
10 landlord or mobile home park owner maintain a security deposit within an
11 interest-bearing account on behalf of a tenant or leaseholder.
12 An act relating to security deposits
13 It is hereby enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Vermont:
14 Sec. 1. 9 V.S.A. § 4461 is amended to read:
15 § 4461. SECURITY DEPOSITS
16 (a)(1) A security deposit is any advance, deposit, or prepaid rent, however
17 named, which is refundable to the tenant at the termination or expiration of the
18 tenancy, including any interest accrued in accordance with this subsection.
19 The function of a security deposit is to secure the performance of a tenant’s
20 obligations to pay rent and to maintain a dwelling unit.
