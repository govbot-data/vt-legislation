5 Subject: Legislature; Legislative Compensation Commission; General

7 Statement of purpose of bill as introduced: This bill proposes to create the
8 Legislative Compensation Commission, which would undertake an initial
9 comprehensive review of issues involving legislative compensation and
10 benefits, staffing, administrative support, and the length of the legislative
11 session. Thereafter, the Legislative Compensation Commission would make
12 biennial recommendations on the rate of compensation and expense
13 reimbursement for members of the General Assembly, the Speaker of the
14 House, and the President Pro Tempore of the Senate. This bill also proposes to
15 make members of the General Assembly eligible to participate in the State
16 employees’ health benefit plan on the same terms and at the same cost as
17 employees of the Executive Branch.

4 § 1021. CREATION OF COMMISSION
5 (a) Creation. There is created the Legislative Compensation Commission

8 (b) Members.

13 (A) three individuals, not all from the same party and one of whom is
14 a former member of the General Assembly, who shall be appointed by the
15 Speaker of the House;
16 (B) three individuals, not all from the same party and one of whom is
17 a former member of the General Assembly, who shall be appointed by the
18 Committee on Committees; and
19 (C) one individual, appointed by the Governor in the Governor’s sole
20 discretion.
