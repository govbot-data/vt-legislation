9 An act relating to establishing energy storage goals
10 It is hereby enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Vermont:
11 Sec. 1. 30 V.S.A. § 256 is added to read:
12 § 256. ENERGY STORAGE GOALS
13 (a) It shall be the goals of the State to:
14 (1) develop 200 MW of installed capacity of energy storage facilities
15 within the State on or before January 1, 2030;
16 (2) develop 450 MW of installed capacity of energy storage facilities
17 within the State on or before January 1, 2035; and
18 (3) develop 800 MW of installed capacity of energy storage facilities
19 within the State on or before January 1, 2040.
