Act No. 115 (H.171). An act relating to criminal justice agency protocols for an officer-
involved shooting
Subjects: Law enforcement; Attorney General; investigation
This act directs the Office of the Attorney General, the Department of State’s
Attorneys and Sheriffs, the Department of Public Safety, and the Vermont Association of
Chiefs of Police to work collaboratively to identify a protocol and best practices for
independent investigations of an officer-involved shooting (OIS). The entities are
required to submit one written report to the House and Senate Committees on Judiciary
on or before December 1, 2026, detailing their work, an agreed-upon protocol for an OIS
investigation, and any legislative changes necessary, including appropriations, to
effectuate the protocol.
Effective Date: June 8, 2026
