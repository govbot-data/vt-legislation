2 An act relating to criminal justice agency protocols for an officer-involved
3 shooting
4 It is hereby enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Vermont:
5 Sec. 1. OFFICER-INVOLVED SHOOTING PROTOCOL
6 (a) The General Assembly finds it is in the best interests of victims and law
7 enforcement officers to efficiently and thoroughly conduct independent
8 investigations of any officer-involved shooting (OIS) in Vermont. It is the
9 intent of the General Assembly to facilitate cooperation amongst the entities
10 responsible for investigating an OIS so there is a well-understood protocol for
11 addressing an OIS that instills public trust in such investigations and resolves
12 investigations in a timely manner to provide certainty to all parties involved.
13 (b) The Attorney General’s Office (AGO), the Department of State’s
14 Attorneys and Sheriffs (DSAS), the Department of Public Safety, and the
15 Vermont Association of Chiefs of Police shall work collaboratively to identify
16 a protocol and best practices for independent investigations of an OIS.
17 (c) A protocol for interagency cooperation in an OIS investigation shall:
18 (1) define “officer-involved shooting” and what types of incidents
19 trigger an investigation;
20 (2) define “conflict of interest” and identify the appropriate steps to
21 manage any conflicts for law enforcement and prosecutors;
