6 Subject: Criminal procedures; stalking; orders against stalking
7 Statement of purpose of bill as introduced: This bill proposes to provide, for
8 purposes of proving a course of conduct in criminal stalking cases and civil
9 stalking orders of protection, that the multiple acts establishing the course of
10 conduct do not have to occur on separate calendar days.
11 An act relating to course of conduct in stalking cases
12 It is hereby enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Vermont:
13 Sec. 1. 12 V.S.A. § 5131 is amended to read:
14 § 5131. DEFINITIONS
15 As used in this chapter:
16 (1)(A) “Course of conduct” means:
17 (i) two or more acts over a period of time, however short, in which
18 a person follows, monitors, surveils, threatens, or makes threats about another
19 person, or interferes with another person’s property; or
