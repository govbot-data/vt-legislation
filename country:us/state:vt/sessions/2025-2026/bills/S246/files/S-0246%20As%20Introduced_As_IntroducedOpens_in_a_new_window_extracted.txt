5 Subject: Taxation; sales and use tax; exemptions; aircraft
6 Statement of purpose of bill as introduced: This bill proposes to add

10 revenues with Vermont airports.

12 sharing with airports

9 Aircraft, as defined in 5 V.S.A. § 202(6), shall be valued and taxed in
