5 Subject: Labor; employment practices; minimum wages; overtime; firefighters
6 Statement of purpose of bill as introduced: This bill proposes to require
7 overtime pay for firefighters for hours worked in excess of 42 hours a week.
8 An act relating to overtime pay for firefighters
9 It is hereby enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Vermont:
10 Sec. 1. 21 V.S.A. chapter 5, subchapter 3 is redesignated to read:
11 Subchapter 3. Minimum Wages and Overtime Pay
12 Sec. 2. 21 V.S.A. § 388 is added to read:
13 § 388. OVERTIME PAY FOR FIREFIGHTERS
14 (a) No State, municipal, or county fire department shall employ any full-
15 time firefighter, as defined in 20 V.S.A. § 3151(4), excluding civilian
16 employees, for an average workweek longer than 42 hours unless the
17 firefighter is compensated at the rate of one and one-half times the firefighter’s
18 regular wage rate for all hours worked in excess of 42 hours based upon an
19 average workweek.
