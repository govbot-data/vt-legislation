2 Sec. 6. 18 V.S.A. § 8350 is amended to read:
3 § 8350. CONSENT BY MINORS FOR MENTAL HEALTH TREATMENT
4 A minor may give consent to receive any legally authorized outpatient
5 treatment from a qualified mental health professional, as defined in section
6 7101 of this title. Consent under this section shall not be subject to
7 disaffirmance due to minority of the person consenting. The consent of a
8 parent or legal guardian shall not be necessary to authorize outpatient
9 treatment. As used in this section, “outpatient treatment” means
10 psychotherapy and other counseling services that are supportive, but not
11 prescription drugs.
