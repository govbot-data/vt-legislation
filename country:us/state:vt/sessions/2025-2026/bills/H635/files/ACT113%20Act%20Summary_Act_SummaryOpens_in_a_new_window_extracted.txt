Act No. 113 (H.635). An act relating to eliminating Department of Corrections supervisory
fees
Subjects: Department of Corrections; furlough; probation; parole; public
institutions and corrections; administration of the Department; supervisory fees;
supervised community sentence
This act repeals the authority for the Commissioner of Corrections to impose
supervisory fees on persons supervised by the Department of Corrections who are on
probation, furlough, pre-approved furlough, supervised community sentence, or parole
and tasks the Department with updating its rules, policies, and procedures accordingly.
Effective Date: July 1, 2027
