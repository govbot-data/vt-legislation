1 Sec. 2. SUPERVISORY FEES; DEPARTMENT OF CORRECTIONS
2 (a) The Department of Corrections shall not assess, bill, or collect any
3 supervisory fee or employ any collection agency or other entity to assess, bill,
4 or collect any supervisory fee.
5 (b) The Department of Corrections shall forgive all outstanding
6 supervisory fees and eliminate all references to outstanding supervisory fees in
7 its records and on its web-based portal for supervisory fee payments.
8 (c) The Department of Corrections shall cease all efforts to collect any
9 outstanding supervisory fees, including through wage garnishment or taxsetoff
10 debt collection, and shall coordinate with any agency made party to
11 supervisory fee collection to cease collection.
12 (d) Notwithstanding any law to the contrary, failure to pay a supervisory
13 fee shall not constitute a violation of probation, parole, furlough, or any other
14 sentence.
15 Sec. 3. RULES; REVIEW; DEPARTMENT OF CORRECTIONS
16 (a) The Department of Corrections shall identify each rule adopted by the
17 Department under the statutory authority of 28 V.S.A. § 102(c)(14). For each,
18 the Commissioner of Corrections shall notify the Secretary of State of its
19 repeal by operation of law pursuant to 3 V.S.A. § 848(a).
