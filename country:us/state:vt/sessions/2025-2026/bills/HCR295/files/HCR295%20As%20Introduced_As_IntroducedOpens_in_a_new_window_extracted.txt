State of Vermont
House of Representatives
Montpelier, Vermont
Concurrent House Resolution
H.C.R. 295
Offered by Representatives Greer of Bennington, Morrissey of Bennington, Cooper of Pownal, Corcoran of
Bennington, Durfee of Shaftsbury, and Nigro of Bennington
Offered by Senators Bongartz and Plunkett
House concurrent resolution honoring Patricia Gibbons for her extraordinary half-century teaching career in
the Town of Bennington
Whereas, in September 1975, Patricia Gibbons became a member of the faculty at Mount Anthony Union
High School in the Town of Bennington, and
Whereas, after three short years of high school teaching, recognizing that the high school pace did not match
her preferred teaching style, she transferred to the North Bennington Graded School, now known as the Village
School of North Bennington, and
Whereas, this new scholastic setting proved ideal, and she has described her good fortune to teach at this
school as having “won the lottery,” and
Whereas, with great creativity, Pat Gibbons taught reading and the magic of books to her students in her
third through sixth grade classes at the school, and
Whereas, her remarkable pedagogical record served as a pillar of the school, which, during her tenure,
transformed in many ways, and
Whereas, Pat Gibbons’s nearly 50 years of faculty service at the Village School of North Bennington
represents an exemplary testament to her love of learning and to the North Bennington community, and
Whereas, she has touched countless lives, nurturing and inspiring generations of students with kindness,
wisdom, and an unwavering commitment to providing them with the best possible education, and
Whereas, after nearly five decades of educating the children of North Bennington, Pat Gibbons’s decision to
put down her pencil and chalk represents the end of an era that many will long cherish and remember, now
therefore be it
Resolved by the Senate and House of Representatives:
That the General Assembly honors Patricia Gibbons for her extraordinary half-century teaching career in the
Town of Bennington, and be it further
Resolved: That the Secretary of State be directed to send a copy of this resolution to Pat Gibbons, the
Village School of North Bennington, and the North Bennington Village Board of Trustees.
