12 An act relating to congenital cytomegalovirus
13 It is hereby enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Vermont:
14 Sec. 1. RULEMAKING; NEWBORN SCREENING PROGRAM
15 The Department of Health shall amend its rule pertaining to the newborn
16 screening program pursuant to 3 V.S.A. chapter 25 to include testing for
17 congenital cytomegalovirus (cCMV). The Department shall further amend its
18 rule to ensure information is distributed to expectant parents, hospitals,
19 birthing centers, and health care providers offering prenatal, postnatal,
