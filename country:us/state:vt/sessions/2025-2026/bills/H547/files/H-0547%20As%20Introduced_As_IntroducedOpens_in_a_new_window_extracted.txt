15 (9) “Military protective order’’ means an order issued against a current
16 member of the U.S. Armed Forces, the Vermont National Guard, or the

18 officer, on a form prescribed by the United States Department of Defense.

2 § 5133. REQUESTS FOR AN ORDER AGAINST STALKING OR
3 SEXUAL ASSAULT
4 (a) A person, other than a family or household member as defined in 15
5 V.S.A. § 1101(2), may seek an order against stalking or sexual assault on
6 behalf of himself or herself themselves or his or her their children by filing a
7 complaint under this chapter. A minor 16 years of age or older may file a

14 person authorized pursuant to 10 U.S.C. § 936 or 5 U.S.C. § 303 to administer
15 oaths shall be admissible evidence in proceedings under this chapter for the
16 purpose of establishing a relevant pattern or course of conduct.
