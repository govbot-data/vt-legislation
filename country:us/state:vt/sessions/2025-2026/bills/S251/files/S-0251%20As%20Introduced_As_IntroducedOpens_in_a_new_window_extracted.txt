15 § 4085. DIAGNOSIS AND TREATMENT OF CERTAIN PEDIATRIC
16 NEUROPSYCHIATRIC CONDITIONS
17 (a) As used in this section:
18 (1) “PANDAS” means Pediatric Autoimmune Neuropsychiatric
19 Disorders Associated with Streptococcal Infections (PANDAS).

1 (2) “PANS” means Pediatric Acute-onset Neuropsychiatric Syndrome
2 (PANS).
3 (b)(1) A health insurance plan shall provide coverage for the medically
4 necessary diagnosis and treatment of PANDAS and PANS that is prescribed or
5 ordered by the covered individual’s treating health care professional. Covered
6 treatments shall include antibiotics, medication therapy and mental health
7 services to manage neuropsychiatric symptoms, immunomodulating
8 medicines, plasma exchange, and intravenous immunoglobulin therapy.
9 (2) Coverage for PANDAS and PANS shall adhere to the treatment
10 recommendations set forth in current clinical practice guidelines published in
11 peer-reviewed medical literature or endorsed by organizations composed of
12 expert treating health care professionals.
13 (c) A health insurance plan shall not impose greater coinsurance, co-
14 payment, deductible, or other cost-sharing requirements for coverage of the
15 diagnosis or treatment of PANDAS or PANS than apply to the diagnosis and
16 treatment of any other physical or mental condition under the plan.
17 (d) For billing and diagnosis purposes, PANDAS and PANS may be coded
18 as autoimmune encephalitis until such time as the American Medical
19 Association and the Centers for Medicare and Medicaid Services create and
20 assign a specific code for PANDAS and PANS. After a specific code has been
21 established, PANDAS and PANS may be coded as autoimmune encephalitis,
