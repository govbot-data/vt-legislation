6 § 8002. DEFINITIONS
7 As used in this chapter:

9 (18) “Plant” means an independent technical facility that generates
10 electricity from renewable energy. A group of facilities, such as wind turbines,
11 shall be considered one plant if the group is part of the same project and uses
12 common equipment and infrastructure such as roads, control facilities, and
13 connections to the electric grid. Common ownership, contiguity in time of
14 construction, and proximity of facilities to each other shall be relevant to
15 determining whether a group of facilities is part of the same project. Multiple
16 electricity-generating facilities, regardless of when each is constructed, shall be
17 considered one plant if the facilities use the same electricity-generating
18 technology and are located on the same parcel or contiguous parcels of land.
19 However, such facilities shall be considered separate plants if:

15 light of emerging issues and shall apply only to applications filed on or after
