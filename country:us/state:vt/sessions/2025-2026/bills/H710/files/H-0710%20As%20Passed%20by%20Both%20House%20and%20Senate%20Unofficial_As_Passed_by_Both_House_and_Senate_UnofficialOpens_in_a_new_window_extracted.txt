5 § 8002. DEFINITIONS
6 As used in this chapter:
7 * * *
8 (18) “Plant” means an independent technical facility that generates
9 electricity from renewable energy. A group of facilities, such as wind turbines,
10 shall be considered one plant if the group is part of the same project and uses
11 common equipment and infrastructure such as roads, control facilities, and
12 connections to the electric grid. Common ownership, contiguity in time of
13 construction, and proximity of facilities to each other shall be relevant to
14 determining whether a group of facilities is part of the same project. Multiple
15 electricity-generating facilities, regardless of when each is constructed, shall be
16 considered one plant if the facilities use the same electricity-generating
17 technology and are located on the same parcel or contiguous parcels of land.
18 However, such facilities shall be considered separate plants if:
19 (A) the facilities are for individual net metering or self-consumption
20 and:
