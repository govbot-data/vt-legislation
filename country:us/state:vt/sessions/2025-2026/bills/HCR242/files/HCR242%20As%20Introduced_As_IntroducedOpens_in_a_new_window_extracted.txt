State of Vermont
House of Representatives
Montpelier, Vermont
Concurrent House Resolution
H.C.R. 242
Offered by Representatives Morrissey of Bennington, Cooper of Pownal, Corcoran of Bennington, Durfee of
Shaftsbury, Greer of Bennington, and Nigro of Bennington
Offered by Senators Bongartz and Plunkett
House concurrent resolution congratulating the 2026 Mount Anthony Union High School Patriots on
winning the Division I boys’ Nordic skiing championship
Whereas, Nordic skiing competition tests skiers’ agility and speed as they race across the snow-covered
course, and, annually, the best high school Nordic skiers engage in individual and team competition at the State
championship meet, and
Whereas, in 2026, the first day’s freestyle races were held at the Rikert Outdoor Center in Ripton and the
competition concluded with the classic races at the Craftsbury Outdoor Center, and
Whereas, in a clean sweep of their Division I competitors, the Patriots boys won all four races: the classic,
in which three Mount Anthony skiers finished among the top eight; the classic relay; the freestyle; and the
freestyle relay, and
Whereas, this unusual accomplishment enabled the Patriots to limit their point tally to 87 (in Nordic skiing,
the better the performance, the lower the score) and easily top second-place Champlain Valley Union High
School, which scored 150 points, and
Whereas, this talented Patriots Nordic skiing contingent consisted of Carson Andrick, Eli McEnaney, Henry
Morrison, Liam Mulligan, Hunter Favreau, Abeh Woods, MaxCutsumpas, Orion Breese, Colin Gulley, Quentin
Phillips, and Edgar Ostler, and
Whereas, this grand skiing adventure would not have been possible without the expert guidance of Head
Coach Rob Short and Assistant Coach Gabe Niles, now therefore be it
Resolved by the Senate and House of Representatives:
That the General Assembly congratulates the 2026 Mount Anthony Union High School Patriots on winning
the Division I boys’ Nordic skiing championship, and be it further
Resolved: That the Secretary of State be directed to send a copy of this resolution to Mount Anthony Union
High School.
