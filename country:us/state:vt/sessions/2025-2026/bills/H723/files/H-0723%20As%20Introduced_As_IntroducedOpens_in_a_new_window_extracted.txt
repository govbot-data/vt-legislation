14 Sec. 1. 10 V.S.A. § 5201 is amended to read:
15 § 5201. NOTICES; POSTING
16 (a)(1) An owner, or a person having the exclusive right to take game upon
17 land or the waters thereon, who desires to protect his or her the owner’s land or
18 private pond or propagation farm over which he or she the owner has exclusive
19 control may maintain notices stating that:
