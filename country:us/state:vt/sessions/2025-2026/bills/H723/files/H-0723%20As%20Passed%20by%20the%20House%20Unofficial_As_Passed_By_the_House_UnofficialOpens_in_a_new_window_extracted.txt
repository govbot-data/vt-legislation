2 An act relating to posting of land
3 It is hereby enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Vermont:
4 Sec. 1. 10 V.S.A. § 5201 is amended to read:
5 § 5201. NOTICES; POSTING
6 (a)(1) An owner, or a person having the exclusive right to take game upon
7 land or the waters thereon, who desires to protect his or her the owner’s land or
8 private pond or propagation farm over which he or she the owner has exclusive
9 control may maintain notices stating that:
10 (A) the shooting hunting, trapping, or taking of game or wild animals
11 is prohibited or is by permission only;
12 (B) fishing or the taking of fish is prohibited or is by permission
13 only; or
14 (C) fishing, hunting, trapping, or taking of game or wild animals is
15 prohibited or is by permission only.
16 (2) “Permission only signs” authorized under this section shall contain
17 the owner’s name and a method by which to contact the property owner or a
18 person authorized to provide permission to hunt, fish, or trap on the property.
19 (b) Notices prohibiting the taking of game Notice signs shall be erected
20 upon or near the boundaries of lands to be affected with notices at each corner
21 and not over 400 feet apart along the boundaries thereof. Legible Notice signs

4 (c) The owner or person posting the lands shall record this posting annually
5 in the town clerk’s office of the town in which the land is located. The posting

10 the right to post the lands. The forms shall contain the information as to the
11 approximate number of acres posted, location in town, date of posting, and
12 signature of person so posting the lands. The town clerk shall file the record,
13 and it shall be open to public inspection. The town clerk shall retain a fee of
14 $5.00 for this recording.
