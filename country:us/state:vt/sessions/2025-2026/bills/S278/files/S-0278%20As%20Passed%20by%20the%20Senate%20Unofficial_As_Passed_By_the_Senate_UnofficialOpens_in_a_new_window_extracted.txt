4 Sec. 27a. COMMERCIAL CANNABIS COMPACT; INTENT
5 The General Assembly finds that the medical and commercial cannabis
6 industry has grown significantly throughout the United States since Vermont
7 transitioned to a recreational cannabis market in 2022. The General Assembly
8 further finds that recent statements from federal officials, including provisions
9 of Executive Order 14370, 90 F.R. 60541, “Increasing Medical Marijuana and
10 Cannabidiol Research,” indicate a shifting federal posture on regulated
11 cannabis markets. Accordingly, it is the intent of the General Assembly to
12 prepare for the possibility of regional or interstate cannabis markets by
13 authorizing the Governor to form agreements with other states that have
14 commercial cannabis markets.
