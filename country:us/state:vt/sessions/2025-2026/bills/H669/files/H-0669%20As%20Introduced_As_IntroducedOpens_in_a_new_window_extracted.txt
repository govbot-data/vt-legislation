14 It is hereby enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Vermont:
15 Sec. 1. 31 V.S.A. § 665 is added to read:
16 § 665. MOBILE AND INTERNET LOTTERY SALES; AUTHORITY AND
17 PROCEDURES
18 (a) Authority of the Board. The Board of Liquor and Lottery, through the
19 Commissioner of Liquor and Lottery, either independently or through a
20 contractor, is authorized to sell tickets, products, and subscriptions through

1 mobile applications, by mobile devices, or over the internet for the lottery
2 games established and operated pursuant to this chapter.
3 (b) Purchases only within the State. All lottery purchases made through
4 mobile applications, by mobile devices, or over the internet shall be initiated
5 and received within the State and shall not be intentionally routed outside the
6 State. The incidental intermediate routing of a purchase shall not determine
7 the location or locations in which the wager is initiated, received, or otherwise
8 made.
9 (c) Public Records Act exemptions. When produced or acquired by the
10 Department of Liquor and Lottery pursuant to this section, the following
11 records are exempt from public inspection and copying under the Public
12 Records Act and shall be kept confidential:
13 (1) personal, financial, and wager information associated with a person’s
14 purchase of tickets, products, and subscriptions;
15 (2) trade secrets, business records, financial records, and related
16 information; and
17 (3) records relating to security, technology, facilities, or systems used
18 for the sale of tickets, products, or subscriptions.
19 (d) Exemption review. The Public Records Act exemptions created in this
20 section shall not be subject to the provisions of 1 V.S.A. § 317(e).

1 (e) Procedures. The Board shall adopt procedures pursuant to 3 V.S.A.
