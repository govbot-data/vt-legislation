6 COVERED BRIDGES
7 The Secretary of Transportation, in consultation with the Secretary of
8 Commerce and Community Development, the Vermont League of Cities and
9 Towns, and, in the discretion of the Secretary of Transportation, other
10 stakeholders, shall:
11 (1) on or before November 15, 2026, develop and make available to
12 municipalities informational materials regarding establishing weight, height,
13 and width limits for covered bridges and the enhanced penalties pursuant to
14 23 V.S.A. § 1434(c) for persons who operate a vehicle on a covered bridge,
15 which exceeds the limits established for that bridge;
16 (2) provide technical assistance to municipalities in relation to
17 establishing weight, height, and width limits for covered bridges;
18 (3) provide information and technical assistance to municipalities
19 regarding the construction of barriers, warning devices, and other measures

9 § 503. SECOND DEGREE ARSON; ARSON OF A COVERED BRIDGE
