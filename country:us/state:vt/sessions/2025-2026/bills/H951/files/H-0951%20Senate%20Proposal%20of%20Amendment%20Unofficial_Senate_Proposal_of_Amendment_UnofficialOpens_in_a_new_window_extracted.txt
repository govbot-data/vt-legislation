5 * * * Purpose, Definitions, Legend * * *
6 Sec. A.100 SHORT TITLE
7 (a) This bill may be referred to as the “BIG BILL – Fiscal Year 2027
8 Appropriations Act.”
9 Sec. A.101 PURPOSE
10 (a) The purpose of this act is to provide appropriations for the operations of
11 State government and for capital appropriations not funded with bond proceeds
12 during fiscal year 2027. It is the express intent of the General Assembly that
13 activities of the various agencies, departments, divisions, boards, offices, and
14 commissions be limited to those that can be supported by funds appropriated in
15 this act or other acts passed prior to June 30, 2026. Agency and department
16 heads are directed to implement staffing and service levels at the beginning of
17 fiscal year 2027 so as to meet this condition unless otherwise directed by
18 specific language in this act or other acts of the General Assembly.
19 Sec. A.102 APPROPRIATIONS
20 (a) It is the intent of the General Assembly that this act serves as the
21 primary source and reference for appropriations for the operations of State

20 debt financing and are allowed under federal laws governing the use of State
21 bond proceeds as described in 32 V.S.A. § 309.

1 Sec. A.105 OFFSETTING APPROPRIATIONS
2 (a) In the absence of specific provisions to the contrary in this act, when
3 total appropriations are offset by estimated receipts, the State appropriations
4 shall control, notwithstanding receipts being greater or less than anticipated.
5 Sec. A.106 FEDERAL FUNDS
6 (a) In fiscal year 2027, the Governor, with the approval of the General
7 Assembly or the Joint Fiscal Committee if the General Assembly is not in
8 session, may accept federal funds available to the State of Vermont, including
9 block grants in lieu of, or in addition to, funds herein designated as federal.
10 The Governor, with the approval of the General Assembly or the Joint Fiscal
11 Committee if the General Assembly is not in session, may allocate all or any
12 portion of such federal funds for any purpose consistent with the purposes for
13 which the basic appropriations in this act have been made.
14 (b) If, during fiscal year 2027, federal funds available to the State and
15 designated as federal in this and other acts of the 2026 session of the General
16 Assembly are converted into block grants or are abolished under their current
17 title in federal law and reestablished under a new title in federal law, the
18 Governor may continue to accept such federal funds for any purpose consistent
19 with the purposes for which the federal funds were appropriated. The
20 Governor may spend such funds for such purposes for not more than 45 days
21 prior to General Assembly or Joint Fiscal Committee approval. Notice shall be

4 Sec. A.107 NEW POSITIONS
5 (a) Notwithstanding any other provision of law, the total number of
6 authorized State positions, both classified and exempt, excluding temporary
7 positions as defined in 3 V.S.A. § 311(a)(11), shall not be increased during
8 fiscal year 2027 except for new positions authorized by the General Assembly
9 during the 2026 session. Limited service positions approved pursuant to
10 32 V.S.A. § 5 shall not be subject to this restriction.
