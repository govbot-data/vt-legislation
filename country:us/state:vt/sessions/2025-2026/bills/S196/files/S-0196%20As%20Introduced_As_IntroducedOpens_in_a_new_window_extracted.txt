5 Subject: Taxation; property taxes; delinquent taxes; tax sale; redemption; right

7 Statement of purpose of bill as introduced: This bill proposes to reduce the
8 redemption period after a municipal tax sale and to authorize the purchaser of a
9 property at tax sale to enter the property during the redemption period for the
10 purpose of securing the property against illegal activity, fire hazard, damage,
11 or deterioration.
12 An act relating to property tax sales

5 Sec. 3. 32 V.S.A. § 5254a is added to read:
6 § 5254a. ENTRY TO SECURE PROPERTY
7 After providing the mortgagee or lien holder of record written notice of at

13 The amendments to 32 V.S.A. §§ 5253 and 5260 (notice and redemption)
14 shall not apply to a property that was subject to a notice of sale nor to a
