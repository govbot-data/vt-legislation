State of Vermont
House of Representatives
Montpelier, Vermont
Concurrent House Resolution
H.C.R. 255
Offered by Representatives Luneau of St. Albans City, Bartley of Fairfax, Branagan of Georgia,
Brigham of St. Albans Town, Demar of Enosburgh, Dickinson of St. Albans Town, Greer of
Bennington, Gregoire of Fairfield, Laroche of Franklin, Oliver of Sheldon, and Walker of Swanton
House concurrent resolution congratulating the 2026 Bellows Free Academy-St. Albans Comets
Division I girls’ championship ice hockey team
Whereas, the talented and collaborative Comets played skillfully all season, garnered the top playoff
seed, and was undefeated during its regular season prior to the championship contest against third-
seeded Essex, and
Whereas, having reached the decisive game in 2024 and 2025, and last year also against Essex, the
2026 Comets were determined to win this year’s championship, and
Whereas, at the University of Vermont’s Gutterson Fieldhouse, the winning path was started at the
11:46 mark of the first period, when, on a power play, Bellows Free Academy (BFA)-St. Albans scored
its opening goal, and with great offensive and defensive play, never lost the lead, concluding the contest
with a 3–1 victory, the Division I crown, and an 18-game undefeated streak, and
Whereas, the thrilled Comets (20–1–2) who skated strongly to the championship were Lily Desautels,
Haylie Lanfear, Chatham Brouillette, Emma Caster, Lillian Ferraro, Kaylyn Hughes, Jillian Smith,
Braelyn Fitzgerald, Isabella Stuart, Rylee LaRocque, Marion Pearson-Stanley, Marley Johnson, Hailey
Aylward, Marah McGinn, Gabby Fraties, Aiyana Lamos, Makayla Couture, Reina Spaulding, Kali
Cushing, Dahlia Steele, Anna Hibbard, Isabelle Mulheron, and JenessaYates, and
Whereas, Head Coach Luke Cioffi and assistant coaches Jeff Rouleau, Chelsea Forsyth, Erin
Shepard, Ben Lovelette, and Kriti Viens guided and inspired the team, now therefore be it
Resolved by the Senate and House of Representatives:
That the General Assembly congratulates the 2026 Bellows Free Academy-St. Albans Comets
Division I girls’ championship ice hockey team, and be it further
Resolved: That the Secretary of State be directed to send a copy of this resolution to BFA-St.
Albans.
