No. R-350. House concurrent resolution congratulating Elaine Larivee of Enosburg
Falls on her centennial birthday.
(H.C.R.292)
Offered by Representatives Branagan of Georgia, Bartley of Fairfax, Brigham of St.
Albans Town, Demar of Enosburgh, Dickinson of St. Albans Town, Gregoire of
Fairfield, Hango of Berkshire, Laroche of Franklin, Long of Milton, Luneau of St. Albans
City, Micklus of Milton, Oliver of Sheldon, and Walker of Swanton
Offered by Senators Brock and Norris
Whereas, a century ago, on May 6, 1926, Elaine Larivee was born in Quebec, where
she married a local farmer, and she has resided for many decades in the village of
Enosburg Falls, and
Whereas, during her long life, Elaine Larivee raised a large family of successful
children, and she is a proud grandmother and great-grandmother, and
Whereas, as a farmer’s wife, she supported her family through the challenges of the
agricultural cycles and the hard work associated with managing a farm, and
Whereas, Elaine Larivee is well known in Franklin County and is a faithful friend to
all, now therefore be it
Resolved by the Senate and House of Representatives:
That the General Assembly congratulates Elaine Larivee of Enosburg Falls on her
centennial birthday, and be it further
Resolved: That the Secretary of State be directed to send a copy of this resolution to
Elaine Larivee.
