9 Sec. 2. 10 V.S.A. § 4263 is amended to read:
10 § 4263. EXPIRATION; NONTRANSFERABILITY
11 Licenses shall not be transferable and shall be dated when issued. Annual
12 licenses shall be valid from January 1 of the calendar year for which they are
13 issued or from the day of purchase and issue in that year as certified by the
14 issuing agent. All annual licenses shall expire on December 31 of the year for
15 which they were issued or 365 days after the date on which they are issued, as
16 determined by the Commissioner. Term licenses shall be valid only for the
17 time period certified on the license form by the issuing agent. Any licenses
18 may be sold prior to the date that they are to become effective.
