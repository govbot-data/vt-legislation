Act No. 159 (H.928). An act relating to technical corrections to fish and wildlife statutes
Subjects: Conservation and development; licenses; fish and wildlife conservation;
penalties and enforcement
This act makes technical corrections to the uniform point system that assigns points
against a hunting or fishing license to those convicted of violation of a fish and wildlife
provision. This act also makes a change to when hunting or fishing licenses may expire
and makes a change to when the land management plan for the Champion lands is
updated. This act codifies in statute fees for the use of Department of Fish and Wildlife
lands and facilities that were previously outlined in a rule.
Effective Date: July 1, 2026
