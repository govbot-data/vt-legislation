14 Sec. 1. ADULT INVOLUNTARY GUARDIANSHIP WORKING
15 GROUP; REPORT
16 (a) Creation. There is created the Adult Involuntary Guardianship Working
17 Group to study jurisdiction of proceedings involving the involuntary
18 guardianship of adults. The Working Group shall examine the advisability of
19 consolidating adult involuntary guardianships under 14 V.S.A. chapter 111,
20 subchapter 12 (Title 14 involuntary guardianships) with guardianships for
