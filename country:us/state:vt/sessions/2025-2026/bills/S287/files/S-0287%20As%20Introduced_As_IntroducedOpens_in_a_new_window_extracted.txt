7 Statement of purpose of bill as introduced: This bill proposes to create a study
8 committee to examine the potential for constructing a new self-funding limited
9 access highway to connect the economic centers of Burlington, Vergennes,
10 Middlebury, and Rutland to Interstate 87 and replace Vermont Route 22A.
11 An act relating to studying the construction of a self-funding limited access
12 highway replacing Route 22A and linking Burlington, Vergennes,
13 Middlebury, and Rutland to Interstate 87
14 It is hereby enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Vermont:
15 Sec. 1. INTERSTATE 22A; STUDY COMMITTEE; REPORT
16 (a) Creation. There is created the Interstate 22A Limited Access Highway
17 Study Committee to examine the potential for constructing a new limited
18 access highway to connect the population centers of central-west and
19 northwest Vermont to Interstate 87 and replace Vermont Route 22A as the
20 primary route from Burlington to Interstate 87.

1 (f) Meetings.
2 (1) The Secretary of Transportation shall call the first meeting of the
3 Committee to occur on or before September 1, 2026.
4 (2) The Secretary of Transportation or designee shall be the Chair.
5 (3) A majority of the membership shall constitute a quorum.
6 (4) The Committee shall cease to exist on February 15, 2027.
7 (g) Compensation and reimbursement. Members of the Committee who
8 are not otherwise compensated for their attendance at meetings shall be entitled
9 to per diem compensation and reimbursement of expenses as permitted under
10 32 V.S.A. § 1010 for not more than six meetings. These payments shall be
11 made from monies appropriated to the Agency of Transportation.
