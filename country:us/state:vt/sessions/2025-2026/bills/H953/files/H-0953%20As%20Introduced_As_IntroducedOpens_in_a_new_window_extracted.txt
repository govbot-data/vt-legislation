1 § 5. RECALL OF ELECTED TOWN OFFICERS
2 (a) Any elected Town officer may be removed from office subject to the
3 following conditions and procedures:
4 (1) A written petition, signed by not less than 15 percent of the
5 registered voters of the Town, seeking the removal of a Town officer and
6 requesting a vote of the Town at a regular or special Town meeting called for
7 the purpose, shall be filed with the Selectboard and the Town Clerk.
8 (2) If the petition is filed not more than 60 days and not less than 47
9 days before the next regular Town meeting, the Selectboard shall place the
10 question of whether the elected officer shall be removed on the warning for the
11 next regular Town meeting. Otherwise, the Selectboard shall call a special
12 Town meeting, to be held within 45 days after receiving the petition, to vote on
13 whether the elected officer shall be removed. In either case, the recall vote
14 shall be held by Australian ballot.
15 (3) When the petitioned removal is approved by a majority of the votes
16 cast at the regular or special Town meeting, the Town officer named in the
17 petition shall cease to hold office.
18 (4) The officer shall be removed only if at least as many registered
19 voters of the Town vote to approve the petition as voted to elect said officer in
20 the most recent election wherein the officer was elected, or at least one-third of
21 the registered voters of the Town vote, whichever is fewer.
