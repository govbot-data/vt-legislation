Act No. M-11 (H.953). An act relating to approval of an amendment to the charter of the
Town of Panton
Subjects: Municipal charters; amendment; municipal and county government;
Town of Panton
This act amends the charter of the Town of Panton to add a new section containing the
procedures for voter-petitioned recall and removal of Town officers.
Effective Date: June 8, 2026
