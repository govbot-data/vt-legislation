3 (a) The Commissioner of Taxes shall study and provide recommendations

14 or more equines owned or boarded by a farmer for gain or profit, including
15 training, showing, and providing instruction and lessons in riding, training, and
16 the management of equines.
17 (3) “Farm buildings” has the same meaning as in 32 V.S.A. § 3752(14).
18 (4) “Farmer” has the same meaning as in 32 V.S.A. § 3752(7).
