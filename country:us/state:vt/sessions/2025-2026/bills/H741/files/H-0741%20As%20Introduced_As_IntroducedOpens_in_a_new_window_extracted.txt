6 Subject: Criminal procedures; failure to appear in court; arrest warrant
7 Statement of purpose of bill as introduced: This bill proposes to require the
8 court to issue an arrest warrant rather than a citation to appear when a criminal
9 defendant is released on conditions and subsequently fails to attend a
10 scheduled court hearing for the offense, or when a person fails to appear in
11 court after being issued a citation to appear by a law enforcement officer.
12 An act relating to requiring the court to issue an arrest warrant when a
13 criminal defendant fails to appear in court
14 It is hereby enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Vermont:
