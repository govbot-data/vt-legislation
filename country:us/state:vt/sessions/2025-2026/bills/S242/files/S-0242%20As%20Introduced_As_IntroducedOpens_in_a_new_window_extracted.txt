1 Sec. 2. APPROPRIATION; DEMENTIA RESPITE GRANT PROGRAM
2 (a) In fiscal year 2027, $750,000.00 shall be appropriated from the General
3 Fund to the Department of Disabilities, Aging, and Independent Living for
4 distribution to the Vermont Association of Area Agencies on Aging to fund the
5 Vermont Dementia Respite Grant.
6 (b) Twenty percent of the grant shall be used to fund “out-of-home day”
7 respite care, including adult day programs, meaning care provided in a group
8 setting outside the home during daytime hours.
9 (c) Individuals with dementia with an annual income at or below 400
10 percent of the federal poverty level shall be eligible for grant funds under this
11 section. Funds shall be capped at $3,000.00 per person for fiscal year 2027.
12 (d) Each area agency on aging shall maintain a waitlist for services funded
13 through this grant program. Funding shall be available on a first-come, first-
14 served basis.
15 Sec. 3. APPROPRIATION; DEMENTIA CAPABLE SOCIAL ACTIVITIES
16 (a) In fiscal year 2027, $100,000.00 shall be appropriated from the General
17 Fund to the Department of Disabilities, Aging, and Independent Living to
18 establish a one-year pilot program to create or expand dementia capable social
19 activities in Rutland and Washington Counties. The grant shall be
20 administered by the Department’s State Dementia Services Coordinator, in
