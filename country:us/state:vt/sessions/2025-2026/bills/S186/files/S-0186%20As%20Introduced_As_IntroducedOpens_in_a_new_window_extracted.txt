2 Introduced by Senator Hashim
3 Referred to Committee on
4 Date:
5 Subject: Juvenile proceedings; jurisdiction of Criminal Division; plea
6 agreements
7 Statement of purpose of bill as introduced: This bill proposes to permit a
8 juvenile offender in a criminal proceeding in the Criminal Division to elect to
9 enter a plea agreement to a lesser offense in the Criminal Division rather than
10 entering the plea agreement in the Family Division.
11 An act relating to jurisdiction of the Criminal Division over plea agreements
12 in proceedings related to juvenile offenders
13 It is hereby enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Vermont:
14 Sec. 1. 33 V.S.A. § 5201 is amended to read:
15 § 5201. COMMENCEMENT OF DELINQUENCY PROCEEDINGS
16 * * *
17 (d)(1) Any Unless subdivision (2) of this subsection applies, any
18 proceeding concerning a child who is alleged to have committed any offense
19 other than those specified in subsection 5204(a) of this title or subdivision
20 (c)(2) or (3) of this section before attaining 19 years of age shall originate in

3 (2) In a proceeding in the Criminal Division concerning a child who is
4 alleged to have committed an offense specified in subsection 5204(a) of this

9 Criminal Division shall retain jurisdiction over the proceeding.

11 Sec. 2. 33 V.S.A. § 5203 is amended to read:
12 § 5203. TRANSFER FROM OTHER COURTS
13 (a) If it appears to a Criminal Division of the Superior Court that the
14 defendant was under 19 years of age at the time the offense charged was
15 alleged to have been committed and the offense charged is an offense not
16 specified in subsection 5204(a) or subdivision 5201(c)(2) or (3) of this title,
17 that court shall, unless the child elects to enter a plea agreement in the Criminal
18 Division pursuant to subdivision 5201(d)(2) of this title, forthwith transfer the
19 proceeding to the Family Division of the Superior Court under the authority of
20 this chapter, and the minor shall then be considered to be subject to this chapter
21 as a child charged with a delinquent act.
