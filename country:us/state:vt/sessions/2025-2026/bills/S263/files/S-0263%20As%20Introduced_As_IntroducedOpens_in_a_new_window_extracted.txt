7 (a) Use. Deployment of ATLE systems on behalf of the Agency of

9 municipal law enforcement agency as provided pursuant to subsection (b) of
10 this section is intended to investigate the benefits of automated law
11 enforcement for speeding, red light, and vehicle noise violations as a way to
12 improve work crew safety and reduce traffic crashes resulting from an
13 increased adherence to traffic laws achieved by effective deterrence of
14 potential violators, which could not be achieved by traditional law enforcement
15 methods or traffic calming measures, or both. Deployment of ATLE systems
16 on behalf of the Agency and municipal law enforcement agencies is not
17 intended to replace law enforcement personnel, nor is it intended to mitigate
18 problems caused by deficient road design, construction, or maintenance.

16 in accordance with the proposal.
17 (c) A municipality may rescind the vote to approve ATLE use by a
18 majority vote of those present and voting at an annual or special meeting
19 warned for that purpose. A proposal to rescind the vote to approve ATLE use
20 may be brought by petition of five percent of the voters of the municipality.
