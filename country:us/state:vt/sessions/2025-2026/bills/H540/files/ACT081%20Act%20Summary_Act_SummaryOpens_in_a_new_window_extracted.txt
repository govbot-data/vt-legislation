Act No. 81 (H.540). An act relating to the recommendations of the Post-Adjudication
Reparative Program Working Group
Subjects: Crimes; corrections; sentencing; community reparative boards
The act adopts recommendations from the final report of the Post-Adjudication
Reparative Program Working Group. The act consolidates provisions for eligibility and
referral to the Restorative Justice Program, establishes factors a court shall consider in
referring an offender to participate in the Restorative Justice Program, and provides for
standardized referral forms and procedural rules.
Effective Date: July 1, 2026
