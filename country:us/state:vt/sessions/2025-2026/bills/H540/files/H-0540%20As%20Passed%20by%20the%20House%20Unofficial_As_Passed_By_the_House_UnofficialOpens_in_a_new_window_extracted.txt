16 28 V.S.A. chapter 12 in the case of an offender who has pled guilty to a

18 involve the subject areas prohibited for referral to a community justice center

20 this subdivision does not require the court to place the offender on probation.

5 Sec. 2. 28 V.S.A. § 910 is amended to read:
6 § 910. RESTORATIVE JUSTICE PROGRAM
7 This chapter establishes a program of restorative justice for use with
8 offenders required to participate in such a program as a sentence or a condition

10 order under 15 V.S.A. § 603. The Program shall be carried out by community
