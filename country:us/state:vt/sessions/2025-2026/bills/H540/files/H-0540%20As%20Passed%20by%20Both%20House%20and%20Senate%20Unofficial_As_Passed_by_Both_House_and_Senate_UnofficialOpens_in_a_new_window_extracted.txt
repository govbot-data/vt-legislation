4 Sec. 2. 28 V.S.A. § 910 is amended to read:
5 § 910. RESTORATIVE JUSTICE PROGRAM
6 This chapter establishes a program of restorative justice for use with
7 offenders required to participate in such a program as a sentence or a condition
8 of a sentence of probation or as ordered for civil contempt of a child support
9 order under 15 V.S.A. § 603. The Program shall be carried out by community
10 reparative boards under the supervision of the Commissioner, as provided by
11 this chapter.
