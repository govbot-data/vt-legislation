Act No. 101 (H.814). An act relating to neurological rights and the use of artificial
intelligence technology in health and human services
Subjects: Health; human services; health insurance; mental health; artificial
intelligence; neurological rights
This act expresses legislative intent with respect to artificial intelligence and
recognizes individuals’ neurological rights. It also modifies the membership of the
Artificial Intelligence Advisory Council, extends the Council’s continued existence until
June 30, 2030, and directs the Council to report to the General Assembly by January 15,
2027, on topics including protections for neurological rights and the ethical and
responsible use of artificial intelligence in health care, human services, education, public
participation, and public finance.
Effective Date: May 18, 2026
