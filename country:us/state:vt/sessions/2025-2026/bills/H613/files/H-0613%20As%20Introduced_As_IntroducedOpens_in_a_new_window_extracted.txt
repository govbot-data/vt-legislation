13 Statement of purpose of bill as introduced: This bill proposes to treat victims
14 of officer-involved shootings of an individual experiencing a mental health
15 crisis as crime victims with greater access to victims’ services and
16 compensation. This bill further proposes to authorize the Center for Crime
17 Victim Services to hire independent victim guardians to advocate for victims
18 and coordinate with the Department of State’s Attorneys and Sheriffs. This
19 bill also proposes to mandate mental health crisis training as part of the
20 Vermont Criminal Justice Council’s law enforcement officer certification and
21 training requirements. Finally, this bill proposes to incorporate mental health
