Act No. 108 (S.239). An act relating to the Child Abuse and Neglect Reporting Working
Group
Subjects: Human services; reporting abuse or neglect of children
This act creates the Child Abuse and Neglect Reporting Working Group for the
purpose of examining the existing statutes and the Department for Children and Families’
rules and policies regarding mandatory reporting of abuse and neglect of a child and
recommending changes to modernize them and reflect current best practices. The
Working Group is comprised of 13 members of various backgrounds and expertise
related to child abuse and neglect, and the act lists additional organizations that should be
consulted by the Working Group while engaged in its work. The Working Group is
required to provide an interim presentation to committees of jurisdiction on or before
April 1, 2027, and a final report on or before October 1, 2027. The Working Group will
have the administrative, technical, and legal assistance of the Department for Children
and Families.
Effective Date: May 21, 2026
