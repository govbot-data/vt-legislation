12 It is hereby enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Vermont:
13 Sec. 1. CHILD ABUSE AND NEGLECT REPORTING WORKING GROUP
14 (a) There is created the Child Abuse and Neglect Reporting Working
15 Group for the purpose of examining the existing statutes, rules, and policies
16 regarding the reporting of abuse and neglect of a child and recommending
17 changes to such to modernize them and reflect current best practices.
18 (b) The Working Group shall be composed of the following members:
