10 An act relating to prohibiting possession of semiautomatic assault weapons
11 It is hereby enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Vermont:
12 Sec. 1. 13 V.S.A. § 4028 is added to read:
13 § 4028. POSSESSION OF SEMIAUTOMATIC ASSAULT WEAPONS
14 PROHIBITED
15 (a) A person shall not possess a semiautomatic assault weapon.
16 (b) A person who violates this section shall be imprisoned for not more
17 than one year or fined not more than $500.00, or both.
18 (c) This section shall not apply to any semiautomatic assault weapon
19 possessed by:
