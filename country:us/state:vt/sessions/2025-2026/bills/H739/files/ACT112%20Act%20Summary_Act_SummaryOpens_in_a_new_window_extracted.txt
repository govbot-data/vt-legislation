Act No. 112 (H.739). An act relating to prohibiting the use and sale of the herbicide
paraquat
Subjects: Agriculture; prohibition; herbicides; control of pesticides; pests and plant
diseases; paraquat
This act bans the use and sale of the herbicide paraquat unless authorized by the
Secretary of Agriculture, Food and Markets. The Secretary may issue written permits for
the use or sale of paraquat within fruit-producing tree orchards or for berries or small
fruits. A written permit shall be valid for not more than three years or until December 31,
2030, whichever comes first. After December 31, 2030, this act bans the use and sale of
paraquat in the State entirely. This act requires that the Secretary provide an annual
report on the use of paraquat in the State.
Effective Date: November 1, 2026
