14 Statement of purpose of bill as introduced: This bill proposes to prohibit the
15 use and sale of the herbicide paraquat in the State. The bill would also define
16 paraquat to include herbicides known as “paraquat,” “paraquat dichloride,” and
17 “paraquat bis(methylsulfate).” The bill would authorize limited exemptions to
18 the prohibition on the use of paraquat only when no other less harmful
19 pesticide would be effective in addressing an environmental or agricultural
20 emergency. These exemption orders would be valid for up to one year and
