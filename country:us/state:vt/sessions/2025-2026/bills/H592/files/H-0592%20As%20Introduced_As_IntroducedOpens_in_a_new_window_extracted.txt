5 Subject: Civil procedure; forfeiture; right to counsel; record keeping
6 Statement of purpose of bill as introduced: This bill proposes to mandate the
7 assignment of a public defender for needy persons in certain civil forfeiture
8 proceedings. The bill would also provide for notice of the right to legal
9 counsel in such proceedings, along with mandates for law enforcement and
10 adjudicators to maintain records of forfeited property.
11 An act relating to the appointment of legal counsel and record keeping in
12 civil forfeiture actions
