* * * Conforming Revisions * * *
Sec. 8. CONFORMING REVISIONS
When preparing the Vermont Statutes Annotated for publication, the Office
of Legislative Counsel shall replace “mobile home” with “manufactured
home” throughout the statutes as needed for consistency with this act,
provided the revisions have no other effect on the meaning of the affected
statutes.
* * * Effective Dates * * *
Sec. 9. EFFECTIVE DATES
This act shall take effect on July 1, 2026, except that Secs. 5 and 6 (sales
and use tax exemption) shall take effect on January 1, 2027.
