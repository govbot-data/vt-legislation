7 § 2601. DEFINITIONS
8 (a) As used in this chapter, unless the context requires otherwise, “mobile
9 home” means:
10 (1) A mobile home as defined in 10 V.S.A. § 6201.
11 (2) An unmotorized vehicle, other than a travel or recreational trailer,
12 designed to be towed and designed or equipped for use as sleeping, eating, or
13 living quarters.
14 (b) A mobile home remains a mobile home for purposes of this chapter
15 even though it may be used for advertising, sales, display, or promotion of
16 merchandise or services, or for any other commercial purposes except the
17 transportation of property.
18 (c) A mobile home that was financed as residential real estate shall be
19 defined as residential real estate.
