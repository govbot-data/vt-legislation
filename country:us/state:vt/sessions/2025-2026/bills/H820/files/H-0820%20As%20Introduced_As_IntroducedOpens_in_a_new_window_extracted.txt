4 for the issuance of civil citations for violations of their respective enabling
5 statutes or rules adopted under those statutes that are enforceable in the
6 Environmental Division. Any proposed rule under this section shall include
7 the full, minimum, and waiver penalty amounts for each violation. The
8 maximum civil penalty for any violation brought under this section shall not
9 exceed $3,000.00 exclusive of court fees.
