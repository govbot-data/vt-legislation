7 Statement of purpose of bill as introduced: This bill proposes to require that
8 individuals who have committed certain speeding violations or whose
9 operator’s licenses or privilege to operate has been suspended or revoked due
10 to speeding and other violations obtain an intelligent speed assistance restricted
11 operator’s license or certificate and operate only vehicles equipped with an
12 intelligent speed assistance device.

14 It is hereby enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Vermont:
15 Sec. 1. SHORT TITLE
16 This act may be referred to as the “Super Speeder Accountability Act.”
