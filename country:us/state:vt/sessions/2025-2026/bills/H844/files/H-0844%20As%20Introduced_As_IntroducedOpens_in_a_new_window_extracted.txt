5 Subject: Utility tariffs; taxation; sales and use tax; sales tax exemptions; sales
6 tax on fuel; surcharge; weatherization assistance
7 Statement of purpose of bill as introduced: This bill proposes to disallow the
8 owner of a property from using the sales and use tax exemption for certain
9 fuels when the property has been used as a second home or short-term rental in
10 the past 12 months. The bill further proposes to impose a surcharge on
11 residential fuel sales to second homes and short-term rentals to help pay for
12 weatherization assistance.
13 An act relating to sales tax and a new surcharge paid on fuel by owners of
14 short-term rental and second home properties
15 It is hereby enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Vermont:
