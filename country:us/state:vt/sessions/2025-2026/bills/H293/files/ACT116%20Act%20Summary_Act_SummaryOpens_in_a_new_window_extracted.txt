Act No. 116 (H.293). An act relating to miscellaneous amendments to the Department of
Health’s reporting and programming requirements
Subjects: Health; disclosure of identifying information; health equity reporting;
cancer registry; amyotrophic lateral sclerosis registry
This act repeals several Department of Health reports, including reports related to the
Community Violence Prevention Program, the Emergency Service Provider Wellness
Commission, the number of service members and veterans who have the necessary
knowledge to obtain a food establishment license, statistics related to the setting of
individuals’ deaths and whether individuals received hospice at the time of death, and
lead screening. It also amends the submission date of reports related to the amyotrophic
lateral sclerosis registry, the Health Equity Advisory Commission, and health equity data
analyses. This act adds one new report, which requires the Department of Health to
review the income and expenditures of recovery service organizations by February 15,
2027. Lastly, this act also requires either institutional review board or privacy board
approval prior to the Commissioner of Health granting access to data in the cancer or
amyotrophic lateral sclerosis registries for research purposes.
Effective Date: July 1, 2026
