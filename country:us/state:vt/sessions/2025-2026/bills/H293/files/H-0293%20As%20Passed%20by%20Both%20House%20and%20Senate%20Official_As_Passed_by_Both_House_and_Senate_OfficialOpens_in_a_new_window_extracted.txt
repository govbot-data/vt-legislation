with other Agency of Human Services’ departments and recovery service

Appropriations, on Health Care, and on Human Services and to the Senate
Committees on Appropriations and on Health and Welfare containing
information on the total actual income and expenditures for recovery service
organizations in fiscal years 2024–2026. Specifically, the report shall address:
