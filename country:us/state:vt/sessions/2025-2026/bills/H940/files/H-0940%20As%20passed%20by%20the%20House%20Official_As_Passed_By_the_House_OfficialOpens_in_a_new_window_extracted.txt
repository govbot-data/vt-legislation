6 Statement of purpose of bill as introduced: This bill proposes to allow an
7 energy efficiency utility that is also a retail electric provider to use thermal
8 energy and process fuel funds on additional projects, to require regional
9 planning commissions to apply to the Department of Public Service for an
10 enhanced energy determination concurrently with their submission of draft
11 regional plans to the Land Use Review Board, to require the adoption of a new
12 Telecommunications Plan every five years instead of three, and to abolish the
13 Telecommunications and Connectivity Advisory Board.
14 An act relating to miscellaneous public utility subjects
15 It is hereby enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Vermont:

1 (a) Notwithstanding any provision of law or order of the Public Utility
