6 Subject: Conservation and development; public water supply; fluoridation;
7 prohibition
8 Statement of purpose of bill as introduced: This bill proposes to prohibit a
9 person from adding fluoride to drinking water provided by a public water
10 system. The bill also would prohibit a municipality from enacting an
11 ordinance or other provision that requires or permits fluoride to be introduced
12 into a public water system that the municipality or a subdivision of the
13 municipality administers, maintains, or operates. In addition, the bill would
14 prohibit a school from providing fluoride to a student as a supplement or
15 medicine.
16 An act relating to prohibiting the fluoridation of public water supplies
17 It is hereby enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Vermont:

9 public water system.

11 enact an ordinance or other provision that requires or permits fluoride to be
