12 An act relating to subscription renewal notifications
13 It is hereby enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Vermont:
14 Sec. 1. 9 V.S.A. § 2454a is amended to read:
15 § 2454a. CONSUMER CONTRACTS; AUTOMATIC RENEWAL
16 (a) A contract between a consumer and a seller or a lessor with an initial
17 term of one year or longer that renews for a subsequent term that is longer than
18 one month shall not renew automatically unless:
19 (1) the contract states clearly and conspicuously the terms of the
20 automatic renewal provision in plain, unambiguous language in bold-face type;

2 affirmative action to opt in to the automatic renewal provision; and
3 (3) if the consumer opts in to the automatic renewal provision, the seller

5 (A) not less than 30 days and not more than 60 days before the

13 the contract will renew automatically unless the consumer cancels the contract

15 (ii) the length and any additional terms of the renewal period.
16 (b) A seller or lessor under a contract subject to subsection (a) of this
17 section shall:
18 (1) provide to the consumer a toll-free telephone number, e-mail email
19 address, a postal address if the seller or lessor directly bills the consumer, or
20 another cost-effective, timely, and easy-to-use mechanism for canceling the
21 contract; and

1 (2) if the consumer accepted the contract online, permit the consumer to
2 terminate the contract exclusively online, which may include a termination e-
3 mail email formatted and provided by the seller or lessor that the consumer can

5 (c) A person who violates a provision of this section commits an unfair and
6 deceptive act in commerce in violation of section 2453 of this title.
7 (d) The provisions of this section do not apply to:
8 (1) a contract between a consumer and a financial institution, as defined
9 in 8 V.S.A. § 11101, or between a consumer and a credit union, as defined in
10 8 V.S.A. § 30101; or
11 (2) a contract for insurance, as defined in 8 V.S.A. § 3301a.
12 (e) A seller or a lessor, under any contract between a consumer and a seller
13 or a lessor with an initial term of one month or longer that renews indefinitely
14 without termination by the consumer, shall notify the consumer annually of the
15 right to terminate the contract. The notice required by this subsection shall
16 comply with the provisions of subsection (b) of this section.
17 Sec. 2. EFFECTIVE DATE
