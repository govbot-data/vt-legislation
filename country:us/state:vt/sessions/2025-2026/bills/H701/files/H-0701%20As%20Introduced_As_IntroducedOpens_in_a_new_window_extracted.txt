8 Statement of purpose of bill as introduced: This bill proposes to require any
9 action recommended by the Secretary of Education to correct deficiencies in
10 education quality standard compliance under 16 V.S.A. § 165(b) to be
11 supported by data. It also requires control of a school to be returned to a
12 school district if the Secretary assumes control to correct deficiencies and has
13 not done so within two years of assuming control.
