11 § 2453. PRACTICES PROHIBITED; ANTITRUST AND CONSUMER
12 PROTECTION
13 (a)(1) Unfair methods of competition in commerce and unfair or deceptive
14 acts or practices in commerce are hereby declared unlawful.
15 (2) It shall be unlawful for any person, partnership, or corporation to
16 disseminate, or cause to be disseminated, any false or misleading
17 advertisement. Advertisements shall be disseminated in language that is clear
18 and conspicuous.
19 (b) It is the intent of the Legislature that in construing subsection (a) of this
20 section, the courts of this State will be guided by the construction of similar
