2 An act relating to addressing and preventing chronic absenteeism
3 The Senate proposes to the House to amend the bill by striking out all after the
4 enacting clause and inserting in lieu thereof the following:
5 Sec. 1. FINDINGS
6 The General Assembly finds that:
7 (1) Chronic absenteeism is primarily an issue that should be addressed
8 through preventative, restorative, and assistance-based measures designed to
9 identify barriers to attendance and reconnect students with school. Schools
10 should respond to chronic absenteeism through written attendance support
11 plans, outreach to families, and appropriate academic, behavioral, and
12 community-based supports.
13 (2) Truancy is distinct from chronic absenteeism and constitutes a
14 student’s failure to comply with compulsory attendance requirements under
15 Vermont law. Truancy should serve as a legal enforcement mechanism only
16 after reasonable school-based interventions have been attempted and have not
17 resulted in improved attendance. Truancy proceedings should be reserved for
18 circumstances in which school-based interventions have not been successful
19 and formal legal enforcement is necessary to ensure compliance with
20 compulsory attendance laws.
