2 An act relating to addressing and preventing chronic absenteeism
3 It is hereby enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Vermont:
4 Sec. 1. FINDINGS
5 The General Assembly finds that:
6 (1) Chronic absenteeism is primarily an issue that should be addressed
7 through preventative, restorative, and assistance-based measures designed to
8 identify barriers to attendance and reconnect students with school. Schools
9 should respond to chronic absenteeism through written attendance support
10 plans, outreach to families, and appropriate academic, behavioral, and
11 community-based supports.
12 (2) Truancy is distinct from chronic absenteeism and constitutes a
13 student’s failure to comply with compulsory attendance requirements under
14 Vermont law. Truancy should serve as a legal enforcement mechanism only
15 after reasonable school-based interventions have been attempted and have not
16 resulted in improved attendance. Truancy proceedings should be reserved for
17 circumstances in which school-based interventions have not been successful
18 and formal legal enforcement is necessary to ensure compliance with
19 compulsory attendance laws.

21 It is the intent of the General Assembly that student attendance policies in
22 Vermont schools prioritize early identification, supportive intervention, and
