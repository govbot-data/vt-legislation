5 Sec. 3. 18 V.S.A. § 5325 is added to read:
6 § 5325. CEMETERY VANDALISM RESPONSE FUND
7 (a) Creation. The Cemetery Vandalism Response Fund is established to
8 provide grants to agencies to repair vandalized cemeteries. The Fund shall be
9 administered by the Vermont Old Cemetery Association (VOCA). Monies in
10 the Fund shall be used solely to provide cemetery vandalism response grants
11 pursuant to section 5324 of this title.
12 (b) Fees and Collection.
13 (1) Every agency shall contribute $5.00 to the Cemetery Vandalism
14 Response Fund for each burial or cremation it performs. A contribution shall
15 not be collected upon the burial of the remains of a deceased person where a
16 contribution was collected upon cremation.
17 (2) On or before January 15 of each year, every agency that has
18 performed a burial or cremation during the preceding calendar year shall
19 submit a check to VOCA for the total amount collected during the preceding
20 year toward payment to the Fund. Payment shall be accompanied by a
21 statement signed by a cemetery commissioner or other proper officer certifying
