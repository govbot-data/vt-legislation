10 An act relating to a tax return checkoff box for Hunger Free Vermont
11 It is hereby enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Vermont:
12 Sec. 1. 32 V.S.A. § 5862g is added to read:
13 § 5862g. HUNGER FREE VERMONT CHECKOFF
14 (a) Returns filed by individuals shall include, on a form prescribed by the
15 Commissioner of Taxes, an opportunity for the taxpayer to designate funds to
16 Hunger Free Vermont, Inc.
17 (b) Amounts so designated shall be deducted from refunds due to, or
18 overpayments made by, the designating taxpayers. All amounts so designated
19 and deducted shall be deposited in an account by the Commissioner of Taxes
20 for payment to Hunger Free Vermont, Inc. If at any time after the payment of
