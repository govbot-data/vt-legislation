6 Subject: Conservation and development; wastewater systems; servicing
7 Statement of purpose of bill as introduced: This bill proposes to require the
8 servicing and cleaning of wastewater systems at least every three years. The
9 bill would require the Agency of Natural Resources to identify in rule the types
10 of wastewater systems that would require regular cleaning. The rules would
11 require those systems requiring regular cleaning to be pumped and inspected
12 for issues and have their components cleaned if necessary. The bill would
13 require that, upon completion of servicing, the person servicing the wastewater
14 system submit a record of the servicing and cleaning to the Agency of Natural
15 Resources.
