14 Subject: Internal security and public safety; federal immigration authorities

16 judicial warrant from a federal immigration authority before the authority
17 enters a nonpublic area of a sensitive location. It also would terminate and
18 restrict the Department of Corrections’ assistance to federal immigration
19 authorities to the extent permitted under federal law. It would also limit how
20 federal immigration authorities can enforce civil immigration laws in Vermont

15 (A) be the primary authority to review a judicial warrant presented by
16 a federal immigration authority at the sensitive location; and
17 (B) designate at least one other employee to serve as a designee of
18 the supervisor in the event that the supervisor is not present when a federal
19 immigration authority appears at the sensitive location.
