12 Statement of purpose of bill as introduced: This bill proposes to define “high-
13 traffic online platform,” to require high-traffic online platforms to block the
14 posting of materially deceptive content related to elections in Vermont during
15 specified periods before and after an election, and to require these platforms to
16 appropriately label inauthentic, synthetic, or false content during specified
17 periods before and after an election in Vermont. This bill also proposes to
18 prohibit a person, committee, or other entity from knowingly distributing an
19 advertisement or other election communication that contains materially
20 deceptive content with malice within 120 days before an election in Vermont.
