5 Subject: Public service; health; weatherization; funding
6 Statement of purpose of bill as introduced: This bill proposes to continue the
7 tax on the purchase of fossil fuels to fund the Home Weatherization Assistance
8 Program. It would expand the eligibility criteria to apply for Home
9 Weatherization Assistance Program grants. It also proposes to accelerate and
10 increase administrative and financial support of weatherization trade workforce
11 recruitment and training programs funded through the Home Weatherization
12 Assistance Program.
13 An act relating to weatherization funding and weatherization workforce
14 recruitment
15 It is hereby enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Vermont:
16 Sec. 1. FINDINGS
17 The General Assembly finds that:
18 (1) In addition to the weatherization efforts provided under the current
19 Home Weatherization Assistance Program established in 33 V.S.A. chapter 25,
20 an increased pace of weatherization would benefit the health of Vermont

1 citizens, the environment, and the economy. Accelerated weatherization
2 efforts will:
3 (A) provide fuel cost savings and savings for the Low Income Home
4 Energy Assistance Program (LIHEAP) and the Home Heating Fuel Assistance
5 Program; and
6 (B) increase job opportunities in the field of weatherization and
7 alternative heating systems by recruiting and training new applicants to the
8 industry.
9 (2) Weatherization is a means for homeowners to protect the
10 environment and diminish the outflow of money to the fossil fuel industry.
11 (3) Workforce availability remains a limiting factor in Vermont’s effort
12 to comply with required reductions in greenhouse gas emissions from the
13 building sector. Increasing wages while aggressively recruiting and training
14 new applicants can eventually help to overcome the workforce shortage.
