5 RULE 804a. HEARSAY EXCEPTION; PUTATIVE VICTIM AGE 12 OR
6 UNDER 16 YEARS OF AGE; PERSON WITH A MENTAL
7 ILLNESS OR AN INTELLECTUAL OR DEVELOPMENTAL
8 DISABILITY
9 (a) Statements by a person who is a child 12 under 16 years of age or under
10 or who is a person with a mental illness as defined in 18 V.S.A. § 7101(14) or
11 intellectual or developmental disability as defined in 1 V.S.A. §§ 146, 148 at

13 court specifically finds at the time they are offered that:

18 assault of a child under 13 V.S.A. § 3253a, lewd or lascivious conduct under

20 § 2602, incest under 13 V.S.A. § 205, abuse, neglect, or exploitation under
21 33 V.S.A. § 6913, sexual abuse of a vulnerable adult under 13 V.S.A. § 1379,
