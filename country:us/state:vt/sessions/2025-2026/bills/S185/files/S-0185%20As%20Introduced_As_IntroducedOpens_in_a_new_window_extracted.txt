6 Sec. 2. 24 V.S.A. § 4348a(a)(12)(D) is amended to read:
7 (D) Transition or infill area. These areas include areas of existing or
8 planned commercial, office, mixed-use development, or residential uses either
9 adjacent to a planned growth or village area; or a new stand-alone transition or
10 infill area and served by, or planned for proximate to an area served by, public
11 water or wastewater, or both, an area where public water or wastewater is
12 planned for, or an area with soils that are adequate for wastewater disposal.
13 The intent of this land use category is to transform these areas into higher-
14 density, mixed-use settlements, or residential neighborhoods through infill and
15 redevelopment or new development. New commercial linear strip
16 development is not allowed as to prevent it negatively impacting the economic
17 vitality of commercial areas in the adjacent or nearby planned growth or
18 village area. This area could also include adjacent greenfields safer from
19 flooding and planned for future growth.

2 § 5804. DESIGNATED NEIGHBORHOOD
3 (a) Designation established.

5 LURB for designation of areas on the regional plan future land use maps as a
