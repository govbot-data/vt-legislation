9 An act relating to screening for fentanyl during a hospital-administered
10 urine drug screening
11 It is hereby enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Vermont:
12 Sec. 1. 18 V.S.A. § 4755 is added to read:
13 § 4755. FENTANYL; URINE DRUG SCREENING
14 (a) If a patient is treated in a hospital licensed pursuant to chapter 43 of this
15 title and the hospital conducts a urine drug screening to assist in diagnosing the
16 patient’s condition, the hospital shall include testing for fentanyl as part of the
17 urine drug screening.
18 (b)(1) A hospital conducting a urine drug screening pursuant to this section
19 and its employees shall not disclose the screening results for fentanyl to a law
