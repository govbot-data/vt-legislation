16 § 914. MENTAL HEALTH AND SUBSTANCE MISUSE LITERACY

1 * * * Peer-to-Peer Mental Health Support in Schools * * *
2 Sec. 2. PEER-TO-PEER MENTAL HEALTH SUPPORT PROGRAM
3 (a) It is the intent of the General Assembly to:
4 (1) define best practices and develop statewide standards for youth peer-
5 to-peer support programs; and
6 (2) strengthen protective factors among Vermont youth and prevent
7 future mental health challenges and substance misuse.
8 (b) The Department of Mental Health shall establish a four-year pilot
9 program for the distribution of grants that fund peer-to-peer mental health
10 support programs in public and approved independent schools. Peer-to-peer
11 mental health support programs shall enable students to provide and receive
12 peer support and coaching from other students in relation to mental health
13 challenges. Each program shall have the oversight of a certified peer support
14 provider and guidance from a school-based mental health service provider.
15 (c) The Department shall:
16 (1) execute a subcontract for youth outreach;
17 (2) host at least two listening sessions to engage children and youth;
18 (3) convene an advisory board to inform the design of the peer-to-peer
19 pilot program established by this section;
