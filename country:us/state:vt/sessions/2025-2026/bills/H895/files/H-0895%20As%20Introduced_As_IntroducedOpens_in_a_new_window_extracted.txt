10 Subject: Motor vehicles; secondary enforcement; searches
11 Statement of purpose of bill as introduced: This bill proposes to designate the
12 following motor vehicle violations as violations that may only be enforced if a
13 law enforcement officer has detained the operator of a vehicle for another
14 suspected violation: (1) failure to carry a registration certificate; (2) failure to
15 display unobstructed license plate numbers; (3) failure to display a license
16 plate on a trailer or semitrailer; (4) operation without a valid license under
17 certain circumstances; (5) operation by an individual with a learner’s permit
18 without a licensed driver in the vehicle; (6) operation on the right side of the
19 highway; (7) prolonged idling; (8) operation with an obstructed windshield or
20 windows; (9) operation of an uninspected motor vehicle; (10) operation with a
21 single broken taillight or brake light; (11) failure to signal a lane change or
