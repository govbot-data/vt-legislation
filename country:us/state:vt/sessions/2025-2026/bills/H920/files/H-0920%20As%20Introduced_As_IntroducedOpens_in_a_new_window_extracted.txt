11 Subject: Executive; Citizens’ Assembly
12 Statement of purpose of bill as introduced: This bill proposes to create the
13 Vermont Citizens’ Assembly. This body of 150 Vermont residents would
14 make legislative recommendations to the Vermont General Assembly. These
15 recommendations would address issues of significant public concern facing the
16 State. Participation would be voluntary, determined by a sortition process to
17 select a representative group of Vermont residents. Members would be paid a
18 daily wage equal to the wage of the median Vermonter and reimbursed for
19 lodging, food, child care, and travel costs. The Assembly would have a one-
20 year term and meet from mid-June to mid-November for two weekends per
21 month during this period. The Assembly would be a fully independent body
