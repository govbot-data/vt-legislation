2 Subchapter 3. Hemp
3 § 851. FINDINGS; PURPOSE
4 (a) Findings. The General Assembly finds that the federal legal status of
5 most hemp products will be contingent upon an amendment to 7 U.S.C.
6 § 1639o, to take effect in November 2026, pursuant to the Continuing
7 Appropriations, Agriculture, Legislative Branch, Military Construction and
8 Veterans Affairs, and Extensions Act of 2026, Pub. L. No. 119-37. The
9 legality of hemp and hemp products in interstate commerce is unsettled and
10 continues to evolve.
11 (b) Purpose. The purpose of this subchapter is to unify oversight of
12 cannabis and hemp-derived cannabinoids under the Cannabis Control Board to
13 more effectively prohibit illicit cannabis and cannabis product trade while
14 positioning growers and processors of nonintoxicating hemp products to take
15 advantage of national market opportunities that may exist. The purpose of this
16 subchapter is also to support small-business hemp producers and processors in
17 taking advantage of opportunities for the cultivation and sale of hemp and
18 hemp products.
