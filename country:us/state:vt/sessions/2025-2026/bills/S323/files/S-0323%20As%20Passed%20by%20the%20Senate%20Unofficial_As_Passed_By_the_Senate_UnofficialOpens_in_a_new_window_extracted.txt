5 Sec. 1. FINDINGS AND INTENT; MUNICIPAL REGULATION OF
6 AGRICULTURE
7 (a) For purposes of Sec. 2 of this act, the General Assembly finds that:
8 (1) Since enactment of 2004 Acts and Resolves No. 115, it has been
9 both the intent of the General Assembly and the controlling law that a
10 municipality shall not regulate farming, including the construction of farm
11 structures.
12 (2) The Vermont Supreme Court’s decision in In re 8 Taft Street DRB &
13 NOV Appeals, 2025 VT 27 reversed application of at least the past 20 years of
14 law to hold that municipalities may regulate farming by municipal bylaw.
15 (3) To avoid the unintended consequences of the decision in In re 8 Taft
16 Street DRB & NOV Appeals, 2025 VT 27, it is necessary for the General
17 Assembly to clarify and restate that municipalities under ordinance or bylaw
18 shall not regulate farming or the construction of farm structures as set forth in
19 24 V.S.A. § 4413(d).
