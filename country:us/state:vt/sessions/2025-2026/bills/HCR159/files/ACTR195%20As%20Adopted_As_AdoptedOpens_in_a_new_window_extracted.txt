No. R-195. House concurrent resolution congratulating the 2025 Milton High School
Yellowjackets Division II championship boys’ soccer team.
(H.C.R.159)
Offered by Representatives Steady of Milton, Micklus of Milton, Morgan, L. of
Milton, Morgan, M. of Milton, and Taylor of Milton
Offered by Senator Mattos
Whereas, the sixth-seeded Yellowjackets reached the Division II championship game
after a semifinal-round overtime victory over the second-ranked Harwood
Highlanders, and
Whereas, Milton faced the top-seeded Mt. Abraham Eagles for the Division II title in a
contest played at the D.G. Weaver Athletic Complex in Burlington, and
Whereas, the Yellowjackets dominated the first half and the Eagles the second,
resulting in an end-of-regulation play tie, just over two minutes into overtime, the
scenario-experienced Milton squad was prepared to execute a successful penalty kick,
securing a 1–0 game victory and the Division II title, and
Whereas, the triumphant Yellowjackets were Brody Allard, Alexander Harlow, Jack
Tomasi, Sam King, Colton Foss, Dante Matott, Cameron FitzGerald, Maxton Greenfield,
Killian Choquette, Mason Martel, Matthew Shedd, Andrew Preuss, Brady Stevens,
Matthew Cusson, Joshua Rhoades, Chase Renaud, Wyatt Wright, Wyatt Wooten, Matias
Ocampo Herrero, Noah Hecker, Brock Bushey, and Brandon Metcalf, and
Whereas, Head Coach Jack Hubbard and assistant coaches Eric Rhoades, Braden
Ripley, and Corey Lombard were essential guides for the team’s success,
now therefore be it
Resolved by the Senate and House of Representatives:
That the General Assembly congratulates the 2025 Milton High School Yellowjackets
Division II championship boys’ soccer team, and be it further
Resolved: That the Secretary of State be directed to send a copy of this resolution to
Milton High School.
