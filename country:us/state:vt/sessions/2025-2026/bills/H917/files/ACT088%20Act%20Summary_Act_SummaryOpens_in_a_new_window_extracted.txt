Act No. 88 (H.917). An act relating to military affairs
Subjects: Internal security; military affairs; National Guard; Adjutant General;
classification of State employees; military spouse preference; motor vehicles;
disabled veterans; municipal parking
This act replaces statutory references to “the Adjutant and Inspector General” with
“the Adjutant General” of the Vermont National Guard. It also creates a hiring
preference for military spouses for employment in State government, after the
requirements of a collective bargaining agreement have been satisfied and a diligent
effort has been made to fill a vacancy from within the classified service. Military spouses
who apply for and meet the requirements for any open competitive recruitment that uses a
point-based examination for employment with the State, and who receive a passing score,
have an additional five points added to their competitive examination rating. Finally, this
act permits a vehicle with disabled veteran license plates issued by any state to park at a
municipal parking meter for free.
Effective Date: July 1, 2026
