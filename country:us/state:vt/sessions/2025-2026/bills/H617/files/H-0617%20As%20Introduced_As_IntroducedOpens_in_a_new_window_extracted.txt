5 Subject: Education; health services; seizure action plan
6 Statement of purpose of bill as introduced: This bill proposes to require school
7 districts and approved independent schools to create seizure action plans for
8 students diagnosed with a seizure disorder, designate school personnel to
9 administer or assist with the self-administration of seizure rescue medications
10 and vagus nerve stimulators, require parent authorization for the administration
11 of such medications within a school, and provide training for school personnel
12 and students on seizures and seizure disorders, and also requires the Agency of
13 Education to collect certain information regarding compliance with these
14 requirements and report it to the General Assembly annually.
15 An act relating to seizure disorders in schools
16 It is hereby enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Vermont:
17 Sec. 1. 16 V.S.A. § 1370 is added to read:
