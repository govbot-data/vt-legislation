10 It is hereby enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Vermont:
11 Sec. 1. 3 V.S.A. § 903 is amended to read:
12 § 903. EMPLOYEES’ RIGHTS AND DUTIES; PROHIBITED ACTS
13 * * *
14 (b) A State employee may not strike or recognize a picket line of an
15 employee or labor organization while in the performance of his or her the
16 employee’s official duties; provided, however, that this prohibition shall not
17 apply to employees of the Vermont State Colleges or the University of
18 Vermont.
