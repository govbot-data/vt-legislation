State of Vermont
Senate Chamber
Montpelier, Vermont
Joint Senate Resolution
By Senator Baruth,
J.R.S. 30. Joint resolution to provide for a Joint Assembly to receive the
State-of-the-State message from the Governor.
Resolved by the Senate and House of Representatives:
That the two Houses meet in Joint Assembly on Wednesday, January 7,
2026, at two o’clock in the afternoon to receive the State-of-the-State message
from the Governor.
