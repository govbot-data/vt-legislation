8 An act relating to publicly accessible meetings of hospital boards
9 It is hereby enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Vermont:
10 Sec. 1. 18 V.S.A. § 1911c is added to read:
11 § 1911c. HOSPITAL BOARD MEETINGS
12 (a) Application. This section shall apply to all regular, special, and
13 emergency meetings of a hospital board, whether in person or by electronic
14 means, as well as to any other assemblage of members of the hospital board at
15 which binding action is taken on behalf of the hospital or an affiliated entity.
16 As used in this section, “hospital board” means a hospital’s board of directors,
17 board of trustees, or other governing body.
18 (b) Public meetings; exceptions. Meetings of a hospital board shall be
19 open to the public and shall provide members of the public an opportunity to

3 (d) Minutes and recordings.
4 (1) All portions of each hospital board meeting that are open to the

13 (e) Participation by electronic or other means.
14 (1) One or more members of a hospital board may attend a regular,

16 physically present at a designated meeting location.

18 other means may participate fully in discussing the board’s business and voting
