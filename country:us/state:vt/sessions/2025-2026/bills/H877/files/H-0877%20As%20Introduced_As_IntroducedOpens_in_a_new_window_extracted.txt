6 Sec. 2. 31 V.S.A. § 1002 is amended to read:
7 § 1002. GREEN MOUNTAIN PASSPORT; ELIGIBILITY
8 (a) Any person is eligible to obtain a Green Mountain Passport who:
9 (1) is:
10 (A) at least 62 years of age; or
11 (B) a veteran of the uniformed services; or
12 (C) a person with a disability; and
13 (2) is a resident of the State.
14 (b) A Green Mountain Passport application shall be issued by the clerk of
15 any town to a resident of that town who swears under oath that he or she the
16 resident meets the eligibility criteria established pursuant to subsection (a) of
17 this section. A fee of $2.00 will be charged at the time of application and shall
18 be retained by the town issuing the passport. The town clerk shall issue the
19 actual passport. Records shall be maintained as prescribed by law. Statistical
20 information shall be furnished to the Commissioner upon request.
