12 An act relating to addressing education spending in fiscal years 2028 and

14 It is hereby enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Vermont:
15 Sec. 1. ALLOWABLE GROWTH IN EDUCATION SPENDING FOR
16 FISCAL YEARS 2028 AND 2029
17 (a) Notwithstanding any other provision of law, for fiscal years 2028 and
18 2029 only, a school district’s per pupil education spending, as defined in
19 16 V.S.A. § 4001(14), shall not exceed the sum of the district’s per pupil
20 education spending in the prior fiscal year and the district’s allowable growth.

1 (b) As used in this section:
2 (1) “Allowable growth” for a school district means the product of the
3 district’s allowable growth percentage and the district’s per pupil education
4 spending in the prior fiscal year.
5 (2)(A) “Allowable growth percentage” for a school district means nine
6 percent of the difference in per pupil education spending in the prior fiscal year
7 between the district and the highest spending district, excluding gores,
8 expressed as a percentage of the district’s per pupil education spending in the
9 prior fiscal year, provided that the minimum allowable growth percentage shall
10 be three percent.
11 (B) A school district’s “allowable growth percentage” is calculated as
12 follows:
13 (i) subtract the district’s per pupil education spending in the prior
14 fiscal year from the highest per pupil education spending in any district,
15 excluding gores, in the prior fiscal year;
16 (ii) divide the difference determined under subdivision (i) of this
17 subdivision (2)(B) by the district’s per pupil education spending in the prior
18 fiscal year; and
19 (iii) multiply the percentage determined under subdivision (ii) of
20 this subdivision (2)(B) by nine percent.
