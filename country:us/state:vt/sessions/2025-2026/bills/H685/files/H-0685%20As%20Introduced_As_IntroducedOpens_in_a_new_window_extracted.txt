9 An act relating to health insurance coverage for scalp cooling systems
10 It is hereby enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Vermont:
11 Sec. 1. 8 V.S.A. § 4095d is amended to read:
12 § 4095d. CHEMOTHERAPY TREATMENT AND ORAL ANTICANCER
13 MEDICATIONS
14 (a) A health insurance plan shall provide coverage for medically necessary
15 growth cell stimulating factor injections taken as part of a prescribed
16 chemotherapy regimen.
17 (b)(1) A health insurance plan shall provide coverage for scalp cooling
18 systems used in connection with cancer chemotherapy treatment.
19 (2) As used in this subsection, “scalp cooling system” means any device
20 used to cool the human scalp to prevent or reduce hair loss during cancer
