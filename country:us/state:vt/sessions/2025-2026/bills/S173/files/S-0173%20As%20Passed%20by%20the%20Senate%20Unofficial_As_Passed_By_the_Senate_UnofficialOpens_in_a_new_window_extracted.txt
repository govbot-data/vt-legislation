4 Sec. 1. 21 V.S.A. § 641 is amended to read:
5 § 641. VOCATIONAL REHABILITATION
6 (a) When as a result of an injury covered by this chapter, an employee is
7 unable to perform work for which the employee has previous training or
8 experience, the employee shall be entitled to vocational rehabilitation services,
9 including retraining and job placement, as may be reasonably necessary to
10 restore the employee to suitable employment. Vocational rehabilitation
11 services shall be provided as follows:
12 (1) The employer shall designate a vocational rehabilitation provider
13 from a list provided by the Commissioner to initially provide services.
14 Thereafter, absent good cause, the employee may have only one opportunity to
15 select another vocational rehabilitation provider from a list provided by the
16 Commissioner upon giving the employer written notice of the employee’s
17 reasons for dissatisfaction with the designated provider and the name and
18 address of the provider selected by the employee.
19 (2) The Department shall provide an injured worker with a form that
20 includes information and employee rights. The form shall clearly and simply
21 explain the worker’s rights, including the choice of provider, the right to

12 Sec. 2. VOCATIONAL REHABILITATION WORKING GROUP; REPORT
13 (a) Creation. There is created the Vocational Rehabilitation Working
14 Group to provide recommendations to the General Assembly on how to
15 improve the current vocational rehabilitation system to ensure that it meets the
16 needs of eligible injured workers in a timely and cost-effective manner.
17 (b) Membership. The Working Group shall be composed of the following
18 members:
19 (1) the Director of Workers’ Compensation and Safety or designee;

15 the following questions:
16 (1) What mechanisms could better identify which claimants are likely to
17 require vocational rehabilitation services?
18 (2) Could utilization of vocational services be improved by enabling
19 claimants to access vocational rehabilitation benefits while receiving wage
20 replacement benefits?
