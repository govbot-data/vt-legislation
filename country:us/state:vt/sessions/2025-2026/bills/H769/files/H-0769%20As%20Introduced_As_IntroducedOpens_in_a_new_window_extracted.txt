9 Subject: Human services; abuse or neglect of children
10 Statement of purpose of bill as introduced: This bill proposes to establish that
11 permitting a child to engage in independent activities without supervision,
12 provided the activities are appropriate based on the child’s age, maturity, and
13 physical condition, does not constitute neglect. It also proposes to make
14 miscellaneous changes to the standards for substantiating a parent or guardian
15 for child abuse or neglect.

1 Sec. 5. IMPLEMENTATION
2 (a) On or before January 1, 2027, the Department for Children and Families
3 shall update any policies to reflect changes included in this act and conduct
4 public outreach to inform families and the community about the protections in
5 law for reasonable childhood independence.
6 (b) On or before July 1, 2027, the Department for Children and Families
7 shall conduct training for appropriate staff regarding the changes included in
8 this act and any updated policies.
9 Sec. 6. EFFECTIVE DATE
10 This act shall take effect on July 1, 2026.
