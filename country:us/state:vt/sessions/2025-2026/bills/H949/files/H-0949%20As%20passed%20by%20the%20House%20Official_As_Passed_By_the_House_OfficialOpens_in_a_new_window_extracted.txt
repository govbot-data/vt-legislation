9 Statement of purpose of bill as introduced: This bill proposes to set the
10 property dollar and income dollar equivalent yields for the purpose of setting
11 homestead property taxrates and proposes to set the nonhomestead property
12 taxrate. This bill also proposes to reserve monies within the Education Fund
13 to offset property taxrate increases. This bill also proposes a technical
14 correction to the statewide adjustment for the property taxcredit system. This
15 bill also proposes to refund education property taxoverpayment made by the
16 City of Barre due to miscalculation of the amount of taxincrement to be
17 retained by the City from its taxincrement financing district fund. This bill
18 also proposes to adjust the special education census grant for inflation for
19 fiscal year 2027 and onward.
