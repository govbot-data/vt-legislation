Act No. M-10 (H.902). An act relating to approval of amendments to the charter of the
City of Barre
Subjects: Municipal charters; amendment; municipal and county government; City
of Barre
This act amends the charter of the City of Barre to authorize the City Manager or any
City councilor duly authorized by the City Council to sign conveyances, grants, or leases
of real property. This act also amends the charter to increase the maximum amount of
unexpended funds that may be retained in an undesignated fund balance at the close of
the City’s fiscal year audit.
Effective Date: June 8, 2026
