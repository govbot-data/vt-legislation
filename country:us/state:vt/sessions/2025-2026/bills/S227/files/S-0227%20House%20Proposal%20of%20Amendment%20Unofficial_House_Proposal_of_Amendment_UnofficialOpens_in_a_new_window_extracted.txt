1 S.227
2 An act relating to creating immigration protocols in Vermont schools
3 The House proposes to the Senate to amend the bill by striking out all after
4 the enacting clause and inserting in lieu thereof the following:
5 Sec. 1. PURPOSE
6 The purpose of this act is to secure the right of every child to equal access
7 to a free public education and to a school that is safe from intimidation and
8 fear, regardless of immigration status. In order to ensure the right to
9 educational equality, schools must take steps to protect the integrity of school
10 learning environments for all children, so that no parent is discouraged from
11 sending a child to, and no child is discouraged from attending, school,
12 including due to the threat of immigration enforcement on a school campus.
