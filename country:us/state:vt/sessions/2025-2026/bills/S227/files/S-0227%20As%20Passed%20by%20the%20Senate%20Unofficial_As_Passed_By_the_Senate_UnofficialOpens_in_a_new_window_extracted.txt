1 S.227
2 An act relating to creating immigration protocols in Vermont schools
3 It is hereby enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Vermont:
4 Sec. 1. PURPOSE
5 The purpose of this act is to secure the right of every child to equal access
6 to a free public education and to a school that is safe from intimidation and
7 fear, regardless of immigration status. In order to ensure the right to
8 educational equality, schools must take steps to protect the integrity of school
9 learning environments for all children, so that no parent is discouraged from
10 sending a child to, and no child is discouraged from attending, school,
11 including due to the threat of immigration enforcement on a school campus.
