5 Subject: Elections; conduct of elections; process of voting; voter identification
6 Statement of purpose of bill as introduced: This bill proposes to require voters
7 to present valid identification, based on proof of citizenship, when voting in
8 person or by early or absentee ballot. This bill will also require the
9 Commissioner of Motor Vehicles to provide free nondriver identification cards
10 to all eligible voters in the State for the purpose of voting, upon presentation of
11 documented proof of U.S. citizenship and proof of residency.
12 An act relating to requiring voters to present identification when voting
13 It is hereby enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Vermont:

10 Sec. 4. FREE STATE-ISSUED VOTER IDENTIFICATION CARDS
11 The Commissioner of Motor Vehicles shall provide free nondriver
12 identification cards to all eligible voters in the State for the purpose of voting,
13 upon presentation of documented proof of U.S. citizenship and proof of
14 residency. Such cards shall meet the requirements for acceptable voter
15 identification under 17 V.S.A. § 2563 and shall indicate U.S. citizenship status.
16 The Commissioner shall adopt rules in accordance with 3 V.S.A. chapter 25
17 regarding the manner of accepting required documentation and issuance and
18 renewal of voter identification cards, including provisions for the expedited
19 issuance and renewal of voter identification cards, and the duration of validity
20 of the voter identification cards.
