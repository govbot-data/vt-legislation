6 Subject: Health care; residential care homes; report
7 Statement of purpose of bill as introduced: This bill proposes to require the
8 Agency of Human Services to submit a report to the General Assembly
9 regarding the creation of one or more residential care homes, which may be
10 level III or otherwise, for individuals exiting a hospital who cannot safely
11 convalesce at home and who do not require a nursing home level of care. The
12 report would identify whether there is a need for residential care homes, level
13 III or otherwise, and if so, identify where the need is; identify how one or more
14 residential care homes, level III or otherwise, might generate savings in the
15 health care system; identify funding sources for residential care homes, level
16 III or otherwise; and recommend how the Agency might partner with
17 community-based organizations to develop residential care homes, level III or
18 otherwise, as needed statewide.
19 An act relating to reporting on residential care homes
