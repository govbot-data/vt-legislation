5 Subject: Health; hospitals; service reductions; Agency of Human Services;

7 Statement of purpose of bill as introduced: This bill proposes to require a
8 hospital to provide notice and opportunity to comment and to receive approval
9 from the Agency of Human Services prior to reducing or eliminating any
10 hospital services. It would also require the Agency to report any approved
11 service reductions or eliminations to the Green Mountain Care Board to allow
12 the Board to make appropriate adjustments to the hospital’s budget.
13 An act relating to an approval process for reducing or eliminating hospital
14 services
15 It is hereby enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Vermont:
16 Sec. 1. 18 V.S.A. § 9405d is added to read:
17 § 9405d. HOSPITAL SERVICE REDUCTIONS; APPROVAL REQUIRED
18 (a) A hospital shall not reduce or eliminate any service without approval
19 from the Secretary of Human Services as set forth in this section.
