4 It is hereby enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Vermont:
5 Sec. 1. 18 V.S.A. § 9405d is added to read:
6 § 9405d. HOSPITAL SERVICE REDUCTIONS; NOTICE REQUIRED
7 (a) A hospital that proposes to intentionally reduce or eliminate any service
8 shall:
9 (1) Provide a notice of intent to the Agency of Human Services, the
10 Green Mountain Care Board, the Office of the Health Care Advocate, and the
11 members of the General Assembly who represent the hospital service area not
12 less than 60 days prior to the proposed reduction or elimination. The notice
13 shall explain the rationale for the proposed reduction or elimination and
14 describe how it is consistent with the Statewide Health Care Delivery Strategic
15 Plan, once established, and the hospital’s most recent community health needs
16 assessment conducted pursuant to section 9405a of this title and 26 U.S.C.
17 § 501(r)(3).
18 (2) Post the notice of intent on the hospital’s website beginning on or
19 before the day on which the notice is provided pursuant to subdivision (1) of
20 this subsection.
