Sec. 3. BURLINGTON COMMUNITY JUSTICE CENTER; ADULT
DIVERSION PILOT; CRIMINAL MUNICIPAL ORDINANCE
VIOLATIONS
Notwithstanding the limitation in 3 V.S.A. § 164(a)(2) relating to restorative
options for persons alleged to have caused harm in violation of a criminal
statute or who have been charged with violating a criminal statute, the referral
of criminal municipal ordinance violations to the Burlington Community
Justice Center is authorized under 3 V.S.A. § 164 from January 1, 2026, until
July 1, 2027.
Sec. 4. EFFECTIVE DATES
(a) This section and Sec. 3 shall take effect on passage.
(b) Secs. 1 and 2 shall take effect on July 1, 2026.
