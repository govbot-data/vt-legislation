14 Moderator; to authorize the Selectboard to appoint the members of the Town
15 Cemetery Commission; to eliminate certain officers from the list of officers
16 that shall be appointed by the Selectboard; to require the Selectboard to
17 employ a public accountant to examine the financial statements of the funds of
18 the Town; and to correct grammar, references, and style throughout the charter.

7 CHAPTER 117. TOWN OF ESSEX
8 * * *
9 § 101. CORPORATE EXISTENCE RETAINED
10 The inhabitants of the Town of Essex, within the corporate limits as now
11 established, shall continue to be a municipal corporation by the name of the
12 Town of Essex. Notwithstanding the provisions of any other municipal
13 charters, territory within the corporate limits shall not be annexed to or become
14 a part of any other municipal corporation except by annexation procedures as
15 set forth in the statutes of the State of Vermont Statutes Annotated.
16 § 102. GENERALLAW, APPLICATION
17 Except as modified by the provisions of this charter, or by any lawful
18 regulation or ordinance of the Town of Essex, all provisions of the statutes of
19 this State Vermont Statutes Annotated applicable to towns shall apply to the
20 Town of Essex.
