Act No. M-6 (H.516). An act relating to approval of amendments to the charter of the
Town of Essex
Subjects: Municipal charters; municipal and county government; amendments;
Town of Essex
This act amends the charter of the Town of Essex (24 App. V.S.A. chapter 117) to add
bases and procedures for the vacancy and temporary appointment of members of the
Selectboard and Town Moderator, amend the offices appointed by the Selectboard or
Town Manager, add language relating to the employment of a public accountant, amend
provisions related to the publication and distributions of the Annual Auditor’s Report and
Annual Town Report, add language relating to the review of property appraisals by the
Department of Assessment, and make technical corrections throughout the Town charter.
Effective Date: March 16, 2026
