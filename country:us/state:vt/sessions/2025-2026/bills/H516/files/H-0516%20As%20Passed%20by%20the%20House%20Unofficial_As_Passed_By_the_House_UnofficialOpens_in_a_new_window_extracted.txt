12 § 101. CORPORATE EXISTENCE RETAINED
13 The inhabitants of the Town of Essex, within the corporate limits as now
14 established, shall continue to be a municipal corporation by the name of the
15 Town of Essex. Notwithstanding the provisions of any other municipal
16 charters, territory within the corporate limits shall not be annexed to or become
17 a part of any other municipal corporation except by annexation procedures as
18 set forth in the statutes of the State of Vermont Statutes Annotated.
