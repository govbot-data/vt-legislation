14 Sec. 1. 2 V.S.A. § 24 is added to read:
15 § 24. MEMBERS OF GENERAL ASSEMBLY; AUTHORIZATION TO
16 CARRY CONCEALED HANDGUNS IN STATE HOUSE
17 (a) Notwithstanding any other provision of law or rule, a member of the
18 General Assembly may carry a concealed handgun in the State House or on the
19 State House grounds if the member has completed a firearms safety and
20 training class from a certified firearms instructor.
