10 An act relating to approval of amendments to the charter of the Town of

14 The General Assembly approves the amendments to the charter of the Town
15 of Williston as set forth in this act. The voters approved the proposals of
16 amendment on March 3, 2026.

1 (C) The Town may vote by a majority of the legal voters present at
2 an annual or special meeting duly warned for that purpose to eliminate the
3 office of lister or change the office to an appointed position. If the Town votes
4 to eliminate the office of lister, the Town manager shall contract with or
5 employ a professionally qualified assessor, who need not be a resident of the
6 Town. The assessor shall have the same powers, discharge the same duties,
7 proceed in the discharge thereof in the same manner, and be subject to the
8 same liabilities as are prescribed for lister or board of listers under the
9 provisions of Title 32 of the Vermont Statutes Annotated.
10 (D) A vote to eliminate the office of lister shall remain in effect until
11 rescinded by majority vote of the legal voters present at an annual or special
12 meeting warned for that purpose.

11 § 21. ASSESSMENT OF PROPERTY
12 (a) The Town shall employ or contract with one or more professionally
13 qualified assessors who meet, prior to conducting any assessment work for the
14 Town, the training requirements established by the Director of Property
15 Valuation and Review under 32 V.S.A. § 4052.
16 (b) The assessors shall have the same powers, discharge the same duties,
17 proceed in the discharge of those duties in the same manner, and be subject to
18 the same liabilities as are prescribed for listers under the provisions of Title 32.
