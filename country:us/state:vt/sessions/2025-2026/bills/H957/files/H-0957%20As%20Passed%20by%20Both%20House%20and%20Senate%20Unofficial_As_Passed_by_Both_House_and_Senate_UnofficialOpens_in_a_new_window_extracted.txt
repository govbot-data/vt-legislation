5 (3) Listers.
6 (A) There shall be three elected listers elected on a nonpartisan basis
7 for three-year staggered terms.
8 (B) Duties and responsibilities of the listers shall be in accordance
9 with State statute and herein limited.
10 (C) The Town may vote by a majority of the legal voters present at
11 an annual or special meeting duly warned for that purpose to eliminate the
12 office of lister or change the office to an appointed position. If the Town votes
13 to eliminate the office of lister, the Town manager shall contract with or
14 employ a professionally qualified assessor, who need not be a resident of the
15 Town. The assessor shall have the same powers, discharge the same duties,
16 proceed in the discharge thereof in the same manner, and be subject to the
17 same liabilities as are prescribed for lister or board of listers under the
18 provisions of Title 32 of the Vermont Statutes Annotated.
19 (D) A vote to eliminate the office of lister shall remain in effect until
20 rescinded by majority vote of the legal voters present at an annual or special
21 meeting warned for that purpose.
