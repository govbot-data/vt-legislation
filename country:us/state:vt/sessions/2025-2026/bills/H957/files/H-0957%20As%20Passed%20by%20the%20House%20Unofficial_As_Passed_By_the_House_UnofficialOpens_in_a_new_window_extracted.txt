6 (3) Listers.
7 (A) There shall be three elected listers elected on a nonpartisan basis
8 for three-year staggered terms.

11 (C) The Town may vote by a majority of the legal voters present at
12 an annual or special meeting duly warned for that purpose to eliminate the
13 office of lister or change the office to an appointed position. If the Town votes
14 to eliminate the office of lister, the Town manager shall contract with or
15 employ a professionally qualified assessor, who need not be a resident of the
16 Town. The assessor shall have the same powers, discharge the same duties,
17 proceed in the discharge thereof in the same manner, and be subject to the
18 same liabilities as are prescribed for lister or board of listers under the
19 provisions of Title 32 of the Vermont Statutes Annotated.

2 § 21. ASSESSMENT OF PROPERTY
3 (a) The Town shall employ or contract with one or more professionally
4 qualified assessors who meet, prior to conducting any assessment work for the
5 Town, the training requirements established by the Director of Property
6 Valuation and Review under 32 V.S.A. § 4052.
7 (b) The assessors shall have the same powers, discharge the same duties,
8 proceed in the discharge of those duties in the same manner, and be subject to
9 the same liabilities as are prescribed for listers under the provisions of Title 32.
10 Sec. 3. TRANSITIONARY PROVISION
11 The term of office of any lister in office in the Town of Williston shall
12 expire 45 days after the passage of this act or upon the date on which an
13 assessor is employed or contracted with in accordance with 24 App. V.S.A. ch.
14 171, § 21, whichever occurs last.
15 Sec. 4. EFFECTIVE DATE
16 This act shall take effect on passage.
