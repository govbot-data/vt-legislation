5 Subject: Alcoholic beverages, cannabis, and tobacco; Board of Liquor and

7 beverages for off-premises consumption; special events permits; local
8 control commissioners
9 Statement of purpose of bill as introduced: This bill proposes to amend
10 various provisions of Title 7 to change the term length for members of the
11 Board of Liquor and Lottery from three years to four years, eliminate the
12 specific limitation on the hours of sale for licensees who sell alcoholic
13 beverages for off-premises consumption pursuant to 7 V.S.A. § 230, state that
14 special events permits are subject to approval by the local control
15 commissioners in conformance with the definition of “special event permit” in
16 Title 7, eliminate language that requires an applicant for a solicitor’s license to
17 pay the license fee with a certified check, and remove the prospective 2026
18 repeal of the “retail establishment” provisions of the statutes governing special
19 venue serving permits.
