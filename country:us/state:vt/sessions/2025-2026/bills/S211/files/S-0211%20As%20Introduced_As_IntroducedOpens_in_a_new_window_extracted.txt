10 It is hereby enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Vermont:
11 Sec. 1. 23 V.S.A. § 1222 is amended to read:
12 § 1222. INSPECTION OF REGISTERED VEHICLES
13 (a) Except for school buses, which shall be inspected as prescribed in
14 section 1282 of this title, and motor buses as defined in subdivision 4(17) of
15 this title, which shall be inspected twice during the calendar year at six-month
16 intervals, all motor vehicles registered in this State shall undergo a safety and
17 visual emissions inspection once each year every two years and all motor
18 vehicles that are registered in this State and are 16 model years old or less shall
19 undergo an emissions or on board on-board diagnostic (OBD) systems
20 inspection once each year every two years as applicable. Any motor vehicle,

4 Sec. 2. 23 V.S.A. § 1230 is amended to read:
5 § 1230. CHARGE
6 For each inspection certificate issued by the Department of Motor Vehicles,

12 Sec. 3. TRANSITION
13 The month of next inspection for all motor vehicles shall be the month
14 shown on the current inspection certificate affixed to the vehicle. Inspection
15 certificates issued after July 1, 2026, shall show a month that is two years after
16 the month in which the vehicle is inspected.
