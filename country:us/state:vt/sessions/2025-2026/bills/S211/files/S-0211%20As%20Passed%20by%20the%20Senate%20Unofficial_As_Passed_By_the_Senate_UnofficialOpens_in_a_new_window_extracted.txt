2 An act relating to motor vehicle inspections
3 It is hereby enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Vermont:
4 Sec. 1. TWO-YEAR MOTOR VEHICLE INSPECTIONS;
5 IMPLEMENTATION PLAN; REPORT
6 (a) The Secretaries of Transportation and of Natural Resources shall
7 develop a plan to transition to a safety and emissions inspections program that
8 requires pleasure cars to be inspected once every two years beginning in
9 January 2028. The plan shall:
10 (1) establish a timeline for developing and implementing changes to the
11 existing safety and emissions inspection program to ensure that the program
12 can transition to a biennial inspection requirement for pleasure cars beginning
13 in January 2028;
14 (2) identify specific actions that are necessary to ensure that Vermont
15 remains in compliance with the requirements of the Clean Air Act, including
16 any necessary changes to the emissions inspection program and the State
17 Implementation Plan;
18 (3) in addition to any issues or actions identified pursuant to subdivision
19 (2) of this subsection, identify any additional issues related to a change to
20 biennial emissions inspections that could prevent Vermont from remaining in
