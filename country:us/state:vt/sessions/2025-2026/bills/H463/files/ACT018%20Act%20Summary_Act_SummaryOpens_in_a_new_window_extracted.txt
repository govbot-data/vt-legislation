Act No. 18 (H.463). An act relating to technical corrections for the 2025 legislative session
Subjects: Technical corrections; legislature; Vermont Statutes Annotated
This act makes technical corrections to numerous sections of the Vermont Statutes
Annotated, including statutes related to the Executive Branch, labor and employment, and
motor vehicles.
Effective Date: May 13, 2025
