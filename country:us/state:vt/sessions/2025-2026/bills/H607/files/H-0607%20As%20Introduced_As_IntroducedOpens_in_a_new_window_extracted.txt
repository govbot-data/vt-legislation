6 Statement of purpose of bill as introduced: This bill proposes to require that
7 institutional real estate investors wait 90 days prior to the purchase or
8 acquisition of single- and two-family residences and to eliminate tax
9 deductions for depreciation or interest related to these properties for
10 institutional real estate investors.
11 An act relating to institutional real estate investors’ purchase of single- and
12 two-family residences
