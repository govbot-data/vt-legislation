2 An act relating to expanding identification of certain lobbying

4 It is hereby enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Vermont:
5 Sec. 1. 2 V.S.A. § 261 is amended to read:
6 § 261. DEFINITIONS
7 As used in this chapter:
8 * * *
9 (9) “Lobby” or “lobbying” means:
10 (A) to communicate orally or in writing with any legislator or
11 administrative official for the purpose of influencing legislative or
12 administrative action;
13 (B) solicitation of others to influence legislative or administrative
14 action;
15 (C) an attempt to obtain the goodwill of a legislator or administrative
16 official by communications or activities with that legislator or administrative
17 official intended ultimately to influence legislative or administrative action; or
18 (D) activities sponsored by an employer or lobbyist on behalf of or
19 for the benefit of the members of an interest group, if a principal purpose of the
20 activity is to enable such members to communicate orally with one or more
21 legislators or administrative officials for the purpose of influencing legislative
22 or administrative action or to obtain their goodwill.

3 § 264c. IDENTIFICATION IN AND REPORT OF CERTAIN LOBBYING

5 (a) Identification.

11 language that the advertisement was paid for, or paid in part, by the lobbyist,
12 lobbying firm, or lobbyist employer; provided, however:

18 expenditure or expenditures:
19 (A) for any advertisement that is described in subsection (a) of this
20 section and that has a cost totaling $1,000.00 or more; or
