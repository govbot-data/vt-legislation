11 Statement of purpose of bill as introduced: This bill proposes to repeal the
12 four-pronged exception that permits clergy not to report child abuse and
13 neglect when it is received in confidence while acting as a spiritual advisor.
14 Additionally, the bill proposes to repeal a related provision mandating the
15 reporting of information that falls outside of the exception even though the
16 information may have been learned in a manner that falls within the exception.
