11 advanced therapeutic procedures specialty that would allow qualified
12 optometrists to perform certain advanced therapeutic procedures.

15 (2) meets the applicable requirements for education and demonstrated
16 competency set forth in subsections (b) and (c) of this section; and
17 (3) has completed the examination requirements set forth in subsection
18 (d) of this section.
