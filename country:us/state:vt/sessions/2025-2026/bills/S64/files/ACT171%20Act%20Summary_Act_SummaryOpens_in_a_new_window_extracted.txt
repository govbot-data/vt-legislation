Act No. 171 (S.64). An act relating to amendments to the scope of practice for optometrists
Subjects: Professions and occupations; health care professionals; Office of
Professional Regulation; optometrists
This act creates an advanced therapeutic procedures specialty issued by the Board of
Optometry that allows a licensed optometrist who meets specific educational,
examination, and demonstrated competency requirements to perform certain surgeries,
laser procedures, and injections. It requires an optometrist holding the advanced
therapeutic procedures specialty to complete an additional five hours of continuing
education in the advanced procedures during each two-year licensing period in addition
to the 40 hours already required for all optometrists. The act requires that an optometrist
report any adverse event related to an advanced procedure within 30 days following the
event. The act also adds two new members to Board of Optometry, one of whom must be
an optometrist and one of whom must be an ophthalmologist, and requires that at least
one of the four optometrist members of the Board hold the advanced therapeutic
procedures specialty by January 1, 2031.
Effective Date: July 1, 2028
