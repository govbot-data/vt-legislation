7 * * * Critical Access Hospitals; Medicare Outpatient Cost Sharing * * *
8 Sec. 9. CRITICAL ACCESS HOSPITALS; MEDICARE OUTPATIENT
9 COST SHARING
10 (a) The General Assembly and the Green Mountain Care Board have
11 recently become aware of a federal requirement that Medicare beneficiaries
12 must bear financial responsibility for 20 percent of the amount charged for
13 outpatient services delivered by critical access hospitals, not 20 percent of the
14 amount that Medicare pays for the service. While the General Assembly
15 understands that it cannot invalidate this federal requirement, it also recognizes
16 both that this requirement has a significant, unfair, and negative financial
17 impact on Medicare beneficiaries in the State’s most rural communities and
18 that Vermont’s critical access hospitals are some of the State’s most financially
19 vulnerable health care facilities. It is the intent of this section to provide
20 information to Vermont’s seniors and other Medicare beneficiaries about the
21 federal requirement while a working group of interested stakeholders

1 endeavors to develop appropriate and enduring solutions that do not undermine
2 the financial sustainability of our critical access hospitals and that comply with
3 federal law.
4 (b) On or before September 1, 2026, each critical access hospital shall do
5 all of the following:
6 (1) Identify all the outpatient services for which the amount that the
7 hospital charges equals five or more times the Medicare allowed amount for
8 that service.
9 (2) Post prominently on its website and in outpatient departments of the
10 hospital a disclosure about the federal requirement that Medicare beneficiaries
11 must pay 20 percent of the charge for outpatient services at critical access
