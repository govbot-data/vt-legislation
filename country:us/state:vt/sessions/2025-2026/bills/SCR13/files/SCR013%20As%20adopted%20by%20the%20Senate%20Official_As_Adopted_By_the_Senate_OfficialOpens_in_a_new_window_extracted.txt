S.C.R. 13. Senate concurrent resolution honoring Middlebury College Professor Jessica Holmes for her
exemplary public service as a member and interim Chair of the Green Mountain Care Board.
Whereas, the Green Mountain Care Board (GMCB) is charged with the critical role of regulating the
delivery of health care services in Vermont, and the membership of the GMCB must be knowledgeable and
effective, and
Whereas, a GMCB member who superbly exemplifies this standard is Middlebury College Professor Jessica
Holmes, whom former Governor Peter Shumlin appointed in 2014 and Governor Philip B. Scott reappointed in
2020, and
Whereas, Jessica Holmes earned her bachelor’s degree from Colgate University and both her master’s and
doctorate in economics from Yale University, and her academic portfolio at Middlebury College focuses on
health economics and public policy, and
Whereas, as the recipient of Middlebury College’s Gladstone Award for Excellence in Teaching and the
Marjorie Lamberti Faculty Appreciation Award, Jessica Holmes’s pedagogic excellence has repeatedly been
recognized, and
Whereas, she is the coauthor of Mastering Healthcare Regulation: A Comprehensive Case Study Approach,
an authoritative exploration of the topic intended for both specialists and general audiences, and
Whereas, Jessica Holmes has thoughtfully led GMCB transformative regulatory health care initiatives that
promoted greater public access, improved quality, and reduced costs, and
Whereas, she twice stepped up as interim chair, ensuring continuity during periods of leadership transition,
and has mentored staff members, and
Whereas, Jessica Holmes has been instrumental in addressing hospital wait times, sustainability, and resource
planning, and as the GMCB dean (the longest serving member), she has served as an invaluable institutional
resource, and
Whereas, with her GMCB tenure concluding, Jessica Holmes’s presence will be truly missed, now therefore
be it
Resolved by the Senate and House of Representatives:
That the General Assembly honors Middlebury College Professor Jessica Holmes for her exemplary public
service as a member and interim chair of the Green Mountain Care Board, and be it further
Resolved: That the Secretary of State be directed to send a copy of this resolution to Jessica Holmes.
