7 Statement of purpose of bill as introduced: This bill proposes that when the
8 Current Use Advisory Board is recommending values for eligible land for the
9 current tax year, the Board shall recommend that land posted against hunting,
10 fishing, or trapping shall be subject to an annual surcharge of $1.00 per acre.
11 The bill would require the revenue from the surcharge to be deposited into the
12 Fish and Wildlife Fund.
13 An act relating to a fee for posting land enrolled in the Use Value Appraisal
14 Program
15 It is hereby enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Vermont:
