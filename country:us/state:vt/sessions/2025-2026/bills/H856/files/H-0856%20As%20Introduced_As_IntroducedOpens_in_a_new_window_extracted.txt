6 Subject: Health; regulated drugs; Drug Use Health and Safety Advisory Board
7 Statement of purpose of bill as introduced: This bill proposes to establish the
8 Drug Use Health and Safety Advisory Board for the purpose of determining
9 the benchmark personal use supply for each regulated drug; decrease low-level
10 drug possession violations to a penalty of six months’ imprisonment or a
11 $500.00 fine, or both; and decrease low-level felony drug possession violations
12 to misdemeanor offenses.
13 An act relating to possessing regulated drugs and establishing the Drug Use
14 Health and Safety Advisory Board
