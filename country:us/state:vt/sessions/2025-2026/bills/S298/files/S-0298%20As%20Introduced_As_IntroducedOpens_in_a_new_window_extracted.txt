10 Statement of purpose of bill as introduced: This bill will explicitly prohibit
11 discrimination based on race or color or membership in a language minority
12 group. It will prohibit local racial gerrymandering. This bill proposes to create
13 language assistance in voting and elections and voting assistance methods for
14 individuals above 65 years of age and individuals with disabilities. It prohibits
15 municipal electoral changes within 60 days preceding a general election and
16 creates a pre-clearance system where municipalities must first seek
17 authorization from the Attorney General’s office for changes to electoral
18 districts or systems. This bill prohibits at-large districts where these would
19 impair the ability of members of a protected class to elect candidates of its
20 choice or its ability to influence the outcome of an election. It creates the
21 Voter Education and Outreach Fund. This bill criminalizes the intimidation of
