3 Subchapter 6. Voter Protections
4 § 2045. VOTE DENIAL OR DILUTION
5 (a) No voting qualification or prerequisite to voting or standard, practice, or
6 procedure shall be imposed or applied by the State or any municipality in a
7 manner that results in a denial or abridgement of the right of any citizen of the
8 United States to vote based on race or color, membership in a language
9 minority group, or having a disability as defined in 9 V.S.A. § 4501.
10 (b) A violation of subsection (a) of this section is established if, on the
11 basis of the totality of circumstances, it is shown that the political processes
12 leading to nomination or election in the State or a municipality are not equally
13 open to participation by members of a protected class in that its members have
14 less opportunity than other members of the electorate to participate in the
15 political processes or to elect representatives of their choice. The extent to
16 which members of a protected class have been elected to office in the State or
17 municipality is one circumstance that may be considered.
18 (c) Nothing in this section shall be construed to establish a right to have
19 members of a protected class elected in numbers equal to their proportion in
20 the population.
