6 § 2045. VOTE DENIAL OR DILUTION
7 (a) No voting qualification or prerequisite to voting or standard, practice, or
8 procedure shall be imposed or applied by the State or any municipality in a
9 manner that results in a denial or abridgement of the right of any citizen of the
10 United States to vote based on race or color, membership in a language
11 minority group, or having a disability as defined in 9 V.S.A. § 4501.
12 (b) A violation of subsection (a) of this section is established if, on the
13 basis of the totality of circumstances, it is shown that the political processes
14 leading to nomination or election in the State or a municipality are not equally
15 open to participation by members of a protected class in that its members have
16 less opportunity than other members of the electorate to participate in the
17 political processes or to elect representatives of their choice. The extent to
18 which members of a protected class have been elected to office in the State or
19 municipality is one circumstance that may be considered.
