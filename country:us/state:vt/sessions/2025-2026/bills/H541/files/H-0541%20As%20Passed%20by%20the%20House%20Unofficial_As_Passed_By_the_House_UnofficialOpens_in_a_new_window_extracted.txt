1 H.541
2 An act relating to interference with voters and election officials
3 It is hereby enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Vermont:
4 Sec. 1. 17 V.S.A. § 1972 is amended to read:
5 § 1972. SHOWING BALLOT; INTERFERENCE WITH VOTER
6 (a) A voter who, except in cases of assistance as provided in this title,
7 allows his or her the voter’s ballot to be seen by another person with an
8 apparent intention of letting it be known how he or she the voter is about to
9 vote or makes a false statement to the presiding officer at an election as to his
10 or her the voter’s inability to mark his or her the voter’s ballot or places a
11 distinguishing mark on his or her the voter’s ballot or a person who interferes
12 with a voter when inside the guard rail or who, within the building in which the
13 voting is proceeding, endeavors to induce a voter to vote for a particular
14 candidate, shall be fined $1,000.00.
15 (b) It shall be the duty of the election officers to see that the offender is
16 duly prosecuted for a violation of this section.
17 Sec. 2. 17 V.S.A. § 1975 is added to read:
18 § 1975. INTERFERENCE WITH VOTERS AND ELECTION OFFICIALS
19 (a) No person shall intentionally or recklessly intimidate, threaten, coerce,
20 or attempt to intimidate, threaten, or coerce:
21 (1) any other person for the purpose of:
