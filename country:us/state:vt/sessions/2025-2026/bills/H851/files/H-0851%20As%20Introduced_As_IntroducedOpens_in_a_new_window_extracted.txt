6 Subject: Motor vehicles; gas stations; electric vehicle supply equipment;
7 public transit; incentives; ride-sharing
8 Statement of purpose of bill as introduced: This bill proposes to create a pilot
9 program within the Agency of Transportation to provide incentives for existing
10 gas stations to install electric vehicle supply equipment together with the
11 addition or expansion at the stations of facilities and services to promote social
12 drivers of health for individuals traveling in electric vehicles. New or
13 expanded facilities that may be supported by the program include facilities that
14 serve or sell food, seating areas, spaces for individuals to sleep or rest while
15 their vehicle is charging, shower facilities, and restrooms.
16 This bill also proposes to create a pilot program within the Agency of
17 Transportation to stabilize and expand the State public transit systems. The
18 program would support the creation of incentives for individuals to use
19 existing transit lines, including opportunities to win prizes or earn discounts at
20 local businesses. The program would also support public transit agencies’
21 examination of the use of autonomous vehicles to supplement or expand
