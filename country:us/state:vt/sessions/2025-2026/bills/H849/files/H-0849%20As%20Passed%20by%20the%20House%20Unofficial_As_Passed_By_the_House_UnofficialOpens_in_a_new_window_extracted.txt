1 H.849
2 An act relating to a civil action for damages for deprivation of federal
3 constitutional rights by any government official
4 It is hereby enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Vermont:
5 Sec. 1. 12 V.S.A. chapter 205 is added to read:
6 CHAPTER 205. DEPRIVATION OF CONSTITUTIONAL RIGHTS
7 § 5797. CIVIL ACTION
8 (a) Every person who, under color of any statute, ordinance, regulation,
9 custom, or usage, subjects or causes to be subjected any citizen of the State of
10 Vermont or other person within the jurisdiction thereof to the deprivation of
11 any rights, privileges, or immunities secured by the Constitution of the United
12 States shall be liable to the party injured in an action at law, suit in equity, or
13 other proper proceeding for redress, except that in any action brought against a
14 judicial officer for an act or omission taken in the officer’s judicial capacity,
15 injunctive relief shall not be granted unless a declaratory decree was violated
16 or declaratory relief was unavailable.
17 (b) For purposes of this section, “color of any statute, ordinance,
18 regulation, custom, or usage” includes color of any statute, ordinance,
19 regulation, custom, or usage of the United States and of any U.S. state or
20 territory or the District of Columbia.

1 (c) Any defense available to a defendant under 42 U.S.C. § 1983 is
2 likewise and to the same extent available to any defendant in an action brought
3 under subsection (a) of this section. This subsection does not alter, amend,

8 as amended, as interpreted by the courts of the United States.
