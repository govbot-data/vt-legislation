4 Sec. 1. LEGISLATIVE INTENT
5 It is the intent of the General Assembly to invest in primary through
6 streamlined primary care payments that build on the Blueprint for Health and
7 will promote the public good by increasing access to primary care in order to
8 improve the health of Vermonters and reduce health care system costs.

7 § 710. PRIMARY CARE SPENDING TARGETS
8 The Agency of Human Services shall establish a target for the amount of
9 per-person per-month spending on Vermont residents that should be for
10 primary care services and shall develop a transitional schedule that increases
11 that target over time. Targets may be adjusted to reflect payer-specific
12 differences, such as age and health status. The increased spending shall be
13 directed to the per-person per-month payments established in section 706(c) of
14 this chapter.
