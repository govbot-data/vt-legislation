5 Subject: Corrections; Parole Board
6 Statement of purpose of bill as introduced: This bill proposes to structure the
7 Parole Board with one full-time chair and four members; provide the Parole
8 Board with a staff attorney and additional administrative assistant; require
9 training for the Parole Board; require the Parole Board to submit a budget
10 directly to the Governor; require that an offender who is sentenced to a
11 minimum term have no pending criminal charges to be eligible for parole;
12 clarify victims’ rights in a parole hearing; permit a parolee to relinquish parole
13 status; and permit a parolee to benefit from earned time.
14 An act relating to restructuring the Parole Board
15 It is hereby enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Vermont:
