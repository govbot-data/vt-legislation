Act No. 91 (H.927). An act relating to technical corrections for the 2026 legislative session
Subjects: Government operations; Vermont Statutes Annotated; 2026 technical
corrections
This act contains technical corrections proposed primarily by the Office of Legislative
Counsel. They include updates and corrections related to terminology and nomenclature;
gender neutrality; statutory cross-references; grammar, punctuation, and style; drafting
conventions and formatting; Legislative Branch and Executive Branch entities; and
statutory errors and omissions. The amendments affect 19 of the 33 titles of the Vermont
Statutes Annotated. The act also includes one substantive amendment, which concerns
the Joint Carbon Emissions Reduction Committee. Specifically, the amendment requires
political diversity with respect to membership on this legislative oversight committee.
Effective Date: April 30, 2026
