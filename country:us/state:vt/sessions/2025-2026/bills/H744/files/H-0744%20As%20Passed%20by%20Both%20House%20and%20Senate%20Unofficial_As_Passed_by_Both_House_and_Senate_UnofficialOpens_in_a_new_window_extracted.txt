1 H.744
2 An act relating to procedures for release after arrest
3 It is hereby enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Vermont:
4 Sec. 1. Rule 3 of the Vermont Rules of Criminal Procedure is amended to
5 read:
6 Rule 3. Arrest Without Warrant; Citation to Appear
7 * * *
8 (k) Temporary Release. A law enforcement officer arresting a person shall
9 contact a judicial officer for determination of temporary release pursuant to
10 Rule 5(b) of these rules without unnecessary delay. The law enforcement
11 officer shall provide the judicial officer with an affidavit or sworn statement as
12 required by Rule 4(a) of these rules, and information upon which the
13 determination as to temporary release may be made. The affidavit or sworn
14 statement must indicate the crimes to be charged by the arresting officer. The
15 procedures and standards established by the Presiding Judge of each unit
16 pursuant to Rule 5(b) may require that the affidavit or sworn statement include
17 the charge or charges that the prosecuting attorney intends to file and may
18 require that the affidavit also include any conditions of release, including bail
19 or an order to hold without bail, that the prosecuting attorney is requesting.
20 Sec. 2. EFFECTIVE DATE
21 This act shall take effect on July 2, 2026.
