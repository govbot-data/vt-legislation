4 Sec. 1. 18 V.S.A. § 7110 is amended to read:
5 § 7110. CERTIFICATION OF MENTAL ILLNESS
6 A certification of mental illness by a licensed physician required by section

10 psychiatrists, or residents in psychiatry are not available to complete admission
11 certifications to the Vermont State Hospital or its successor in interest, the
