5 § 4017. PERSONS PROHIBITED FROM POSSESSING FIREARMS;
6 CONVICTION OF VIOLENT CRIME
7 (a) A person shall not possess a firearm if the person has been convicted of

9 (b) A person who violates this section shall:
10 (1) for a first offense, be imprisoned not more than two years or fined

21 (iii) any firearm muffler or firearm silencer.
