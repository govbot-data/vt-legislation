5 Subject: Criminal procedures; firearms
6 Statement of purpose of bill as introduced: This bill proposes to enact the
7 following provisions related to firearms: (1) provide that stealing a firearm of
8 any value constitutes grand larceny; (2) increase the penalties for second or
9 subsequent offenses of firearms possession by prohibited persons; (3) prohibit
10 the possession of firearms by persons who have been found by a court to pose
11 a danger of harm to themselves or others as the result of a mental illness; (4)
12 prohibit the possession of machine guns; and (5) prohibit the possession of
13 firearms on premises licensed to serve alcohol.
14 An act relating to criminal procedures involving firearms
