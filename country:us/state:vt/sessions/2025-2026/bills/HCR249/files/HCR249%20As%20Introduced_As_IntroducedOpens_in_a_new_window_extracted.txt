State of Vermont
House of Representatives
Montpelier, Vermont
Concurrent House Resolution
H.C.R. 249
Offered by Representative Southworth of Walden
House concurrent resolution congratulating the 2026 Hazen Union Lady Cats Division III
championship girls’ basketball team
Whereas, the excitement of playoff competition was maximized at the 2026 Division III girls’
basketball championship game at the Barre Auditorium, in which top-seeded Hazen Union faced
second-ranked Windsor for the third consecutive year, the Lady Cats having won in 2024 and the
Yellow Jackets in 2025, and
Whereas, the Lady Cats’ switch this season to the more challenging Capital Division improved the
team’s performance, and
Whereas, two more evenly matched contenders were unimaginable, as Hazen exited the court at half-
time with a narrow 23–21 lead, but over the balance of the game, including the overtime period required
to determine a winner, the lead changed an incredible 13 times, and the Lady Cats evened the score at
48–all with under five seconds remaining in regulation play, and
Whereas, the final outcome was determined in an equally dramatic fashion, when a Hazen player
succeeded on two foul shots with only 2.4 seconds remaining in overtime, resulting in a hard-won and
undeniably satisfying 55–53 win, and
Whereas, the thrilled and relieved Lady Cats were Isabelle Gouin, Ari Nichols, Taylor Thompson,
Marina Smith, Eloise Foster, Autumn Dailey, Mya Lumsden, Ella Renaud, Sadie Gann, Julia des
Groseilliers, Kelsie Rivard, Kassidy Gann, and Kennidee Gouin, and
Whereas, Head Coach Randy Lumsden and assistant coaches Alison Blaney and Sue Rivard expertly
guided the Lady Cats, and team managers Allie Foster, Ivy Demers, and Estella Foster contributed to the
team’s great 2025–2026 season, now therefore be it
Resolved by the Senate and House of Representatives:
That the General Assembly congratulates the 2026 Hazen Union Lady Cats Division III
championship girls’ basketball team, and be it further
Resolved: That the Secretary of State be directed to send a copy of this resolution to Hazen Union
High School.
