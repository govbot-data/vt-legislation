6 Sec. 2. 33 V.S.A. § 1904a is added to read:
7 § 1904a. SCHOOL-BASED MEDICAID REIMBURSEMENT FUND
8 (a) The School-Based Medicaid Reimbursement Fund is established as a
9 special fund to receive federal reimbursements for medically related services
10 delivered pursuant to the Medicaid school-based services program described in
11 section 1904 of this chapter. Monies received by the State pursuant to this
12 section shall be transferred to the School-Based Medicaid Reimbursement
13 Fund, which shall be administered by the Agency of Human Services, and
14 Fund receipts shall be allocated in accordance with this section.
15 (b) The Department of Vermont Health Access shall pay 55 percent of the
16 federal reimbursement monies generated by the medically related services
17 delivered in each supervisory union pursuant to the Medicaid school-based
18 services program to the supervisory union based on the Medicaid cost reports,
19 service claims, and Medicaid administrative costs submitted by the supervisory
20 union.
