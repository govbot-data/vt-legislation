Act No. 129 (H.527). An act relating to extending the sunset of 30 V.S.A. § 248a
Subjects: Land use; telecommunications; public service; siting
This act extends the sunset on 30 V.S.A. § 248a to July 1, 2029. It also makes the pre-
application public hearing mandatory for the municipality, the applicant, and the
Department of Public Service.
Effective Date: June 15, 2026
