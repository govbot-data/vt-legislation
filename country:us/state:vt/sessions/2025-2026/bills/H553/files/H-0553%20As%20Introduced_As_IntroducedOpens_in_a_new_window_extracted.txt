5 Subject: Health; hospitals; hospital budgets; Green Mountain Care Board;

7 Statement of purpose of bill as introduced: This bill proposes to require
8 hospitals to hold public hearings on their proposed budgets and to submit a
9 summary of the public comments received on the proposed budget to the
10 Green Mountain Care Board as part of the hospital budget review process. The
11 bill would also allow the Green Mountain Care Board to order hospitals to
12 align or coordinate their administrative functions in order to maximize
13 efficiencies and contain administrative costs.
14 An act relating to hospital public hearings and coordination of hospital
15 administrative functions
16 It is hereby enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Vermont:
17 Sec. 1. 18 V.S.A. § 9452 is added to read:
18 § 9452. HOSPITAL BUDGET DEVELOPMENT; PUBLIC HEARING

1 (a) At least 30 days prior to submitting a proposed budget to the Green
2 Mountain Care Board for review under this subchapter, each hospital shall
3 hold a public hearing in one or more of the communities in its service area to:
4 (1) explain the proposed hospital budget under development for the
5 upcoming fiscal year, including explaining any services that the hospital
6 intends to add, reduce, or eliminate during the year; and
7 (2) provide meaningful opportunities for the public to provide input on
8 the proposal budget and changes in hospital services for the hospital to
9 consider as it completes its budget proposal.
10 (b) The hospital shall submit a summary of the public comments received
11 during the hearing or hearings, as well as copies of any written comments
12 submitted to the hospital regarding its proposed budget, to the Green Mountain
13 Care Board as part of the information required pursuant to section 9454 of this
14 chapter.
