Act No. 94 (H.410). An act relating to the calculation of recidivism and other related
criminology measures
Subjects: Crimes; criminology measures; recidivism
This act establishes a new chapter within Title 13 of the Vermont Statutes Annotated
regarding criminology measures, establishing a new statutory definition of and
calculation method for recidivism, and requiring the Vermont Statistical Analysis Center
to issue four annual reports on an on-going basis. Reports include data on bail rates,
recidivism, arrest and clearance rates, and aggregated information on the 20 crimes with
the most convictions. The act appropriates $25,000.00 in fiscal year 2027 from the
General Fund to the Department of Public Safety for contracted support related to the
reports.
Effective Date: July 1, 2026
