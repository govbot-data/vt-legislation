No. R-332. House concurrent resolution congratulating Mount Mansfield Union High
School Cougar Bea Molson on winning her second girls’ individual State tennis
championship.
(H.C.R.274)
Offered by Representatives Graning of Jericho, Brown of Richmond, Squirrell of
Underhill, Stevens of Waterbury, and Wood of Waterbury
Offered by Senators Chittenden, Lyons and Ram Hinsdale
Whereas, in 2022, ninth grader Bea Molson stunned her older competitors when she
clinched the girls’ individual tennis title, and
Whereas, her State tournament on-court performances in 2023 and 2024, although
praiseworthy, unfortunately did not garner the ultimate prize, and
Whereas, during her senior year as a Mount Mansfield High School Cougar, Bea
Molson polished her tennis skills and carefully focused on her objective each time she
met an opponent on the tennis court, an effort resulting in her winning all her regular
season matches and the top playoff seed, and
Whereas, the concentration she displayed on the court in her prior 2025 matches was
evident at the State tournament as she proceeded through all the preliminary rounds to
secure a final match berth, where she defeated the third-seeded player 6–4, 6–3 to capture
a second individual State crown, now therefore be it
Resolved by the Senate and House of Representatives
That the General Assembly congratulates Mount Mansfield Union High School
Cougar Bea Molson on winning her second girls’ individual State tennis championship,
and be it further
Resolved: That the Secretary of State be directed to send a copy of this resolution to
Bea Molson.
