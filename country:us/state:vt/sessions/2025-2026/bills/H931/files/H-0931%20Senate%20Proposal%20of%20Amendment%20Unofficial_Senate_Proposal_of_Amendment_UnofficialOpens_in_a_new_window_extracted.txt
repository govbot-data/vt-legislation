12 § 1501. PURPOSE AND POLICY—ARTICLE I
13 (a) It is the purpose of this compact to:
14 (1) establish and maintain close cooperation and understanding among
15 executive, legislative, professional educational, and lay leadership on a
16 nationwide basis at the state and local levels;
17 (2) provide a forum for the discussion, development, crystallization, and
18 recommendation of public policy alternatives in the field of education;
19 (3) provide a clearinghouse of information on matters relating to
20 education problems and how they are being met in different places throughout
21 the nation, so that the executive and legislative branches of state government

2 of the entire country, and so that both lay and professional groups in the field
3 of education may have additional avenues for the sharing of experience and the
4 interchange of ideas in the formation of public policy in education;
5 (4) facilitate the improvement of state and local education systems so
6 that all of them will be able to meet adequate and desirable goals in a society

14 quality and quantity of education furnished in each of the other states, as well
15 as in the excellence of its own education systems and institutions, because of
16 the highly mobile character of individuals within the nation, and because the
17 products and services contributing to the health, welfare, and economic
18 advancement of each state are supplied in significant part by persons educated
19 in other states.

17 affected as to all severable matters.
