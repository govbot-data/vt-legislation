13 § 4662. CYBERSECURITY ADVISORY COUNCIL
14 (a) Creation. There is created the Cybersecurity Advisory Council to
15 advise on the State’s cybersecurity infrastructure, best practices,
16 communications protocols, standards, training, and safeguards.
17 (b) Membership. The Council shall be composed of the following
18 members:
19 (1) the Chief Information Officer, who shall serve as the Chair or
20 appoint a designee from the Council to serve as the Chair;
