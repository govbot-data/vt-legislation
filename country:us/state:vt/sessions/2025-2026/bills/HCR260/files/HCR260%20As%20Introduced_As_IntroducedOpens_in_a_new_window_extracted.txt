State of Vermont
House of Representatives
Montpelier, Vermont
Concurrent House Resolution
H.C.R. 260
Offered by Representatives Cole of Hartford, Christie of Hartford, and Hoyt of Hartford
Offered by Senators White, Clarkson, and Major
House concurrent resolution congratulating Hartford Hurricanes Captain Charlotte Jasmin on being
named the 2026 Vermont girls’ basketball Gatorade Player of the Year
Whereas, to score 1,000 points during a four-year tenure on a high school girls’ basketball team is a
laudable achievement, but to double that number is truly spectacular and an occasion to celebrate, and
Whereas, only a small number of high school girls’ basketball players earn this special distinction,
and in 2026, Hartford Hurricanes Captain Charlotte Jasmin became only the fifth Vermonter to reach
this pinnacle, and her four-year scoring total of 2,025 points also places her at number five among all
Vermont high school female basketball players, and
Whereas, Charlotte Jasmin is only the fourth 2,000-point scorer to play for at least one championship
team, as the Hurricanes defeated the Lamoille Lancers for the 2026 Division II title, 37–20, and
Whereas, for National Honor Society member Charlotte Jasmin, dominance on the basketball court
has hardly distracted her classroom focus, and she has set aside time to volunteer with the Hope
Happens Here Foundation, an organization dedicated to destigmatizing mental health issues for student-
athletes, and to serve on the Hartford High School Athletic Leadership Council, and
Whereas, these multiple achievements persuaded the panel of judges to honor Charlotte Jasmin as the
2026 Vermont girls’ basketball Gatorade Player of the Year, now therefore be it
Resolved by the Senate and House of Representatives:
That the General Assembly congratulates Hartford Hurricanes Captain Charlotte Jasmin on being
named the 2026 Vermont girls’ basketball Gatorade Player of the Year, and be it further
Resolved: That the Secretary of State be directed to send a copy of this resolution to Charlotte
Jasmin.
