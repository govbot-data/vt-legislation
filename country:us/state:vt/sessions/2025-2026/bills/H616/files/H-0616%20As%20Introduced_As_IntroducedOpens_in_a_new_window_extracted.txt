5 Subject: Education; flexible pathways; virtual learning
6 Statement of purpose of bill as introduced: This bill proposes to require a
7 school board to pay tuition to the virtual learning provider that the Agency of
8 Education maintains access to and oversight over pursuant to 16 V.S.A. § 948
9 upon request of the student’s parent or legal guardian, regardless of whether
10 virtual learning is part of the student’s personalized learning plan.
11 An act relating to the right to enroll full time in virtual learning
12 It is hereby enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Vermont:
