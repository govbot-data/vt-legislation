Act No. 123 (S.179). An act relating to the Uniform Disclaimer of Property Interests Act
Subjects: Decedents’ estates; Uniform Disclaimer of Property Interests Act
This act enacts the revised Uniform Disclaimer of Property Interests Act. Existing
Vermont law contains the prior version of this uniform act, and this act passes the revised
version, which establishes uniform legal procedures for a formal refusal to accept a
transfer of property.
Effective Date: June 8, 2026
