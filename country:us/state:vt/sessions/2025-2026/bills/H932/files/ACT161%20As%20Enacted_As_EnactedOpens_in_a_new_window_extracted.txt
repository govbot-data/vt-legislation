* * *
(ee) No permit or permit amendment is required for logging and forestry
below the elevation of 2,500 feet that will not conflict with or violate any
condition of a permit issued pursuant to this chapter. Permits shall include a
statement that logging and forestry activities consistent with this subsection
and below the elevation of 2,500 feet are exempt from amendment jurisdiction.
