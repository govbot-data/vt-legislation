SENATE PROPOSAL OF AMENDMENT H.932
2026 Page 1 of 1
1 H.932
2 An act relating to the regulation of forestry under Act 250
3 The Senate proposes to the House to amend the bill in Sec. 1, 10 V.S.A.
4 § 6001, in subdivision (3), by striking out subdivision (F) in its entirety and
5 inserting in lieu thereof a new subdivision (F) to read as follows:
6 (F) When development is proposed to occur on a parcel or tract of land that
7 is devoted to logging and forestry, only those portions of the parcel or the tract
8 that support the development shall be subject to regulation under this chapter.
9 Permits issued under this chapter shall not impose conditions on, apply to, or
10 restrict or conflict with the Acceptable Management Practices for Maintaining
11 Water Quality on Logging Jobs in Vermont on other portions of the parcel or
12 tract of land that do not support the development.
