House Proposal of Amendment
S. 181
An act relating to eliminating the requirement for a presentence
investigation for imposition of a deferred sentence
The House proposes to the Senate to amend the bill in Sec. 1, 13 V.S.A.
§ 7041, by inserting a new subdivision to be subdivision (a)(3) to read as
follows:
(3) If the offense is not a listed crime as provided in subdivision 5301(7)
of this title, the court may, at its discretion, order a presentence investigation.
