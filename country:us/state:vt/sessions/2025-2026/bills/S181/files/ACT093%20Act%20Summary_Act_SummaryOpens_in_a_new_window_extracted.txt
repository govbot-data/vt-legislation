Act No. 93 (S.181). An act relating to eliminating the requirement for a presentence
investigation for imposition of a deferred sentence
Subjects: Crimes and criminal procedure; sentencing; deferred sentence
This act removes the requirement that a presentence investigation be completed and
reviewed by the court prior to imposition of a deferred sentence for an offense that is not
a listed crime as defined in 13 V.S.A. § 5301. If the court determines that a presentence
investigation is necessary for the court to have the information needed to approve a
deferred sentence agreement between the parties for a nonlisted crime, the court may
request one pursuant to 13 V.S.A. § 7041(a)(3). If there is no agreement between the
parties, the court may request a presentence investigation in considering a deferred
sentence for a nonlisted crime according to Rule 32 of the Vermont Rules of Criminal
Procedure. If the parties agree to a deferred sentence for a listed crime, a presentence
investigation will continue to be required.
Effective Date: July 1, 2026
