4 It is hereby enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Vermont:
5 Sec. 1. 13 V.S.A. § 7041 is amended to read:
6 § 7041. DEFERRED SENTENCE
7 (a)(1) Upon an adjudication of guilt and after the filing of a presentence
8 investigation report, the court may defer sentencing and place the respondent
9 on probation upon such terms and conditions as it may require if a written
10 agreement concerning the deferring of sentence is entered into between the
11 State’s Attorney and the respondent and filed with the clerk of the court.
12 (2) If the offense is a listed crime as provided in subdivision 5301(7) of
13 this title, a presentence investigation shall be conducted unless the State’s
14 Attorney and the respondent agree to waive the presentence investigation.
15 (3) If the offense is not a listed crime as provided in subdivision 5301(7)
16 of this title, the court may, at its discretion, order a presentence investigation.
17 (b) Notwithstanding subsection (a) of this section, the court may defer
18 sentencing and place the respondent on probation without a written agreement
19 between the State’s Attorney and the respondent if the following conditions are
20 met:
