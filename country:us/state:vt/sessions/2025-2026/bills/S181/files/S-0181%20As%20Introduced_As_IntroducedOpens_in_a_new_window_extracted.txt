9 An act relating to eliminating the requirement for a presentence

11 It is hereby enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Vermont:

18 State’s Attorney and the respondent and filed with the clerk of the court.
19 (b) Notwithstanding subsection (a) of this section, the court may defer
20 sentencing and place the respondent on probation without a written agreement
