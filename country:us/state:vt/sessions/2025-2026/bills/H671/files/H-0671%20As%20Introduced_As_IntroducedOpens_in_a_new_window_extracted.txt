7 Statement of purpose of bill as introduced: This bill proposes to require
8 documented proof of U.S. citizenship to register to vote. It also requires that
9 the Secretary of State enter into a memorandum of agreement with the U.S.
10 Department of Homeland Security to access and utilize the Systematic Alien
11 Verification for Entitlements (SAVE) Program for the purposes of verifying
12 citizenship status during voter registration and for ongoing voter roll
13 maintenance.

1 Sec. 5. SYSTEMATIC ALIEN VERIFICATION FOR ENTITLEMENTS
2 (SAVE) PROGRAM; MEMORANDUM OF AGREEMENT
3 On or before January 1, 2027, the Secretary of State shall enter into a
4 memorandum of agreement with the U.S. Department of Homeland Security to
5 access and utilize the Systematic Alien Verification for Entitlements (SAVE)
6 Program for the purposes of verifying citizenship status during voter
7 registration and for ongoing voter roll maintenance. The Secretary shall
8 implement bulk uploads and other available enhancements and shall report to
9 the General Assembly on the status of this agreement and implementation
10 within six months following the effective date of this act.
11 Sec. 6. APPROPRIATION
12 The sum of $300,000.00 is appropriated from the General Fund to the
13 Secretary of State in fiscal year 2027 for the purpose of integrating the
14 Systematic Alien Verification for Entitlements (SAVE) Program into State and
15 local systems and training town clerks.
16 Sec. 7. EFFECTIVE DATE
17 This act shall take effect on passage.
