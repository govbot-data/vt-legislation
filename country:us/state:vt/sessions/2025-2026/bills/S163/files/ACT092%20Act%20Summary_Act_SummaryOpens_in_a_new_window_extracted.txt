Act No. 92 (S.163). An act relating to the role of advanced practice providers in hospital
care
Subjects: Health; hospitals; advanced practice registered nurses; physician
assistants
This act amends hospital licensure requirements and the Bill of Rights for Hospital
Patients to include advanced practice registered nurses and physician assistants, in
addition to physicians, as the types of health care professionals who may be serving as
the attending clinician responsible for coordinating a patient’s care in the hospital and for
signing patient records. The act also specifies that a clinician’s failure to comply with the
Bill of Rights for Hospital Patients may serve as the basis for disciplinary action by the
Office of Professional Regulation, in addition to the Board of Medical Practice,
depending on the type of license the clinician holds.
Effective Date: May 6, 2026
