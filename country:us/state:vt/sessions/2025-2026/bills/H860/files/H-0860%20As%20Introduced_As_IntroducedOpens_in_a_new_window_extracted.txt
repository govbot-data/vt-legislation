1 (1) Justice and recovery hub. The Working Group shall engage with the
2 City of Burlington and stakeholders local to Burlington, including housing
3 providers, private landowners, businesses, workers, neighbors, and
4 community-based service providers, to develop a proposal for a justice and
5 recovery hub to be constructed on State land adjacent to the Costello
6 Courthouse and the Department of Labor in Burlington as part of a coordinated
7 public-private partnership for the redevelopment of Burlington’s downtown
8 core. The proposal shall include as components of the justice and recovery
9 hub:
10 (A) a 200-bed residential facility for justice-involved individuals who
11 are detained or in reentry from incarceration;
12 (B) recovery housing and supportive housing for youth in transition,
13 prioritizing youth with history of involvement with the Department for
14 Children and Families; and
15 (C) nonresidential components, giving specific consideration to a
16 new firehouse or other public safety infrastructure, storefronts for small
17 businesses and service providers, and a public observation tower.
18 (2) Justice and recovery spokes. The Working Group shall engage with
19 municipalities and stakeholders across Addison, Chittenden, Franklin, and
20 Washington Counties, including housing providers, private landowners,
21 businesses, workers, neighbors, and community-based service providers, to
