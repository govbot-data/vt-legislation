10 Statement of purpose of bill as introduced: This bill proposes to require all
11 regional and municipal emergency management plans to utilize an access and
12 functional needs framework and ensure that the plans apply the
13 Communication, Medical needs, Independence, Supervision, and
14 Transportation (C-MIST) process to identify needs, provide accommodations,
15 and operationalize support. This bill also proposes to require regional and
16 local emergency management organizations to include both members with
17 lived disability experience and those who represent organizations serving
18 vulnerable populations. This bill also would require municipalities to partner
19 with disability-led organizations throughout all phases of emergency
20 management planning. This bill also proposes to require the Division of
21 Emergency Management to update local emergency plan templates and
