9 An act relating to flexible working arrangements
10 It is hereby enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Vermont:
11 Sec. 1. 21 V.S.A. § 309 is amended to read:
12 § 309. FLEXIBLE WORKING ARRANGEMENTS
13 (a)(1) An employee may request a flexible working arrangement that meets
14 the needs of the employer and employee. The employer shall consider a
15 request using the procedures in subsections (b) and (c) of this section at least
16 twice per calendar year.
17 (2) As used in this section, “flexible working arrangement” means
18 intermediate or long-term changes in the employee’s regular working
19 arrangements, including changes in the number of days or hours worked,
20 changes in the time the employee arrives at or departs from work, work from
