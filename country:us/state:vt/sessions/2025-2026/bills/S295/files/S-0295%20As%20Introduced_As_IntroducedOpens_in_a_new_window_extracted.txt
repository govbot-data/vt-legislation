8 Sec. 2. 3 V.S.A. § 461 is amended to read:
9 § 461. ACCIDENTAL AND OCCUPATIONALLY RELATED
10 DISABILITY RETIREMENT
11 (a) Upon the application of a member or of his or her the member’s
12 department head not later than 90 days, or longer for cause shown, after the
13 date the member may have separated from service, any member may be retired
14 by the Retirement Board on an accidental disability retirement allowance, not
15 less than 30 nor more than 90 days after filing such application, provided that
16 the Retirement Board shall find on the basis of such evidence as may come
17 before it, including a report by the Medical Board after a medical examination
18 of such member, that the member is mentally or physically incapacitated for
19 the further performance of duty as the natural and proximate result of an
20 accident occurring at a definite time and place during the course of his or her
21 the member’s performance of duty as an employee, that such accident was not

14 § 496. TRANSFER FROM GROUP F TO GROUP G; ELECTION
