7 Statement of purpose of bill as introduced: This bill proposes to authorize the
8 Agency of Natural Resources (ANR) to delegate to municipalities the
9 permitting and technical review of potable water supply and wastewater
10 system connections. The bill also would require ANR to adopt a general
11 permit for municipal potable water supply and wastewater system connections
12 that are reviewed by delegated municipalities.
13 An act relating to potable water supply and wastewater system connections
