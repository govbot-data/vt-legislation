6 Subject: Professions and occupations; accountants
7 Statement of purpose of bill as introduced: This bill proposes to add a pathway
8 for candidates seeking licensure as a certified public accountant via an
9 additional combination of education and experience to be eligible to take the
10 licensing exam. This bill also proposes to adopt a mobility practice privilege
11 for qualified certified public accountant licensees of other states to practice
12 accountancy in Vermont without obtaining a license in this State.
