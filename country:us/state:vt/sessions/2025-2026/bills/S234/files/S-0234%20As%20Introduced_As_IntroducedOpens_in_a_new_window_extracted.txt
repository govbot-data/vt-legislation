3 Sec. 2. 32 V.S.A. § 112 is added to read:
4 § 112. VERMONT GREEN FC SPECIAL FUND
5 (a) There is established the Vermont Green FC Special Fund, which shall
6 be managed in accordance with chapter 7, subchapter 5 of this title. The
7 purpose of the Fund is to provide support for environmental justice and the
8 outdoor recreation economy.
9 (b) The Fund shall consist of:
10 (1) receipts from the sale of Vermont Green FC motor vehicle
11 registration plates pursuant to 23 V.S.A. § 304d;
12 (2) any gifts, grants, or contributions made to the Fund; and
13 (3) any amounts appropriated by the General Assembly.
14 (c)(1) All interest earned on Fund balances shall be credited to the Fund.
15 (2) The Treasurer and the board created pursuant to subsection (d) of
16 this section may seek and accept gifts, donations, and grants from any source,
17 public or private, to be dedicated for deposit into the Fund.
18 (3) Monies in the Fund shall be used solely to provide support for
19 environmental justice and the outdoor recreation economy and for any
20 administrative costs incurred in administering the Fund.

1 (d)(1) The Fund shall be overseen by a board composed of:
2 (A) the Treasurer or designee;
