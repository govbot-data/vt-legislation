6 Subject: Commerce and trade; residential rental agreements; landlord tenant;

8 Statement of purpose of bill as introduced: This bill proposes to require that a
9 landlord clearly and accurately advertise the utilities and estimated costs that
10 are the responsibility of a tenant under a rental agreement and the utilities and
11 costs that are included as part of the rent charged for a dwelling unit, and to
12 document the information within a residential rental agreement. The bill
13 proposes to require that, where possible, a landlord provide individual
14 metering to separate dwelling units or otherwise ensure that the costs of
15 utilities paid by the tenant reflect the tenant’s actual use.
