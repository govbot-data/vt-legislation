4 Sec. 1. 33 V.S.A. § 5285 is amended to read:
5 § 5285. MODIFICATION OR REVOCATION OF DISPOSITION
6 (a)(1) If it appears that the youth has violated the terms of juvenile
7 probation ordered by the court pursuant to subdivision 5284(c)(1) of this title, a
8 motion for modification or revocation of youthful offender status may be filed
9 in the Family Division of the Superior Court. The court shall set the motion
10 for hearing as soon as practicable. The hearing may be joined with a hearing
11 on a violation of conditions of probation under section 5265 of this title. A
12 supervising juvenile or adult probation officer may detain in an adult facility a
13 youthful offender who has attained 18 years of age for violating conditions of
14 probation.
15 (2) Notwithstanding subdivision 5103(c)(2)(B) of this title, when a
16 motion for revocation of youthful offender status is pending pursuant to this
17 section, the Family Division’s jurisdiction over the youth shall remain in effect
18 until the youth is discharged or until probation is revoked. The Family
19 Division may extend its jurisdiction over the youth beyond the youth’s 22nd
20 birthday to the extent necessary to maintain jurisdiction under this subdivision.
