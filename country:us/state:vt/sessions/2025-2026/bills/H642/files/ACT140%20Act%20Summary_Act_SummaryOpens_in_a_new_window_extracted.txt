Act No. 140 (H.642). An act relating to youthful offender proceedings
Subjects: Human services; court procedures; youthful offender
This act makes several changes to the requirements for youthful offender proceedings
when the youth violates conditions of probation. If a youthful offender violates
probation, the act authorizes the court to extend jurisdiction beyond the youth’s 22nd
birthday until the probation violation motion is resolved, lists factors for the court to
consider when deciding whether to retain jurisdiction or send the youth to the Criminal
Division, and authorizes the court to order an officer to pick up the youth and bring the
youth to court if the youth fails to appear at the hearing. The act also permits the victim
to attend and be heard at a youthful offender consideration hearing.
Effective Date: July 1, 2026
