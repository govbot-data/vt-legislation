1 H.84
2 An act relating to allowing telehealth appointments to be recorded with
3 patient and provider consent
4 It is hereby enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Vermont:
5 Sec. 1. 18 V.S.A. § 9361 is amended to read:
6 § 9361. HEALTH CARE PROVIDERS DELIVERING HEALTH CARE
7 SERVICES THROUGH TELEMEDICINE OR BY STORE-AND-
8 FORWARD MEANS
9 * * *
10 (d) Neither a health care provider nor a patient shall create or cause to be
11 created a recording of a provider’s telemedicine consultation with a patient
12 unless both the patient and the provider consent to the recording.
13 * * *
14 Sec. 2. 18 V.S.A. § 9362 is amended to read:
15 § 9362. HEALTH CARE PROVIDERS DELIVERING HEALTH CARE
16 SERVICES BY AUDIO-ONLY TELEPHONE
17 * * *
18 (d) Neither a health care provider nor a patient shall create or cause to be
19 created a recording of a provider’s telephone consultation with a patient unless
20 both the patient and the provider consent to the recording.
