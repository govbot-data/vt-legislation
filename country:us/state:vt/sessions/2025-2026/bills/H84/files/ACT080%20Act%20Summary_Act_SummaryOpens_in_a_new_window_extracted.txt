Act No. 80 (H.84). An act relating to allowing telehealth appointments to be recorded with
patient and provider consent
Subjects: Health; consent; telehealth; recordings
This act allows patients and health care providers to record consultations conducted by
telemedicine or telephone if both the patient and the provider consent to the recording.
Effective Date: April 20, 2026
