5 § 9772. LIMITATIONS ON CONTROL OVER CLINICAL DECISION
6 MAKING BY PRIVATE EQUITY GROUP OR HEDGE FUND
7 (a) The purpose of this section is to ensure that clinical decision making
8 and treatment decisions are exclusively in the hands of health care providers
9 and to safeguard against nonlicensed individuals or entities, such as private
10 equity groups and hedge funds, exerting influence or control over health care
11 delivery.
12 (b) A private equity group or hedge fund involved in any manner with a
13 health care facility doing business in this State, including as an investor in a
14 health care facility or as an investor or owner of the assets of a health care
15 facility, shall not do any of the following with respect to the health care
16 facility:
17 (1) interfere with the judgment of health care providers in making health
18 care decisions, including any of the following:
19 (A) determining which diagnostic tests are appropriate for a
20 particular condition;
