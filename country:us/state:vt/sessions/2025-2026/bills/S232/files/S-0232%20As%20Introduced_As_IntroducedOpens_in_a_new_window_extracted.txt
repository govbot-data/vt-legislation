5 * * * Findings * * *
6 Sec. 1. FINDINGS
7 The General Assembly finds:
8 (1) The State of Vermont and its communities depend on libraries as
9 centers for learning, social connection, technological access, disaster
10 preparedness and response, support of health care and social services, and
11 numerous other vital services.
12 (2) As places of public accommodation, public libraries are obligated to
13 ensure that their services, facilities, and resources are inclusive and accessible
14 to all. However, many of Vermont’s existing library facilities require
15 improvements in order to fulfill this obligation and meet programming needs.
16 (3) Early literacy is integral to student preparedness and success.
17 Vermont’s Department of Libraries and statewide network of public libraries
18 are uniquely suited to play a critical role in improving the literacy skills of
19 students throughout the State.
