4 * * * Findings * * *
5 Sec. 1. FINDINGS
6 The General Assembly finds:
7 (1) The State of Vermont and its communities depend on libraries as
8 centers for learning, social connection, technological access, disaster
9 preparedness and response, support of health care and social services, and
10 numerous other vital services.
11 (2) As places of public accommodation, public libraries are obligated to
12 ensure that their services, facilities, and resources are inclusive and accessible
13 to all. However, many of Vermont’s existing library facilities require
14 improvements in order to fulfill this obligation and meet programming needs.
15 (3) Early literacy is integral to student preparedness and success.
16 Vermont’s Department of Libraries and statewide network of public libraries
17 are uniquely suited to play a critical role in improving the literacy skills of
18 students throughout the State.
19 (4) It is essential that the State raise awareness about public libraries and
20 their offerings in order to ensure their continued beneficial impact and
21 continued accessibility for all Vermont residents.
