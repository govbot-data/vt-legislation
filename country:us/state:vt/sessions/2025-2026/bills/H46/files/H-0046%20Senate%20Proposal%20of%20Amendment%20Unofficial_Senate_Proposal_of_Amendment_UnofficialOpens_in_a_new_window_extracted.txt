1 H.46
2 An act relating to the Rare Disease Advisory Council
3 The Senate proposes to the House to amend the bill by striking out all after
4 the enacting clause and inserting in lieu thereof the following:
5 Sec. 1. FINDINGS
6 The General Assembly finds that:
7 (1) lack of awareness contributes to common and harmful obstacles that
8 rare disease patients face, such as delays in diagnosis, misdiagnosis, lack of
9 treatment options, high out-of-pocket costs, and limited access to medical
10 specialists; and
11 (2) with the support of the National Organization for Rare Disorders,
12 various patient organizations, and stakeholders in the rare disease community,
13 rare disease advisory councils are enabling states to strategically identify and
14 address barriers that prevent individuals living with rare disease from
15 accessing adequate and effective treatment and care for their condition.
