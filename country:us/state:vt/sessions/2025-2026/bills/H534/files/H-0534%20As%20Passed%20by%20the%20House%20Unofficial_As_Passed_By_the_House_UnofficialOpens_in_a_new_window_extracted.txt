5 CHAPTER 59. COMMUNITY SERVICES ACTION AGENCIES
6 § 3901. FINDINGS AND PURPOSE
7 (a) Recognizing that the economic well-being and social equity of every
8 Vermonter has long been a fundamental concern of the State, it remains
9 evident that poverty continues to be the lot of a substantial number of
10 Vermont’s population continues to experience poverty. It is the policy of this
11 the State to help develop the full potential of each of its citizens so they can
12 contribute to the fullest extent possible to the life of our communities and the
13 State as a whole.
14 (b) It is the purpose of this chapter to strengthen, supplement, and
15 coordinate efforts that further this policy through:
16 (1) the strengthening of community capabilities for planning,
17 coordinating, and managing federal, State, and other sources of assistance
18 related to the problem of poverty;
19 (2) the better organization and utilization of a range of services related
20 to the needs of the poor individuals with low income; and

1 § 3903. DESIGNATION OF AGENCIES TO PROVIDE SERVICES AND
2 ACTIVITIES TO AMELIORATE OR ELIMINATE POVERTY
3 The Director shall designate private nonprofit community based
4 community-based organizations who that have demonstrated or who that can
5 demonstrate the ability to provide services and activities as defined in
6 subsection 3902(b) of this title as community services action agencies.
7 § 3904. COMMUNITY SERVICES ACTION AGENCY ASSESSMENT
8 AND PLAN
9 Each designated community services action agency shall determine the need
10 for activities and services within the area served by the agency and shall
11 thereafter prepare a community services plan that describes the method by
12 which the agency will provide those services every three years, complete an
13 assessment, and incorporate that information into an annual community action
14 plan. The plan shall include a schedule for the anticipated provision of new or
15 additional ongoing services and shall specify the resources that are needed by
16 and available to the agency to implement the plan. The community services
17 plan shall be updated annually.
