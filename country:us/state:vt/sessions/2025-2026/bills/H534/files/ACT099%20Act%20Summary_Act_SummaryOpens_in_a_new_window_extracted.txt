Act No. 99 (H.534). An act relating to community action agencies
Subjects: Governance; human services; duties; community action agencies
This act makes miscellaneous changes to existing statutes governing community
action agencies, including changing their name from community services agencies to
community action agencies to reflect common parlance. It further updates outdated
language pertaining to individuals with low income or individuals with substance use
disorder. This act requires each community action agency to determine the need for
activities and services within the area served by the agency every three years, complete
an assessment, and incorporate that information into an annual community action plan. It
removes statutory requirements around term limits for community action agency board
members and instead allows each board to adopt its own term limits.
Effective Date: July 1, 2026
