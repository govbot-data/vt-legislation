8 Statement of purpose of bill as introduced: This bill proposes to require the
9 Division of Emergency Management to provide financial assistance to flood-
10 impacted communities, establish a Voluntary Buyout Program and
11 Homeowner Assistance Program for flood-prone or flood-impacted properties,
12 and provide technical and grant application assistance to municipalities
13 through two new liaison positions added to the Division. This bill also
14 proposes to authorize the Agency of Transportation to use the Municipal
15 Mitigation Assistance Program to assist rural and flood-prone municipalities
16 with infrastructure upgrades and require the Agency to provide technical and
17 design assistance for certain infrastructure projects. This bill also requires the
18 Agency of Natural Resources to establish programs to implement and improve
19 stream flow and river corridor naturalization projects.

1 It is hereby enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Vermont:
2 Sec. 1. 20 V.S.A. § 3b is added to read:
3 § 3b. FINANCIAL ASSISTANCE FOR IMPACTED MUNICIPALITIES
4 (a) A municipality that has been impacted by an all-hazard flooding event
5 shall be eligible for financial assistance from the Division of Emergency
6 Management to cover expenses incurred due to the all-hazard while awaiting
7 reimbursement from the Federal Emergency Management Agency, including:
8 (1) emergency response costs;
9 (2) temporary housing and shelter services; and
10 (3) local infrastructure repairs that are necessary to maintain public
11 safety.
12 (b) A municipality that has been impacted by an all-hazard flooding event
13 shall be eligible for State funding to cover all of the local match requirements
14 required by federal law or the Federal Emergency Management Agency for
15 flood recovery and assistance.
