5 Sec. 1. 18 V.S.A. chapter 91, subchapter 7 is added to read:
6 Subchapter 7. Vermont Prescription Drug Discount Card Program
7 § 4691. VERMONT PRESCRIPTION DRUG DISCOUNT CARD
8 PROGRAM
9 (a) There is established the Vermont Prescription Drug Discount Card
10 Program, administered by the Office of the State Treasurer, for the purpose of
11 pooling prescription drug purchasing power with other U.S. states and
12 territories and nongovernmental organizations. The Program shall be made
13 available to all Vermont residents.
14 (b)(1) To further the purposes of the Program, the State Treasurer may
15 cooperate with other U.S. states and territories, regional consortia, or
16 nongovernmental organizations, or a combination of these, to pool prescription
17 drug purchasing power to:
18 (A) reduce prescription drug costs;
19 (B) negotiate discounts with prescription drug manufacturers;
20 (C) centralize prescription drug purchasing; and
21 (D) establish volume discount contracting.
