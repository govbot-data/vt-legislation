SENATE PROPOSAL OF AMENDMENT H.577
2026 Page 1 of 1
1 H.577
2 An act relating to establishing the Vermont Prescription Drug Discount
3 Card Program
4 The Senate proposes to the House to amend the bill as follows:
5 First: In Sec. 1, 18 V.S.A. chapter 91, subchapter 7, in section 4691, by
6 striking out subsection (e) in its entirety and inserting in lieu thereof a new
7 subsection (e) to read as follows:
8 (e) On or before January 15, 2028, and annually thereafter, the State
9 Treasurer shall submit a report to the House Committee on Health Care, the
10 Senate Committee on Health and Welfare, and the Governor detailing the
11 activities of the Program during the previous calendar year, including the
12 number of Vermont residents and pharmacies participating in the Program; the
13 amount of savings on prescription drug costs achieved; and the impact, if any,
14 of the Program on the viability of Vermont’s pharmacies.
15 Second: In Sec. 3a, 32 V.S.A. § 111, in subsection (b), in the second
16 sentence, following “Drug Discount”, by inserting “Card” preceding
17 “Program”
