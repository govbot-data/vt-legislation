15 § 4691. VERMONT PRESCRIPTION DRUG DISCOUNT CARD
16 PROGRAM
17 (a) There is established the Vermont Prescription Drug Discount Card
18 Program, administered by the Office of the State Treasurer, for the purpose of
19 pooling prescription drug purchasing power with other U.S. states and

3 (b)(1) To further the purposes of the Program, the State Treasurer may

17 by the Treasurer based on actual costs.

3 § 4692. VERMONT PRESCRIPTION DRUG DISCOUNT CARD
4 PROGRAM FUND
5 (a) The Vermont Prescription Drug Discount Card Program Fund is
6 established as a special fund to be administered by the State Treasurer to
7 support the Vermont Prescription Drug Discount Card Program established in
8 this subchapter.
9 (b) The Fund shall consist of:
10 (1) any monies appropriated to the Fund by the General Assembly;
11 (2) any monies transferred to the Fund from the federal government,
12 other State agencies, or other government source;
13 (3) any monies from the payment of fees or other monies due to the
14 Program; and
15 (4) any gifts, grants, or donations made to the Fund and any gifts, grants,
16 donations, or investments received by the Treasurer for the Program.
17 (c) The Treasurer shall credit to the Fund all interest earned on Fund
18 balances and any other income derived from the deposit and investment of
19 monies in the Fund.
20 (d) Any unexpended and unencumbered monies in the Fund at the end of a
21 fiscal year shall remain in the Fund.

1 Sec. 2. VERMONT PRESCRIPTION DRUG DISCOUNT CARD
2 PROGRAM; IMPLEMENTATION REPORT
3 On or before January 15, 2027, the State Treasurer shall report to the
4 General Assembly regarding implementation of the Vermont Prescription Drug
5 Discount Card Program established in 18 V.S.A. chapter 91, subchapter 7, as
6 added by Sec. 1 of this act, as of that date, including any recommendations for
7 improving the administration of the Program, any fees to be charged to
8 participants, and an estimate of the projected costs to the State in the event that
9 additional financial support is determined to be necessary to administer the
10 Program.
11 Sec. 3. VERMONT PRESCRIPTION DRUG DISCOUNT CARD
12 PROGRAM; EVALUATION AND START-UP FUNDING
13 In fiscal year 2027, the sum of $50,000.00 is appropriated from the General
14 Fund to the Office of the State Treasurer for the costs of developing and
15 implementing the Vermont Prescription Drug Discount Card Program as set
16 forth in this act.
17 Sec. 4. EFFECTIVE DATE
18 This act shall take effect on July 1, 2026.
