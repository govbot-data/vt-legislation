5 Subject: Motor vehicles; operating under the influence of alcohol or drugs;
6 penalties
7 Statement of purpose of bill as introduced: This bill proposes to clarify that
8 the enhanced penalty for a second or subsequent violation of operating a motor
9 vehicle under the influence of alcohol or drugs applies when the operator has
10 been convicted of a previous violation of the offense within the 20 years
11 preceding the date of the subsequent violation, not the date of the conviction
12 for the subsequent violation.
13 An act relating to penalties for second or subsequent violations of operating
14 a motor vehicle under the influence of alcohol or drugs
15 It is hereby enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Vermont:
16 Sec. 1. 23 V.S.A. § 1210 is amended to read:
17 § 1210. PENALTIES
