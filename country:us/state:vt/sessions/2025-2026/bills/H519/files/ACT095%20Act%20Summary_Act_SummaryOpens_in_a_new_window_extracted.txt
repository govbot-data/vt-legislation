Act No. 95 (H.519). An act relating to Vermont State Employees’ Retirement System
Group G membership
Subjects: Vermont State Employees’ Retirement System; Group G membership;
municipal and county government; law enforcement officers
This act amends the Vermont State Employees’ Retirement System Group G
membership to add employees who are employed on or after January 1, 2027, who:
(1) are employed by a municipal employer that participates in the Vermont Employees’
Retirement System; (2) have attained a Level II or Level III law enforcement office
certification from the Vermont Criminal Justice Council; and (3) are required to perform
law enforcement duties as the primary function of their employment.
The act provides employees that are currently employed in these positions a one-time
irrevocable election to join Group G membership.
Effective Date: July 1, 2026
