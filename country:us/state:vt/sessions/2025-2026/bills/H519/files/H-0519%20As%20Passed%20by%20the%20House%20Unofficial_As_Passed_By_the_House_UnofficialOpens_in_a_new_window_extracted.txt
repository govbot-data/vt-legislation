5 Sec. 3. 3 V.S.A. § 489 is amended to read:
6 § 489. BENEFITS
7 Persons who become members of the Vermont State Retirement System
8 under this subchapter and on behalf of whom contributions are paid as
9 provided in this subchapter shall be entitled to benefits under the Vermont
10 State Retirement System as though they were employees of the State of
11 Vermont. These employees shall be considered “Group F members” as
12 defined in subdivision 455(a)(11)(E) of this title, except that:
13 (1) elected municipal employees shall not be subject to mandatory
14 retirement requirements; and
15 (2) sheriffs and those deputy sheriffs who meet the requirements
16 pursuant to subdivision 455(a)(11)(F)(ii) of this chapter shall be considered
17 members of Group G; and
18 (3) municipal law enforcement officers who meet the requirements
19 pursuant to subdivision 455(a)(11)(F)(iii) of this chapter shall be considered
20 members of Group G.
