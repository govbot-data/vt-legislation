2 An act relating to an energy navigator program report
3 It is hereby enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Vermont:
4 Sec. 1. ENERGY NAVIGATOR PROGRAM; REPORT
5 (a) The Department of Public Service shall contract with a third-party
6 consultant to design a Vermont community-based home energy navigator and
7 coaching program, in collaboration with the Climate Action Center of Addison
8 County and other existing community-based energy navigator programs in
9 Vermont, that will provide in-person and remote energy coaching services to
10 residential consumers in communities statewide. The Department’s consultant
11 shall build on findings from the Department’s comprehensive process and
12 performance evaluation of more than 100 publicly funded energy programs
13 focused on affordability, including electric and thermal efficiency,
14 weatherization for customers with low income, and beneficial electrification
15 initiatives to inform the design of a Vermont community-based home energy
16 navigator and coaching program. The Department’s consultant shall consult
17 with Efficiency Vermont, the Vermont State Energy Office, the Vermont
18 Climate Action Office, Vermont’s community action agencies, the Vermont
19 Energy and Climate Action Network, Vermont’s electric utilities, community-
20 based home energy navigator and coaching programs, and other states,
21 including Connecticut and Massachusetts, that have experience with
