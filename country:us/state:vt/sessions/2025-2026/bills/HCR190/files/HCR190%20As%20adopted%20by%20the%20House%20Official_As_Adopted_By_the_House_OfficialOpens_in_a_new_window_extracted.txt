State of Vermont
House of Representatives
Montpelier, Vermont
Concurrent House Resolution
H.C.R. 190
Offered by Representatives Holcombe of Norwich and Masland of Thetford
House concurrent resolution congratulating the Thetford Academy Panthers boys’ indoor
track and field team on winning a second consecutive Division II championship
Whereas, although Thetford Academy is primarily a Division III varsity sports school, for
indoor track and field it is a Division II competitor against larger secondary institutions, and
Whereas, the boys’ indoor track and field team treats this size disparity as a challenge and not
a deterrence to successful competition, as the Panthers demonstrated when they clinched the
2025 Division II crown, and
Whereas, in 2026, the Panthers again demonstrated their track and field skills, placing first in
the 55-meter dash (6.60 seconds), the 300-meter run (36.96 seconds), the high jump (5 feet,
10.75 inches), the long jump (21 feet, 11.75 inches), and the weight throw (40 feet, nine inches),
as well as in their winning time of 3 minutes, 55 seconds in the first-round race of the 4x400
relay, to repeat as Division II champions, narrowly outscoring rival U-32 115–109, and
Whereas, Panthers Zac Adams, Wendell Durham, Oliver Emery, Oliver Goodrich, Owen
Goodrich, Tanner Hardy, Peter Horan, Daniel Mann, Kit Payson, Sebastian Perdrizet, Stone
Riegler, Connor Sloop, Hayden Southworth, Emmett Terry, and David Thaxton, and
Whereas, Head Coach Charlie Buttrey and assistant coaches Jorl Breakstone, Charlize Brown,
Cole Chapman, and Vanessa Riegler were proud of these dedicated athletes, and
Whereas, all the Vermont indoor varsity track and field athletes and coaches are hoping the
sport will continue, as the availability of a location for the State championship meet is limited
and now compels the season to be truncated with an early January conclusion, now therefore be
it
Resolved by the Senate and House of Representatives:
That the General Assembly congratulates the Thetford Academy Panthers boys’ indoor track
and field team on winning a second consecutive Division II championship, and be it further
Resolved: That the Secretary of State be directed to send a copy of this resolution to Thetford
Academy.
