13 Sec. 1. SPECIAL EDUCATION FAMILY ADVOCACY PILOT PROGRAM
14 (a) In fiscal year 2027, $250,000.00 is appropriated from the General Fund
15 to the Agency of Human Services to establish a two-year pilot program that
16 awards grants to develop special education family advocacy pilot programs.
17 The pilot programs shall aim to:
18 (1) ensure that families of kindergarten through grade 12 students have
19 access to knowledgeable, neutral advocates to guide them through the
