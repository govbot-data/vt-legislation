1 S.255
2 An act relating to establishing a pilot Law Enforcement Governance
3 Council in Windham County
4 It is hereby enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Vermont:
5 Sec. 1. DEFINITIONS
6 As used in this chapter:
7 (1) “Council” means the Windham County Law Enforcement
8 Governance Council established pursuant to this chapter.
9 (2) “Member municipality” means a municipality within Windham
10 County that has voted to participate in the Council and receive public safety
11 services pursuant to this chapter.
12 (3) “Nonmember municipality” means a municipality within Windham
13 County that has not voted or voted not to participate in the Council.
14 (4) “Public safety services” means law enforcement, dispatch services,
15 animal control, and other related services as determined by the Council.
16 (5) “Sheriff” means the Sheriff of Windham County or designee.
17 Sec. 2. AUTHORIZATION AND ESTABLISHMENT
18 (a) Municipalities within Windham County are authorized to establish a
19 law enforcement governance council for the purpose of providing regional law
20 enforcement and related services during the pilot period.
21 (b) The Council shall be established when five or more eligible
22 municipalities vote to become member municipalities.

1 (c) The Council shall be established and associated operations and taxation
2 shall commence on either July 1, 2026, or the date on which five or more
3 member municipalities have voted to become member municipalities,
4 whichever occurs later.
5 Sec. 3. MEMBERSHIP
6 (a) A municipality within Windham County may join the Council by
7 majority vote of the legislative body of the municipality.
8 (b) Each member municipality shall:
9 (1) Have one representative, with one vote, on the Council. A member
10 municipality’s representative shall be a serving selectboard member of that
11 municipality, to be selected by the municipality’s selectboard.
12 (2) Receive law enforcement and related services as determined by the
13 Council.
14 (3) Pay its proportionate share of costs through the county tax
15 mechanism.
16 (c) A member municipality may withdraw from the Council by majority
17 vote of the legislative body of the municipality, with such withdrawal
18 becoming effective at the end of the next fiscal year following the vote.
19 Sec. 4. GOVERNANCE
20 (a) The Council shall consist of one representative from each member
21 municipality.

3 (c) The Council shall meet at least quarterly and at such other times as

15 (5) monitor service delivery and evaluate program effectiveness;
16 (6) submit the approved budget to the Windham County Assistant
17 Judges for inclusion in the county budget; and
18 (7) enter into agreements necessary for the provision of services.
19 Sec. 5. BUDGET AND FINANCING
