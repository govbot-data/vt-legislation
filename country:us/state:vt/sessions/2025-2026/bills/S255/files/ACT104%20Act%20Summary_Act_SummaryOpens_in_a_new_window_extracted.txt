Act No. 104 (S.255). An act relating to establishing a pilot Law Enforcement Governance
Council in Windham County
Subjects: Public safety; law enforcement; municipal and county government;
sheriffs; assistant judges
This act creates a pilot program named the Windham County Law Enforcement
Governance Council to provide orchestrated law enforcement and related services to
participating towns within Windham County through June 30, 2034. Towns in Windham
County may join and exit the Council by vote of the town’s legislative body, five of
which are required to establish the Council. The specific services are determined by the
Council, provided by the Windham County Sheriff’s Department, and funded by a special
assessment on member municipalities in proportion to the population of each member.
The Council shall submit annually reports to certain legislative committees, the Windham
County legislative delegation, and member municipalities containing basic program
metrics, and also submit midterm and final reports to the General Assembly containing an
assessment of the program’s success and a proposed framework for statewide replication,
if warranted.
Effective Date: May 19, 2026
