HOUSE PROPOSAL OF AMENDMENT S.255
2026 Page 1 of 1
1 S.255
2 An act relating to establishing a pilot Law Enforcement Governance
3 Council in Windham County
4 The House proposes to the Senate to amend the bill in Sec. 7, reporting and
5 evaluation, by striking out subsection (b) in its entirety and inserting in lieu
6 thereof a new subsection (b) to read as follows:
7 (b) On or before September 30, 2030, and again on or before December 31,
8 2033, the Council, in consultation with the Windham County Sheriff and
9 Windham County Assistant Judges, shall submit a comprehensive evaluation
10 of the pilot program to the House Committee on Government Operations and
11 Military Affairs and the Senate Committee on Government Operations,
12 including:
13 (1) an assessment of cost-effectiveness compared to alternative service
14 delivery models;
15 (2) an analysis of service quality improvements;
16 (3) an evaluation of the governance model’s effectiveness;
17 (4) recommendations regarding continuation, modification, or expansion
18 of the program; and
19 (5) a proposed framework for statewide replication, if warranted.
