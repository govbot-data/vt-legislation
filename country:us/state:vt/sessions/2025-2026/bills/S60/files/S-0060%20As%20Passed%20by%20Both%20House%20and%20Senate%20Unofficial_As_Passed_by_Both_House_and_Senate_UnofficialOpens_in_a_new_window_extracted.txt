2 An act relating to establishing the Farm and Forestry Operations Security
3 Special Fund to provide payments for farm and forestry operation losses
4 due to weather conditions
5 It is hereby enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Vermont:
6 Sec. 1. FINDINGS
7 The General Assembly finds that:
8 (1) In 2023 and 2024, Vermont experienced extreme flooding and other
9 weather-based disasters that devastated farms and other working lands
10 businesses across the State.
11 (2) Many existing State and federal programs that are designed to
12 support farms and forestry operations are difficult to access, are
13 administratively burdensome, are not equitably distributed to small- and
14 medium-scale farmers and forestry operations, and currently do not meet the
15 needs of Vermont farmers and forestry operations in a holistic way.
16 (3) In particular, because federal crop insurance programs are not
17 designed to serve the needs of smaller scale or more diversified farming
18 operations, many Vermont farmers are not covered by crop insurance.
19 (4) The State should establish a permanent funding support program to:
20 (A) maintain the viability of farms and forestry operations in
21 Vermont in order to ensure food security, resilience, rural economic vitality,
22 and environmental health;

11 As used in this subchapter:

16 (B) excessive moisture, intense precipitation, or flooding;
17 (C) extreme heat;
18 (D) abnormal freeze conditions;
19 (E) a forest fire or wild fire event;
20 (F) hail;

8 § 4643. FARM AND FORESTRY OPERATIONS SECURITY SPECIAL
9 FUND; PAYMENTS
10 (a) The Secretary, after consultation with the Review Board, shall award
11 payments from the Farm and Forestry Operations Security Special Fund to
12 persons owning farms and forestry operations that have incurred financial
13 losses or expenses due to an eligible weather condition.
14 (1) Payments from the Fund shall be in an amount that reimburses a
15 farm or forestry operation for up to 50 percent of the unreimbursed, uninsured,
16 or otherwise uncovered losses due to eligible weather conditions, up to a
17 maximum annual award of five percent of the undesignated and unreserved
18 funds in the Farm and Forestry Operations Security Special Fund at the
19 beginning of each fiscal year, provided that the award shall not exceed
20 $150,000.00 per qualified applicant farm or forestry operation per year.
