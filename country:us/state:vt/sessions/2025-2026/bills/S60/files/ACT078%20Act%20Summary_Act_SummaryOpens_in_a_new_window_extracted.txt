Act No. 78 (S.60). An act relating to establishing the Farm and Forestry Operations
Security Special Fund to provide payments for farm and forestry operation losses
due to weather conditions
Subjects: Funding; agriculture; disaster relief
This act establishes the Farm and Forestry Operations Security Special Fund from
which the Secretary of Agriculture, Food and Markets shall award payments to persons
owning farms and forestry operations that have incurred financial losses or expenses due
to an eligible weather condition. The act defines “eligible weather condition” as any
weather condition that is found to be closely correlated with agricultural or forest
operation income losses, including high winds; excessive moisture, intense precipitation,
or flooding; extreme heat; abnormal freeze conditions; a forest fire or wild fire event;
hail; or drought.
Payments from the Fund shall be in an amount that reimburses a farm or forestry
operation for up to 50 percent of the unreimbursed, uninsured, or otherwise uncovered
losses due to eligible weather conditions, up to a maximum annual award of five percent
of the undesignated and unreserved funds in the Farm and Forestry Operations Security
Special Fund at the beginning of each fiscal year, provided that the award shall not
exceed $150,000.00 per qualified applicant farm or forestry operation per year. Losses
reimbursable by a payment from the Fund include wages or compensation; replacement
of lost income from destroyed crops, impacted livestock, or timber; debt payments or
other ongoing expenses; costs of replanting; livestock feed replacement costs;
infrastructure or equipment repair and replacement; repair of farm roads or roads
necessary to access farms; repair of washed out or otherwise damaged logging roads;
inability to access harvested timber due to flooding or other weather conditions; and other
losses as determined by the Secretary of Agriculture, Food and Markets after consultation
with the Farm and Forestry Operations Security Special Fund Review Board.
The act creates the Farm and Forestry Operations Security Special Fund Review
Board to review applications for assistance from the Farm and Forestry Operations
Security Special Fund and recommend to the Secretary applicants who should receive
assistance.
Effective Date: July 1, 2026
