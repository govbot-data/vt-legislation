State of Vermont
House of Representatives
Montpelier, Vermont
Concurrent House Resolution
H.C.R. 267
Offered by Representatives Graning of Jericho, Brown of Richmond, Squirrell of Underhill, Stevens
of Waterbury, and Wood of Waterbury
Offered by Senators Chittenden, Lyons, and Ram Hinsdale
House concurrent resolution congratulating the Mt. Mansfield Union High School Cougars girls’
Nordic ski team on winning a second consecutive Division I championship
Whereas, as the winners of the 2025 Division I title, Mt. Mansfield Union High School (MMU)
sought to repeat last year’s triumph, and
Whereas, the Nordic skiing championship is a two-day event; the day-one freestyle competition was
held at the Rikert Outdoor Center in Ripton, and the concluding second day of classic skiing occurred at
the Craftsbury Outdoor Center, and, at both locations, individual and relay races were conducted, and
Whereas, as in 2025, the Cougars skiers again eclipsed their competitors, winning the freestyle relay
with a time of 28:44.6; and, in the associated individual race, they clinched the top two slots with times
of 14:35.1 and 14:43.0; and, while at Craftsbury, MMU was the speediest with the relay time of 30:40.1;
and, once again, the top two individual finishers, at 16:31.1 and 16:41.13, proudly wore the Cougars
colors, and
Whereas, when all the scores were tallied, MMU far surpassed runner-up St. Johnsbury Academy
51–133 (the lower being the better score in Nordic skiing), and
Whereas, the Nordic trail-loving Cougars were Elizabeth Compo, Katherine Compo, Emma Connell,
Corinne Courtemanche, Acadia Enman, Lena Haggerty, Astrid Longstreth, Pearl Page, Fiona Repp,
Norah Thompson, Rosemary Thompson, Avery Wood, Macallister Wyatt, and Ryley Morigeau, and
Whereas, throughout the Nordic skiing season, the team’s coaches, Wylie Picotte and Al Sartwell,
offered expert guidance that provided the skiers a winning advantage on the trails, now therefore be it
Resolved by the Senate and House of Representatives:
That the General Assembly congratulates the Mt. Mansfield Union High School Cougars girls’
Nordic ski team on winning a second consecutive Division I championship, and be it further
Resolved: That the Secretary of State be directed to send a copy of this resolution to Mt. Mansfield
Union High School.
