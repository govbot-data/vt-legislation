8 Statement of purpose of bill as introduced: This bill proposes to make all
9 public employees who are eligible to receive health benefits through their
10 employment, including current and retired State employees, school employees,
11 municipal employees, and employees of the University of Vermont and the
12 Vermont State Colleges, eligible for coverage through the State employees’
13 health plan, which would become the public employees’ health plan. It would
14 establish the independent Commission on Public Employee Health Benefits to
15 determine the design of the health benefit plan and the respective percentages
16 of plan premiums that will be borne by the public employers and by the
17 participating public employees. The bill would also remove health care from
18 the matters subject to collective bargaining between public employers and
19 public employees.
