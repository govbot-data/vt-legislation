State of Vermont
House of Representatives
Montpelier, Vermont
Concurrent House Resolution
H.C.R. 256
Offered by Representatives Luneau of St. Albans City, Bartley of Fairfax, Branagan of Georgia, Brigham of
St. Albans Town, Demar of Enosburgh, Dickinson of St. Albans Town, Gregoire of Fairfield, Hango of
Berkshire, Laroche of Franklin, Oliver of Sheldon, and Walker of Swanton
House concurrent resolution congratulating Grant Montgomery on his dual State championship alpine skiing
victories
Whereas, the absence of a varsity alpine skiing team at Bellows Free Academy-St. Albans has not deterred
junior Grant Montgomery’s competitive aspirations, and he arranged to train as an independent with Rice
Memorial High School at Cochran’s ski area, gaining the benefits of Rice Coach Bob Cochran’s guidance and
the experience of racing with the Rice team, and
Whereas, this unusual arrangement, in combination with racing alongside coaches from the Smugglers’
Notch Ski and Snowboard Club, built a great foundation for Grant Montgomery to establish an enviable record
of achievement during the 2025–2026 alpine ski racing season, and
Whereas, Grant Montgomery won nearly every race he entered, and his high expectations for strong
performances at the season finale’s district, State, and regional meets were exceeded, and
Whereas, at the start of this critical circuit, Grant Montgomery warmed up by capturing first place in both
the giant slalom and slalom races at the northern district event at the Middlebury Snowbowl, and
Whereas, after only a few intervening days, Grant Montgomery headed to Burke Mountain Resort for the
greater challenge of the two-day State championship, and
Whereas, in day one’s giant slalom, he finished the first of the two runs in fourth place, and a strong second
run gave him a combined time of 1:53:68, a narrow but decisive .22 seconds ahead of the second-place finisher,
and on day two at Burke, his two outstanding slalom runs scored a combined time of 1:29:24, a comfortable
3.72 seconds ahead of the runner-up, enabling him to clinch both individual titles and complete a double-header
victory, and
Whereas, his superb 2026 winter on the slopes earned Grant Montgomery a coveted spot on Vermont’s
Eastern High School Championship alpine team, and at the Attitash Mountain Resort event in New Hampshire,
the Vermonters were pleased with their overall fourth-place finish, and
Whereas, in recognition of his stupendous results at Burke Mountain, the St. Albans Messenger named him
the newspaper’s Athlete of the Week, now therefore be it
Resolved by the Senate and House of Representatives:
That the General Assembly congratulates Grant Montgomery on his dual State championship alpine skiing
victories, and be it further
Resolved: That the Secretary of State be directed to send a copy of this resolution to Grant Montgomery.
