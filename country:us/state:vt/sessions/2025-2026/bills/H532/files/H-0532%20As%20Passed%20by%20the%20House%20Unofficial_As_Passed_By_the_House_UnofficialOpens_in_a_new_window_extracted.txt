1 H.532
2 An act relating to mandatory retirement of college professors
3 It is hereby enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Vermont:
4 Sec. 1. 21 V.S.A. § 495g is amended to read:
5 § 495g. PROVISION APPLICABLE TO COLLEGE PROFESSORS
6 Nothing in this subchapter shall be construed to prohibit any institution of
7 higher education as defined by section 1201(a) of the federal Higher Education
8 Act of 1965 from retiring any employee who is serving under a contract of
9 unlimited tenure, who attains 70 years of age. Any employee whose tenure
10 contract is terminated may, in the discretion of the institution, be allowed to
11 continue in the employ of the institution on a nontenured basis. [Repealed.]
12 Sec. 2. EFFECTIVE DATE
13 This act shall take effect on July 1, 2026.
