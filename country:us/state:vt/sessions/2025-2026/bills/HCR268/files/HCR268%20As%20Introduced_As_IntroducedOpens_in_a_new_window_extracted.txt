State of Vermont
House of Representatives
Montpelier, Vermont
Concurrent House Resolution
H.C.R. 268
Offered by Representatives Graning of Jericho, Brown of Richmond, Squirrell of Underhill, Stevens
of Waterbury, and Wood of Waterbury
Offered by Senators Chittenden, Lyons, and Ram Hinsdale
House concurrent resolution congratulating the 2026 Mt. Mansfield Union High School Cougars
State co-champions girls’ snowboarding team
Whereas, the annual State high school snowboarding championship is conducted under the aegis of
the Snowboarding Council, consisting of coaches from the participating schools, and
Whereas, this year’s decisive high school snowboarding conclave was held on March 4, 2026, at Jay
Peak, and
Whereas, the competing snowboarding teams raced in three events: slopestyle, rail jam, and giant
slalom; and upon the completion of their skiing on the slopes, the judges tallied the scores, recording a
tie between the Cougars of Mt. Mansfield Union High School (MMU) and the Wasps of Woodstock
Union High School, resulting in the two teams sharing unusual co-championship honors, and
Whereas, MMU also brought home the individual title, and
Whereas, the first-ever Cougars girls’ championship snowboarders, all members of the Cougars first-
year ski and ride club, which Snowboarding Coach Derek Howard and MMU Athletic Director Mike
Abbott established, were Marlee Anderson, Riley Ayers, Sarah Diglio, Cassidy Thompson, and Sydney
Howard, and
Whereas, coaches Derek Howard and Chris Kasper were thrilled with the MMU girls’ snowboarders’
success on the slopes, now therefore be it
Resolved by the Senate and House of Representatives:
That the General Assembly congratulates the 2026 Mt. Mansfield Union High School Cougars State
co-champions girls’ snowboarding team, and be it further
Resolved: That the Secretary of State be directed to send a copy of this resolution to MMU.
