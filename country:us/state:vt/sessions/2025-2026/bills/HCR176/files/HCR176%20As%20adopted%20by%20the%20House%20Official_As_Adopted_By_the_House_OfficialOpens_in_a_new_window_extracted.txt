State of Vermont
House of Representatives
Montpelier, Vermont
Concurrent House Resolution
H.C.R. 176
Offered by Representatives Bosch of Clarendon and Burditt of West Rutland
Offered by Senators Collamore, Weeks, and Williams
House concurrent resolution congratulating the 2025 West Rutland High School Golden
Horde’s Division IV championship softball team
Whereas, seldom does a softball team, even one seeded first in playoff competition, tally the
stratospheric number of runs that the Golden Horde amassed in Castleton during the 2025
Division IV championship game against the third-seeded Leland & Gray Rebels, and
Whereas, the Golden Horde’s early 2–0 lead was temporarily sidelined when the Rebels
scored four runs in the fourth inning, but the West Rutlanders proved resilient as they crossed
home plate an impressive seven times in the fifth inning and an even more remarkable nine times
in the sixth to conclude the game with an unusually powerful 19–4 victory, and
Whereas, the 2025 softball title marked West Rutland High School’s class of 2025’s sixth
athletic crown, truly an enviable achievement, and
Whereas, the fantastic Golden Horde Division IV championship softball players were Isabella
Coombs, Peyton Guay, Kennah Wright-Chapman, Aubrey Beaulieu, Camryn Williams, Sohyr
Perry, Izzy Griffith, Hayley Raiche, Emily Hopp, Karen Firpo, Bradee Traverse, Olivia Pratt,
Sadie McGee, Brianna Gallipo, Rylee Hawkins, Sofia Coombs, Emma Lee, and Emma
Haley, and
Whereas, Head Coach Laurie Serrani, assistant coaches Carl Serrani and Mikayla Taylor,
student manager Gabby Davis, and athletic trainer Zach Royer were all thrilled with West
Rutland’s perseverance and amazing success, now therefore be it
Resolved by the Senate and House of Representatives:
That the General Assembly congratulates the 2025 West Rutland High School Golden
Horde’s Division IV championship softball team, and be it further
Resolved: That the Secretary of State be directed to send a copy of this resolution to West
Rutland High School.
