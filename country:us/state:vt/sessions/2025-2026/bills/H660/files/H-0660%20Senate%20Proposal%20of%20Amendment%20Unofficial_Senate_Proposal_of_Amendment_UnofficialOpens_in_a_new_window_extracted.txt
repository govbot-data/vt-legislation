7 Sec. 5. QUARTERLY REPORTING; EXPENDITURE OF OPIOID
8 ABATEMENT SPECIAL FUND MONIES
9 The Department of Health shall submit to the General Assembly quarterly
10 reports regarding expenditures from the Opioid Abatement Special Fund.
11 Specifically, the reports shall identify funds appropriated from the Special
12 Fund that remain unobligated or unspent, or both, and shall include an
13 explanation as to why the funds have not been fully distributed. Reports due
14 on October 1, 2026, and July 1, 2027, shall be submitted to the Joint Fiscal
15 Committee. Reports due on January 1, 2027, and April 1, 2027, shall be
16 submitted to the House Committees on Appropriations and on Human Services
17 and to the Senate Committees on Appropriations and on Health and Welfare.
18 The Department shall post reports required pursuant to this section on its
19 website.
