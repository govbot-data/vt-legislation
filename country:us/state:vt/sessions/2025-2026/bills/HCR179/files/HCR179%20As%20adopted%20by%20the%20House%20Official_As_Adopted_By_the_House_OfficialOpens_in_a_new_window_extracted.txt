State of Vermont
House of Representatives
Montpelier, Vermont
Concurrent House Resolution
H.C.R. 179
Offered by Representatives Black of Essex, Dodge of Essex, Dolan of Essex Junction, Garofano of
Essex, Houghton of EssexJunction, and Steady of Milton
Offered by Senators Baruth, Gulick, Mattos, and Vyhovsky
House concurrent resolution congratulating the Essex High School Hornets girls’ volleyball team on
winning the school’s fifth consecutive divisional championship
Whereas, for the prior four seasons, the Essex High School girls’ volleyball team concluded the
season as the State titleholder, and the 2025 squad, featuring a starting lineup of five seniors and one
sophomore, possessed the talent to extend this championship streak, and
Whereas, the top-seeded and undefeated Hornets revisited their 2024 final contest as, once again, the
second-ranked BFA–St. Albans Comets were their worthy opponents in a game played in Colchester at
St. Michael’s College, and
Whereas, the opening set was an exciting and closely played affair, necessitating exceeding the
typical 25-point finish mark before the Hornets secured a 26–24 victory, while Essex’s overwhelming
mastery on the court earned the team a 25–11 second-set win, and although the Comets recovered their
offensive stamina in the third set, the Hornets still emerged on top 25–21, for an overall 3–0 game
victory, their fifth consecutive undefeated season and a championship title, and the school’s 86th
consecutive win, and
Whereas, the 2025 Hornets (17–0), who celebrated another triumphant season, were Riley Austin,
Anna Hotaling, Rheya Gregory, Reese Gregory, Lucy Walker-Borch, Hannah Knickerbocker, Eleanor
Hutton, Riley Hammond, Emma Orlow, Abigail Desilets, Mia Eklof, and Lia Wolinsky, and
Whereas, Head Coach Jennifer Liguori and assistant coaches Hope Lagemann and Phil Reynolds
provided insightful guidance all season, now therefore be it
Resolved by the Senate and House of Representatives:
That the General Assembly congratulates the 2025 Essex High School Hornets girls’ volleyball team
on winning the school’s fifth consecutive divisional championship, and be it further
Resolved: That the Secretary of State be directed to send a copy of this resolution to Essex High
School.
