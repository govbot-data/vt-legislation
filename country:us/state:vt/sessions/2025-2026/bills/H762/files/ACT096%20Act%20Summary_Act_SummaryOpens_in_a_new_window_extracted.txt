Act No. 96 (H.762). An act relating to the County and Regional Governance Study
Committee
Subjects: Municipal and county government; structure of county governance
This act amends 2024 Acts and Resolves No. 118, which created the County and
Regional Governance Study Committee. Specifically, this act inserts a legislative intent
section, modifies some of the Committee’s powers and duties, and extends the reporting
deadline by two years and the Committee’s date of termination by a year and a half.
Effective Date: May 13, 2026
