2 An act relating to the County and Regional Governance Study Committee
3 It is hereby enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Vermont:
4 Sec. 1. 2024 Acts and Resolves No. 118, Sec. 1 is amended to read:
5 Sec. 1. COUNTY AND REGIONAL GOVERNANCE STUDY
6 COMMITTEE; REPORT
7 (a) Creation Legislative intent and creation of Committee.
8 (1) Legislative intent. It is the intent of the General Assembly that
9 existing direct coordination and communication between the State and
10 Vermont municipalities should be retained or strengthened. County or regional
11 governance should enhance the work of local and State governments and not
12 become a procedural barrier between the State and municipalities.
13 (2) Creation of Committee. There is created the County and Regional
14 Governance Study Committee to address local government capacity
15 challenges, enhance and optimize public safety, regional collaboration and
16 planning, efficient, equitable, and transparent public resource allocation, and
17 effective regional public services for individuals and municipalities.
18 (b) Membership. The Committee shall be, to the extent possible, composed
19 of members from geographically diverse regions of the State. The Committee
20 shall elect its chair from among its members. The Committee shall be
21 composed of the following members:
