State of Vermont
House of Representatives
Montpelier, Vermont
Concurrent House Resolution
H.C.R. 285
Offered by Representatives Greer of Bennington, Cooper of Pownal, Corcoran of Bennington, Durfee of
Shaftsbury, Morrissey of Bennington, and Nigro of Bennington
Offered by Senators Bongartz and Plunkett
House concurrent resolution recognizing the fifth anniversary of the Middlebury Language Schools–
Bennington College intercultural education collaboration
Whereas, in the 21st century, knowledge of foreign languages has become essential to succeed in an
increasingly interconnected world, and
Whereas, in 1915, just as advances in telecommunication and transportation were beginning to transform the
globe, the forward-thinking leadership of Middlebury College established the first Middlebury Language
School, offering an intensive summer course of instruction in German, and over the next 111 years, the
curriculum expanded to include Abenaki, Arabic, Chinese, French, Hebrew, Italian, Japanese, Korean,
Portuguese, Russian, and Spanish, and
Whereas, the mission of Middlebury Language Schools is to “provide expert education in languages and
cultures through innovative, immersive instruction,” and
Whereas, Middlebury Language Schools welcome students from around the world, who receive expert
instruction and sign a pledge to speak only in the language being studied for the duration of the totally
immersive course and associated living experience, and
Whereas, in 2019, the presidents of Middlebury College and Bennington College (which was established in
1932 as an institution dedicated to innovative approaches to higher education), entered into a five-year
agreement, which became effective in 2021, to relocate the Arabic, Italian, and Portuguese Middlebury
Language Schools to the Bennington College campus, and
Whereas, during these past five years, this agreement has served as a model collaboration between the two
Vermont colleges, demonstrating their mutual commitment to academic excellence and intercultural exchange,
now therefore be it
Resolved by the Senate and House of Representatives:
That the General Assembly recognizes the fifth anniversary of the Middlebury Language Schools–
Bennington College intercultural education collaboration, and be it further
Resolved: That the Secretary of State be directed to send a copy of this resolution to Bennington College
and Middlebury College.
