15 Subject: Criminal and civil procedures; firearms
16 Statement of purpose of bill as introduced: This bill proposes to enact the
17 following provisions related to firearms: (1) provide that stealing a firearm of
18 any value constitutes grand larceny; (2) increase the penalties for second or
19 subsequent offenses of firearms possession by prohibited persons; (3) prohibit
20 the possession of firearms by persons who have been found by a court to pose
21 a danger of harm to themselves or others as the result of a mental illness; (4)
