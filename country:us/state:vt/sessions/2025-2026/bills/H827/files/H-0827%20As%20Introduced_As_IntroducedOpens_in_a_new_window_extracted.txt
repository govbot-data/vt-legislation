5 Subject: Property; conveyance of real estate; flooding disclosure
6 Statement of purpose of bill as introduced: This bill proposes to authorize a
7 seller of real property who has never resided on the property while under the
8 seller’s possession to notify a potential buyer that the seller cannot affirm
9 whether the property was subject to flooding or flood damage while the seller
10 possessed the property.
11 An act relating to flooding disclosures when conveying real estate
