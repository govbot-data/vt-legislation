20 and other programs, and to streamline reporting requirements on greenhouse

12 Sec. 2. RULEMAKING
13 On or before March 1, 2027, the Secretary of Natural Resources shall
14 submit to the Legislative Committee on Administrative Rules proposed final
15 rules for greenhouse gas reporting as required under 10 V.S.A. § 582(e)(2).
16 Sec. 3. APPROPRIATION
17 In fiscal year 2027, the sum of $800,000.00 is appropriated from the
18 General Fund to the Secretary of Natural Resources to be used to draft the
19 greenhouse gas emission reporting rules, to develop a greenhouse gas emission
20 source database, and to fund staff time on emission source data collection.
