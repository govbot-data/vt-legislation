2 An act relating to the greenhouse gas inventory and registry
3 It is hereby enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Vermont:
4 Sec. 1. 10 V.S.A. § 582 is amended to read:
5 § 582. GREENHOUSE GAS INVENTORIES; REGISTRY
6 * * *
7 (e) Rules.
8 (1) The Secretary may adopt rules to implement the provisions of this
9 section and shall review existing and proposed international, federal, and State
10 greenhouse gas emission reporting programs and make reasonable efforts to
11 promote consistency among the programs established pursuant to this section
12 and other programs, and to streamline reporting requirements on greenhouse
13 gas emission sources. Except as provided in subsection (g) of this section,
14 nothing in this section shall limit a State agency from adopting any rule within
15 its authority.
16 (2) The Secretary has authority to adopt rules that create a
17 comprehensive greenhouse gas emission reporting program that covers all
18 sources of emissions, including fuel suppliers. Suppliers of transportation and
19 heating fuels covered by the rules shall comply with requests from the
20 Secretary for information. The Secretary shall adopt a rule that at a minimum
21 includes the types and volume of fossil fuels sold by sector for the
