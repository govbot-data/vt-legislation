14 An act relating to first responders in communications
15 It is hereby enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Vermont:
16 Sec. 1. PURPOSE
17 The purpose of this act is to give formal recognition and validation to public
18 safety and emergency services dispatchers, whose work in responding to
19 emergencies is critical and often traumatic, by providing access to benefits

3 Sec. 2. 20 V.S.A. chapter 161 is added to read:
4 CHAPTER 161. FIRST RESPONDERS IN COMMUNICATIONS
5 § 2461. DEFINITIONS
6 “First responder in communications” means any individual who is currently
7 or formerly certified by the Vermont Enhanced 911 Board as a 911 call taker, a
8 public safety dispatcher, public safety telecommunicator, emergency operator,
9 or emergency telecommunicator, or who is employed as an emergency
10 communications dispatcher providing service for an emergency provider
11 organization. A first responder in communications is responsible for the
12 administration or supervision of lifesaving communication services for
13 emergency situations.
14 § 2462. TRAINING
15 To the extent practicable, employers of a first responder in communications
16 shall provide training to maintain and enhance the knowledge, skills, and
17 proficiency of the first responder in communications.
