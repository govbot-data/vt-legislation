10 Sec. 2. EDUCATIONAL TECHNOLOGY REGISTRATION REVIEW;
11 CERTIFICATION; AGENCY OF EDUCATION; REPORT
12 (a) Task. The Agency of Education shall:
13 (1) in consultation with the Secretary of State, review all educational
14 technology product provider registrations pursuant to 9 V.S.A. § 2444a;
15 (2) in consultation with schools, create a list of educational technology
16 products in use across the State;
17 (3) cross-reference the information gathered in subdivisions (1) and (2)
18 of this subsection to determine the names of any unregistered educational
19 technology providers operating in the State and forward the names of such
20 providers to the Office of the Attorney General;
