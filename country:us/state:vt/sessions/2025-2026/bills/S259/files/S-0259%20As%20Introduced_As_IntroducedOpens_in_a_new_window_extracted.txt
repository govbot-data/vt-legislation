10 It is hereby enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Vermont:
11 Sec. 1. 16 V.S.A. § 1045 is amended to read:
12 § 1045. DRIVER TRAINING COURSE
13 (a)(1) A driver education and training course, approved by the Agency of
14 Education and the Department of Motor Vehicles, shall be made available to
15 students whose parent or guardian is a resident of Vermont and who have
16 reached their 15th birthday 15 years of age and who are regularly enrolled in a
17 public or independent high school approved by the State Board.
18 (2) The driver education and training course made available to students
19 may be an in-person course or an asynchronous online course.

1 (e) Online driver education and training courses shall meet the content
2 standards for driver education curricula in the most recent edition of the
3 Novice Teen Driver Education and Training Administrative Standards.
4 (f) The Agency of Education and the Department of Motor Vehicles shall
5 only approve online driver education and training courses that:
6 (1) include a means for each student to measure performance outcomes;
7 (2) use a pool of rotating exam questions;
8 (3) incorporate accountability features to verify the identity of each
9 student while the student is engaged in the course of study;
10 (4) measure the amount of time that each student spends in the course;
11 (5) provide technical support to customers;
12 (6) store course content and student data on a secure server with
13 adequate safeguards in place to protect against data breaches and data loss;
14 (7) incorporate appropriate measures to prevent unauthorized access to
15 private information; and
16 (8) include the ability to update course content uniformly throughout the
17 State.
18 Sec. 2. EFFECTIVE DATE
19 This act shall take effect on July 1, 2026.
