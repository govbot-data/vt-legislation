Act No. 151 (S.243). An act relating to distributing funds to the Vermont Language Justice
Project
Subjects: Health; public health; language access; informational materials
This act authorizes the Office of Racial Equity to contract with the Vermont Language
Justice Project in fiscal year 2027 to prepare informational materials at the request of
State agencies and departments, as needed, to assist Vermonters who speak languages
other than English in the event of a disease outbreak, natural disaster, or other public
health emergency. It also directs the Department of Buildings and General Services to
assist the Vermont Language Justice Project as it navigates the process to become added
to the State’s list of approved contractors.
Effective Date: July 1, 2026
