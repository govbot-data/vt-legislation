2 Introduced by Senator Gulick

4 Date:
5 Subject: Health; public health; informational materials; language access
6 Statement of purpose of bill as introduced: This bill proposes to require the
7 Department of Health to distribute funds to the Vermont Language Justice
8 Project for the creation of informational materials related to a disease outbreak
9 or other public health emergency.
10 An act relating to distributing funds to the Vermont Language Justice
11 Project
12 It is hereby enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Vermont:
13 Sec. 1. APPROPRIATION; VERMONT LANGUAGE JUSTICE PROJECT
14 In fiscal year 2027, $150,000.00 is appropriated from the General Fund to
15 the Department of Health for distribution to the Vermont Language Justice
16 Project to prepare informational materials for Vermonters who speak
17 languages other than English in the event of a disease outbreak or other public
18 health emergency.
19 Sec. 2. EFFECTIVE DATE
20 This act shall take effect on July 1, 2026.
