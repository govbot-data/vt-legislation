5 Date:
6 Subject: Public service; energy; renewable energy; net metering
7 Statement of purpose of bill as introduced: This bill proposes to allow for
8 modifications to a net metering system without needing an updated certificate
9 of public good if the change does not increase the capacity of the system to
10 more than 25 kW.
11 An act relating to substantial change to a net metering system
12 It is hereby enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Vermont:
13 Sec. 1. 30 V.S.A. § 8010 is amended to read:
14 § 8010. SELF-GENERATION AND NET METERING
15 * * *
16 (d) An amendment to a certificate of public good that has been issued
17 under the rules of this section shall only be needed if the modifications to a net
18 metering system increase the total capacity of the system to more than 25 kW.
