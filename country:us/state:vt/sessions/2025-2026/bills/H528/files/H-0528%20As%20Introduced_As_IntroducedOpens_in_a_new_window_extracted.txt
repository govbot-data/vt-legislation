14 It is hereby enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Vermont:
15 Sec. 1. 18 V.S.A. § 4479 is added to read:
16 § 4479. IMPROPER DISPOSAL OF A HYPODERMIC NEEDLE OR
17 SYRINGE IN A SENSITIVE AREA
18 (a) As used in this section:
19 (1) “Playground” means any park or outdoor recreational area
20 specifically designed or intended to be used by children that has play

3 (2) “Sensitive area” means the grounds of a playground, beach, park,

6 (b) A person shall not knowingly or recklessly discard or dispose of a

8 section shall be imprisoned not more than one year or fined not more than

10 (c) Subsection (b) of this section shall not apply to the containment,
11 treatment, and disposal of medical sharps waste from medical care or first aid
12 services rendered on the grounds of a child care facility, public or independent

14 treatment, and disposal of hypodermic needles or syringes used for
15 instructional or educational purposes at such places.
