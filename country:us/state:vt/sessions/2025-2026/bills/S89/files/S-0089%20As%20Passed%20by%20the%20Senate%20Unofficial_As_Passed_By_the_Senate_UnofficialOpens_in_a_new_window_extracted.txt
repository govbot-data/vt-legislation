5 Sec. 2. 20 V.S.A. § 3175 is amended to read:
6 § 3175. EMERGENCY PERSONNEL SURVIVORS BENEFIT SPECIAL
7 FUND
8 (a) The Emergency Personnel Survivors Benefit Special Fund is
9 established in the Office of the State Treasurer for the purpose of the payment
10 of claims distributed pursuant to this chapter. The Fund shall comprise
11 appropriations transfers made by the General Assembly, amounts transferred
12 by the Emergency Board when the General Assembly is not in session, and
13 contributions or donations from any other source. All balances in the Fund at
14 the end of the fiscal year shall be carried forward. Interest earned shall remain
15 in the Fund.
16 (b) In the event that the balance of the Fund is insufficient to pay monetary
17 benefits awarded by the Board when the General Assembly is not in session,
18 the Emergency Board may, pursuant to its authority under 32 V.S.A. § 133,
19 transfer into the Fund additional amounts necessary to pay the monetary
20 benefits.
