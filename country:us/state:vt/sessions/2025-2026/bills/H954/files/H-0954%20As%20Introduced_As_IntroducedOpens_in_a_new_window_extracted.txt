2 § 904. LOCAL OPTION TAX
3 (a) The Selectboard is authorized to impose a two percent local option tax
4 on sales, rooms, meals, and alcoholic beverages. A tax imposed pursuant to
5 this section shall be collected and administered by the Department of Taxes
6 pursuant to 24 V.S.A. § 138.
7 (b) The local option tax authorized by this section supersedes any local
8 option tax previously adopted and approved by the Town.

10 Sec. 3. EFFECTIVE DATE
