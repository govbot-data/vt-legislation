CHAPTER 153. TOWN OF STOWE
Subchapter 9. Taxes
* * *
§ 904. LOCAL OPTION TAX
(a) The Selectboard is authorized to impose a two percent local option tax
on sales, rooms, meals, and alcoholic beverages. A tax imposed pursuant to
this section shall be collected and administered by the Department of Taxes
pursuant to 24 V.S.A. § 138.
(b) Notwithstanding any contrary provision of 24 V.S.A. §138 or 32 V.S.A.
§ 3709, of any local option tax collected in excess of one percent, 50 percent
shall be paid to the Local Option Municipal Transportation Special Fund
