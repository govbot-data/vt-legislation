6 The all-hazards event provisions of this chapter shall not be brought into

8 the legislative body of the municipality, the city or town manager, or the mayor

10 emergency and request the Governor to find that a state of emergency exists
11 and the Governor so finds, or unless the Governor declares a state of
12 emergency under section 9 of this title. This section shall not be construed to
