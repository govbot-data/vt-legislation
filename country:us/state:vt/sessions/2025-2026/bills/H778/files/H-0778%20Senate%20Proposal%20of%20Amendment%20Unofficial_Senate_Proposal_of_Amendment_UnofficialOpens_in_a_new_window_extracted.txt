5 Sec. 1. 20 V.S.A. § 10 is amended to read:
6 § 10. REQUEST TO GOVERNOR BY MUNICIPAL AUTHORITIES
7 The all-hazards event provisions of this chapter shall not be brought into
8 action unless the municipal director of emergency management, a member of
9 the legislative body of the municipality, the city or town manager, or the mayor
10 of a city that is within the area affected by an all-hazards event shall declare an
11 emergency and request the Governor to find that a state of emergency exists
12 and the Governor so finds, or unless the Governor declares a state of
13 emergency under section 9 of this title. This section shall not be construed to
14 prevent the Governor or the Director of Emergency Management without
15 municipal approval from requiring the evacuation of an area subject to
16 inundation from a dam failure when there is a dam failure or an imminent risk
17 of failure.
