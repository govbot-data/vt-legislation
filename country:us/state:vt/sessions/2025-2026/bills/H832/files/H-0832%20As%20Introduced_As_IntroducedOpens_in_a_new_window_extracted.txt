5 Subject: Alcoholic beverages, cannabis, and tobacco; licensing; retail licenses

7 Statement of purpose of bill as introduced: This bill proposes to authorize
8 licensed alcoholic beverage manufacturers, at not more than 10 fourth-class
9 license locations, to sell alcoholic beverages produced by other manufacturers.
10 This bill also proposes to amend the application requirements for retail
11 alcoholic beverage tasting permits to allow a licensee to submit the application
12 and fee at least one day before the proposed date of the tasting event.
13 An act relating to fourth-class licenses and retail alcoholic beverage tasting
14 permits
15 It is hereby enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Vermont:
