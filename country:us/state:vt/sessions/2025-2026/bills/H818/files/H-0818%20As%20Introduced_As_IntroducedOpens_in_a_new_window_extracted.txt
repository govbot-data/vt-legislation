11 § 1433. MENTAL HEALTH SCREENING PROGRAM

13 (1) “Agency” means the Agency of Education.
14 (2) “Department” means the Department of Mental Health.

18 schools it oversees. The mental health screening services provided pursuant to
19 this section shall be approved by the Agency, in consultation with the
