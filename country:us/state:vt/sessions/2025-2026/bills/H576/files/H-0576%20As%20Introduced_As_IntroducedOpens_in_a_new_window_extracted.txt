4 § 552. AFFIRMING HEALTH CARE TRUST FUND PROGRAM
5 (a) There is created the Affirming Health Care Trust Fund Program to
6 provide monetary awards to pay for and support access to gender-affirming
7 health care services.
8 (b) The Affirming Health Care Trust Fund Program is authorized to
9 provide monetary awards directly to:
10 (1) health care providers who provide or are seeking to provide gender-
11 affirming health care services in Vermont; and
12 (2) nonprofit organizations whose missions include increasing access to
13 gender-affirming health care services in Vermont.
14 (c) Monetary awards shall be used:
15 (1) to pay for costs associated with providing and receiving gender-
16 affirming health care services, including:
17 (A) support for providers of gender-affirming health care services
18 and relevant nonprofit organizations in order to increase access to gender-
19 affirming health care services;
20 (B) paying for care that would otherwise be uncompensated;

2 (D) reducing disparities in access to gender-affirming health care

4 (2) for capital costs associated with establishing and operating Vermont-
5 based health care clinics to provide gender-affirming health care services; and

15 § 553. AFFIRMING HEALTH CARE TRUST FUND
16 (a) There is established the Affirming Health Care Trust Fund as a special
17 fund pursuant to 32 V.S.A. chapter 7, subchapter 5, to be administered by the
18 Treasurer. The purpose of the Fund is to provide funds for and support the
19 administration of the Affirming Health Care Trust Fund Program established in
20 this chapter.

1 (b) The Fund may receive State appropriations, gifts, grants, federal funds,
2 and funds from any other public or private source. Monies from the Fund may
3 be expended for monetary awards and for administrative and related purposes.
4 (c)(1) Notwithstanding any provision of 32 V.S.A. chapter 7, subchapter 5

13 under the Public Records Act and shall be kept confidential.
