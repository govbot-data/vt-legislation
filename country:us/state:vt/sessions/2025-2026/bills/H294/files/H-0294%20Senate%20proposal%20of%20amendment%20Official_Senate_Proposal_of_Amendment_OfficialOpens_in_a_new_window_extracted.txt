Senate proposal of amendment
H. 294
An act relating to telecommunications services and wages in correctional
facilities.
The Senate proposes to the House to amend the bill in Sec. 2,
Department of Corrections; evaluation of telecommunications services; report,
in subsection (b), by striking out subdivision (4) in its entirety and by
renumbering the remaining subdivisions to be numerically correct.
