Act No. 139 (H.294). An act relating to telecommunications services and wages in
correctional facilities
Subjects: Labor; telecommunications; employment practices; minimum wage;
commissary; public institutions and corrections; State contracts
This act requires the Department of Corrections on or before December 1, 2026, to
provide to the House Committee on Corrections and Institutions and the Senate
Committee on Institutions reports on options for providing no-cost telecommunications
services to inmates in the Department’s custody and on the impacts of current wages for
inmates in the Department’s custody.
Effective Date: June 16, 2026
