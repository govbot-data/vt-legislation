1 H.294
2 An act relating to telecommunications services and wages in correctional
3 facilities
4 It is hereby enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Vermont:
5 Sec. 1. INTENT
6 It is the intent of the General Assembly to:
7 (1) create conditions of incarceration that encourage the development
8 and maintenance of the personal supports necessary for rehabilitation;
9 (2) mitigate the disruptions to family and community connections
10 caused by incarceration by reducing communication barriers;
11 (3) divest the State from the for-profit prison industry; and
12 (4) inform State correctional policy decision-making with data, fiscal
13 analysis, and agency expertise.

15 TELECOMMUNICATIONS SERVICES; REPORT

18 Committee on Institutions a written report evaluating options for providing no-
19 cost telecommunications services to inmates in the Department’s custody that:
20 (1) describes the current telecommunications service model, including
21 usage rates, costs, and contract terms under existing provider arrangements;
