State of Vermont
House of Representatives
Montpelier, Vermont
Concurrent House Resolution
H.C.R. 272
Offered by Representatives Graning of Jericho, Brown of Richmond, Squirrell of Underhill, Stevens
of Waterbury, and Wood of Waterbury
Offered by Senators Chittenden, Lyons and Ram Hinsdale
House concurrent resolution congratulating the 2026 Mount Mansfield Union High School Cougars
dance team on winning the school’s eighth State jazz and 18th overall State dancing championship
Whereas, dance is often considered an exclusively leisure pursuit, bereft of a competitive aspect, but
the annual Vermont Principals’ Association’s State dance championship offers enthusiastic high school
hip-hop, jazz, and pom dancers the chance to compete for honors as the best in their respective
genres, and
Whereas, the Cougars’ history of excellence in jazz and hip-hop dancing is impressive, as, prior to
2026, Mount Mansfield Union High School (MMU) had won 18 championships in either one or both
genres, including both titles in 2017 and 2019–2024; hip-hop in 2016, 2018, and 2025; and jazz in
2026, and
Whereas, in 2026, although MMU was the respectable runner-up to Middlebury High School in the
hip-hop category, the Cougars excelled in the jazz competition, as the MMU dancers responded to the
music’s beat and rhythm with the ideal steps and swings to clinch the 2026 championship, proving once
again their appreciation for America’s own musical art form, and
Whereas, the winning jazz-dancing Cougars were Abrum Alexander, Maddie Brown, Norah Burton,
Emilia Carini, Elise Cournoyer, Gabby Cowan, Addie Dowd, Quinn Gilmer, Lily Ide, and Kate
LaMantia, Annie Marek, Zoey Marek, Anya Muller, Nadia Muller, Munashe Pfende, Olivia Reyome,
Audrey Rogers, Zoe Van Duyn, and
Whereas, the MMU team’s coaching staff, consisting of Rose Bedard, Lisa Russin, Edie Watson,
Addie Price, and Ella Davis, served as a fantastic group of instructors and mentors, now therefore be it
Resolved by the Senate and House of Representatives:
That the General Assembly congratulates the 2026 Mount Mansfield Union High School Cougars
dance team on winning the school’s eighth State jazz and 18th overall State dancing championships, and
be it further
Resolved: That the Secretary of State be directed to send a copy of this resolution to MMU.
