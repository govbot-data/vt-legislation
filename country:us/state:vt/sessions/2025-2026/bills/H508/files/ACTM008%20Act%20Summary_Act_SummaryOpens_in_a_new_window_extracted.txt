Act No. M-8 (H.508). An act relating to approval of amendments to the charter of the City
of Burlington
Subjects: Municipal charters; municipal and county government; City of
Burlington; amendments
This act amends the charter of the City of Burlington to remove the codified City
election area boundaries and add a provision that states the election area boundaries
established pursuant 2023 Acts and Resolves No. M-7 shall remain in effect until
changed by the City Council. This act also adds provisions granting the City Council
authority to make changes to the election area boundaries in order to provide an equal
division of population among City districts or wards. This act also provides that changes
to the City’s election area boundaries shall be approved by the voters at an annual or
special City meeting.
Effective Date: April 27, 2026
