6 Date:
7 Subject: Taxation and finance; salaries and fees; General Assembly
8 Statement of purpose of bill as introduced: This bill proposes to incentivize
9 members of the General Assembly to complete the legislative session before
10 the first Friday in May by providing that members will receive the same
11 amount of compensation if the regular session concludes before the first Friday
12 in May and by providing that members will receive only expense
13 reimbursement if the regular session extends up to three weeks beyond the first
14 Friday in May, with no compensation or expense reimbursement if the regular
15 session extends more than three weeks beyond the first Friday in May.
16 An act relating to legislative pay
17 It is hereby enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Vermont:
18 Sec. 1. SHORT TITLE
19 This act may be cited as the “Legislative Economic Incentive Act of 2026.”
