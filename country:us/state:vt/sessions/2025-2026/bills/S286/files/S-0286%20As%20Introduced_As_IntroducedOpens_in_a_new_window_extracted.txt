12 It is hereby enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Vermont:
13 Sec. 1. 32 V.S.A. chapter 225, subchapter 5 is added to read:
14 Subchapter 5. School Construction Surcharge
15 § 9311. IMPOSITION; SCHOOL CONSTRUCTION SURCHARGE
16 (a)(1) An operator shall collect a surcharge of two percent of the rent of
17 each occupancy subject to tax under subsection 9241(a) of this title.
18 (2) An operator shall collect a surcharge of two percent on the sale of
19 each taxable meal subject to tax under subsection 9241(b) of this title.
